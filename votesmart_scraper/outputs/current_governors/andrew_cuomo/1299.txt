Governor Cuomo Urges New Yorkers to be Prepared for Severe Storms During Tornado Watch
Press Release
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: June 8, 2015
Location: Albany, NY

Governor Andrew M. Cuomo today urged New Yorkers to be prepared for severe storms that are projected to pass through the state this afternoon and evening. Due to the storms, the National Weather Service has issued a Tornado Watch in effect until 8:00 p.m. The Tornado Watch is in effect for Albany, Broome, Chemung, Chenango, Columbia, Cortland, Delaware, Dutchess, Fulton, Greene, Hamilton, Herkimer, Madison, Montgomery, Oneida, Onondaga, Otsego, Rensselaer, Saratoga, Schenectady, Schoharie, Sullivan, Tioga, Tompkins, Ulster, Warren, and Washington Counties.
"We are asking all New Yorkers in the path of these storms to remain alert, stay informed and exercise caution," Governor Cuomo said. "New Yorkers know firsthand the dangers of extreme weather. By taking these simple, proactive steps, residents can prepare for what Mother Nature has in store."
