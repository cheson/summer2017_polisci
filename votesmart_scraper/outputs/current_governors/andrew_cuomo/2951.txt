Public Statements
Statement From Governor Andrew M.Cuomo
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: March 14, 2011
Location: Albany, NY

"Given Ophadell Williams' criminal record and driving history, I have directed the New York State Inspector General to commence an immediate investigation into the circumstances surrounding how Mr. Williams was able to obtain and retain a commercial driver's license. Inspector General Ellen Biben will coordinate her investigation with the State Police, as well as the ongoing investigation by Bronx District Attorney Robert T. Johnson into this tragic accident."

Source: http://www.governor.ny.gov/press/031411statement
