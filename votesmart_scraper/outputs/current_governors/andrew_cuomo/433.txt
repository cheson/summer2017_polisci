Public Statements
Statement from Governor Andrew M. Cuomo on the Confirmation of Superintendent George P. Beach
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: June 9, 2016
Location: Albany, NY

"Superintendent Beach is a deeply respected public servant who has dedicated his life to protecting the people of New York State. With more than 30 years of diverse experience in emergency response management, counterintelligence and civil and criminal investigations, Superintendent Beach has demonstrated unimpeachable skill and integrity throughout his career. I commend the Senate for their swift confirmation, and I look forward to working with Superintendent Beach to promote public safety and improve the quality of life for all New Yorkers."
