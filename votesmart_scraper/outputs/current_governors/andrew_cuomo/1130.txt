Governor Cuomo Signs Bills to Honor Law Enforcement Officers Killed in the Line of Duty
Press Release
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Aug. 13, 2015
Location: Albany, NY

Governor Cuomo signed legislation to rename several bridges and portions of the state highway system in honor of three New York law enforcement officers who died in the line of duty.
"These exceptional law enforcement professionals paid the ultimate price while working to protect their neighbors and their communities," Governor Cuomo said. "This legislation memorializes their service and will ensure their sacrifice and commitment will always be remembered."
