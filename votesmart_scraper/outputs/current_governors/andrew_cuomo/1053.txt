Governor Cuomo Announces Additional Transit Service For Pope Francis' Visit to New York
Press Release
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Sept. 10, 2015
Location: Albany, NY

Governor Cuomo announced there will be an increased number of trains and buses to support the expected amount of travelers during Pope Francis' visit to New York later this month. The Metropolitan Transportation Authority and the Port Authority will add extra service and will have additional crew on hand to coincide with the papal events.
"We are proud to host Pope Francis here in New York, and we are working to expand mass transit options to ensure that everyone can travel safely and securely during this time," Governor Cuomo said. "I encourage anyone planning to be in New York City at that time to plan ahead and check the new travel schedules."
