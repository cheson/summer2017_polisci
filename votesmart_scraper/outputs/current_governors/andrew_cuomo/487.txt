Statement from Governor Andrew M. Cuomo on Passage of Legislation to Repeal Tax on Feminine Hygiene Products
Press Release
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: May 25, 2016
Location: Albany, NY

"Repealing this regressive and unfair tax on women is a matter of social and economic justice, and I commend Assembly Member Rosenthal and Senator Serino for their strong advocacy on this measure. I look forward to signing it into law."
