Governor Cuomo Announces Completion of Parkway Bridge Over Sunrise Highway
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Aug. 7, 2015
Location: Albany, NY

Governor Andrew M. Cuomo today announced completion of an $8.3 million project to rehabilitate the bridge carrying the Southern and Heckscher State parkways over the Sunrise Highway (State Route 27) in the town of Islip, Suffolk County.
"The Parkway Bridge is an important part of Long Island's transportation network and by building it back better and stronger, we are ensuring that traffic moves safely and smoothly for many motorists that depend on it," Governor Cuomo said. "This is another example of the progress we have made in bolstering key infrastructure projects that will support economic activity and keep this region on the move."
