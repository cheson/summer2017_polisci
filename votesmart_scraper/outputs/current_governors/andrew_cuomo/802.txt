Governor Cuomo Announces New Recharge NY Power Allocations to 12 Businesses and Non-Profits Statewide
Press Release
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Dec. 17, 2015
Location: Albany, NY

Governor Cuomo announced that the New York Power Authority Board of Trustees has approved 12,364 kilowatts of low-cost power allocations under the ReCharge NY program for 11 business operations and one non-profit. These allocations are expected to leverage more than $450 million in capital investments and will support 4,637 jobs statewide, including 1,066 new positions.
"ReCharge NY has been strengthening New York's economy since its inception," Governor Cuomo said. "Providing businesses with low-cost power helps ensure that they are able to stay and grow in New York State, which creates jobs and helps revitalize communities."
