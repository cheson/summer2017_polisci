Statement From Governor Cuomo on The U.S. Attorney's Court Filing Related to Disabled Access to Taxis
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Oct. 14, 2011
Location: Albany, NY

"I understand and appreciate the concerns raised by the U.S. Attorney's office. Moreover, I understand the human needs of the disabled community when it comes to access to taxis. We will be addressing the issue as we consider modifications to the pending legislation."

Source: http://www.governor.ny.gov/press/10142011StatementFromGovernoronDisabledAccesstoTaxis
