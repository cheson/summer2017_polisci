Governor Cuomo Launches Holiday Toy, Coat and School Supply Drive to Benefit New Yorkers in Need
Press Release
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Dec. 4, 2014
Location: Albany, NY

Governor Andrew M. Cuomo today launched a statewide toy, coat and school supplies holiday giving drive to benefit families in need throughout New York.
"New Yorkers have always been ready to step up for those in need, and it is important that we celebrate the holidays in that same spirit," Governor Cuomo said. "This donation drive is a great way to support those who are less fortunate, and I encourage all New Yorkers to do their part in making this a happy holiday season for everyone."
The Office of General Services has set up drop-off locations across the state for businesses and individuals to bring the toys, coats and school supplies, which will be distributed to families in underserved communities throughout New York. The drop-off locations will be collecting donations from Friday, December 5 to Friday, December 19.
