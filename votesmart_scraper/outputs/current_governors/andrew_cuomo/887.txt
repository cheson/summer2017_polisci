Governor Cuomo Announces New $7.7 Million Center for Advanced Ceramic Manufacturing and Education at Alfred University
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Nov. 13, 2015
Location: Unknown

Governor Andrew M. Cuomo today announced a $7.75 million grant for the construction of a new Center for Advanced Ceramic Manufacturing and Education at the New York State College of Ceramics at Alfred University. The award is supported by the fourth round of the Governor's NYSUNY 2020 Challenge Grant Program.
"This new facility will play a key role in the economic revival of the Southern Tier and further solidifies New York as a global leader in high-tech research and manufacturing," Governor Cuomo said. "Companies the world over have turned to this college for the resources and highly skilled workforce needed to compete in today's high-tech economy and with today's announcement, even more will."
