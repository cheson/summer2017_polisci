Public Statements
Statement From Governor Andrew M. Cuomo
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: May 1, 2014
Location: Albany, NY

"While the specific terms of City contracts are up to the discretion of the Mayor and the union, after nearly a decade of impasse, it is a great accomplishment to bring these difficult negotiations to an end. I congratulate the teacher's union and the Mayor in completing the agreement."
