Governor Cuomo Honors Eight Fallen Firefighters
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Oct. 6, 2015
Location: Albany, NY

Governor Cuomo announced the names of eight fallen firefighters who have been added to the New York State Fallen Firefighters Memorial located at the Empire State Plaza. With the addition of these eight firefighters, the Fallen Firefighters Memorial now contains the names of 2,398 individuals who lost their lives in the line of duty.
"Today, we pay tribute to these brave individuals who gave their lives while protecting their neighbors and their communities," Governor Cuomo said. "With the additions to the Fallen Firefighter Memorial, we will help ensure that their sacrifices and their dedication to the safety of others will not be forgotten."
