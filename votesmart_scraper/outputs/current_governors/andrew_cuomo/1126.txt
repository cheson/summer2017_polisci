Governor Cuomo Signs Legislation Prohibiting Powdered Alcohol
Press Release
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Aug. 14, 2015
Location: Albany, NY

Governor Cuomo signed legislation that will prohibit the sale of any powdered or crystalline alcohol product, also known as "palcohol," in New York State.
"This dangerous product is a public health disaster waiting to happen," Governor Cuomo said. "I am proud to sign this legislation that will keep powdered alcohol off the shelves and out of the wrong hands."
