Governor Cuomo Deploys DFS Mobile Command Center to Freeport, Nassau County to Deliver Foreclosure Prevention Assistance to Homeowners
Press Release
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: March 19, 2015
Location: Albany, NY

Governor Andrew M. Cuomo today announced that he is deploying the Department of Financial Services (DFS) Mobile Command Center to Freeport, Nassau County to provide foreclosure prevention assistance to financially struggling homeowners.
DFS representatives will be on-site to meet homeowners from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m., Friday, March 20. The command center will be located outside the Freeport Memorial Library, 144 W. Merrick Rd., Freeport.
"We want to ensure that anyone in danger of a foreclosure knows their rights and what options are available," Governor Cuomo said. "Trained personnel from the Department of Financial Services will be on-hand, and I encourage anyone at risk of foreclosure to come and find out what solutions are available."
Benjamin M. Lawsky, Superintendent of Financial Services, said, "This program is designed specifically to offer assistance to homeowners facing difficult economic choices. It enables homeowners to understand that there may be options available to help them prevent foreclosure."
