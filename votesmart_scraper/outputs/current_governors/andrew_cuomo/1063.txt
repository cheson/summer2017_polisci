Statement from Governor Andrew M. Cuomo on U.S. News & World Report's Ranking of SUNY College of Environmental Science and Forestry
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Sept. 9, 2015
Location: Albany, NY

"SUNY College of Environmental Science and Forestry is in a league of its own when it comes to providing students with the skills and know-how they need to succeed in challenging careers. From protecting our natural resources to preventing pollution and adapting to the effects of climate change, ESF's faculty and staff are inspiring the best minds in the field on a daily basis. The fact that ESF has been recognized by U.S. News & World Report as one of the top 100 colleges nationwide is proof of its premier status, and I offer my congratulations to the entire ESF family for this fitting honor."
