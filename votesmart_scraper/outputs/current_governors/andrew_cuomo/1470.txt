Statement from Governor Andrew M. Cuomo on Passover
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: April 3, 2015
Location: Unknown

"This evening, members of the Jewish community all across New York will gather with families and friends to begin the celebration of Passover.
"Passover is a commemoration of the freeing of the Jewish people from slavery and oppression. It is a story of fierce determination that has inspired generations across the globe. As New Yorkers join with family and friends to celebrate, let us resolve to further this timely lesson by ensuring a safe society and brighter future for all.
"My best wishes for a happy and joyous Passover."
