Statement From Governor Andrew M. Cuomo on Rosh Hashanah
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Sept. 24, 2014
Location: Albany, NY

"Members of the Jewish community throughout New York State will gather this evening with friends and loved ones to celebrate Rosh Hashanah, the Jewish New Year.

"This sacred holiday is an opportunity to reflect on the year that has passed and look to the year ahead. During this period of reflection, we once again celebrate the diversity that strengthens and energizes our state and recommit ourselves to work together to create a safer and more peaceful future for all.

"I extend warm wishes to all for a healthy, happy and prosperous New Year. L'Shanah Tovah."
