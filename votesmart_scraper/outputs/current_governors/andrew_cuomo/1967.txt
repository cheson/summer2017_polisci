Public Statements
Statement from Governor Andrew M. Cuomo
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: April 13, 2014
Location: Albany, NY

"Our thoughts and prayers go out to the victims of the shootings that took place earlier today at the Jewish Community Center of Greater Kansas City and Village Shalom. On the eve of Passover, New Yorkers stand in solidarity with the Jewish community in Kansas City, mourning those who were lost and wishing a speedy recovery to those who were injured as a result of today's tragic events."

Source: http://www.governor.ny.gov/press/04132013-kansas-city
