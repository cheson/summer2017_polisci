Public Statements
Statement from Governor Andrew M. Cuomo
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Jan. 25, 2011
Location: Albany, NY

"Today's announcement is an exciting day for New York. Pfizer's partnership with many of our great medical research universities will accelerate the process of developing and bringing to market new life-saving medicines and therapies. This is a model for collaboration that will enhance New York's status as a global center of the life sciences industry."

Source: http://www.governor.ny.gov/press/012511statement
