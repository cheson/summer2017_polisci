Governor Cuomo Announces Results of State Police Crackdown on Impaired, Distracted & Speeding Drivers During Thanksgiving Weekend 2015
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Dec. 3, 2015
Location: Albany, NY

Governor Cuomo announced that the New York State Police issued more than 14,000 tickets as a result of increased enforcement efforts this Thanksgiving weekend. The enforcement period kicked off on Wednesday, November 25 and ran through Sunday, November 29, 2015.
"New York has zero tolerance for impaired and reckless drivers who put themselves and others at risk," Governor Cuomo said. "I commend State Police keeping these dangerous drivers off the roads and helping to prevent needless tragedies this holiday weekend."
