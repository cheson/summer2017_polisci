Governor Cuomo Addresses Terrorist Attack at Manchester Arena in England
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: May 23, 2017
Location: Albany, NY

"It's also appropriate at this moment that we recognize what happened in Manchester last night, which is another sobering reminder of the world we live in now and the death and destruction that can be wrought relatively easily, and our hearts and prayers go out to those affected."
