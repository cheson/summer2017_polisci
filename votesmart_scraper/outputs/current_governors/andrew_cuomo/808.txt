Governor Cuomo Announces Completion of Three New Bridges in the North Country
Press Release
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Dec. 16, 2015
Location: Albany, NY

Governor Cuomo announced the completion of three North Country bridge projects totaling $27.7 million. The new bridges will significantly improve interstate access, enhance bicycle and pedestrian safety and reduce the risk of future traffic collisions.
"By investing in a top-performing transportation network, we keep New York on a path to sustained economic growth and long-term prosperity," Governor Cuomo said. "Infrastructure plays a vital role in creating vibrant, thriving communities and these bridge projects ensure the North Country region will remain a first-rate destination for generations to come."
