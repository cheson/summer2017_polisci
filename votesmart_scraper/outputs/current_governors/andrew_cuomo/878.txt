Governor Cuomo Announces Grand Opening of $40.5 Million Ferguson Enterprises Distribution Facility in Coxsackie
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Nov. 17, 2015
Location: Albany, NY

Governor Andrew M. Cuomo today announced the grand opening of Ferguson Enterprises, Inc.'s new distribution facility in Coxsackie. Ferguson Enterprises, the nation's largest wholesale distributor of residential and commercial plumbing supplies, has committed to create 95 new jobs at the facility and retain 319 retail jobs across the state with the completion of this project.
"Today is yet another great example of how New York is open for business," Governor Cuomo said. "With fierce competition from other states, we're proud that Ferguson chose New York and the Capital Region to invest, grow and create jobs. I welcome them to their new home in Greene County and look forward to their continued success."
