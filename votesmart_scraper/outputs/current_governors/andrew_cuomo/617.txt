Video & Rush Transcript: Governor Cuomo Delivers Statement on Attacks in Brussels
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: March 22, 2016
Location: Albany, NY

Governor Cuomo: "We will be there for Brussels. Whatever they need, they should ask the State of New York and we will deliver."
At Governor's Direction, Stepped Up Security from National Guard, State Police, Port Authority and MTA at all Major Transit Hubs Downstate
Governor Andrew M. Cuomo today delivered a statement on the attacks in Brussels. Earlier today, the Governor directed stepped-up security from the National Guard, State Police, Port Authority and MTA at all major transit hubs in the New York City region, including at JFK and LaGuardia Airports, Penn Station and Grand Central Terminal.
