Public Statements
Statement from Governor Andrew M. Cuomo
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Nov. 20, 2011
Location: Albany, NY

"The arrest tonight of an individual suspected of planning to commit a terrorist act in New York demonstrates once again the effectiveness and bravery of our men and women in law enforcement. As families across our state gather this holiday season, we will continue to remain in close contact with our federal and local law enforcement partners to ensure the safety of all New Yorkers."
