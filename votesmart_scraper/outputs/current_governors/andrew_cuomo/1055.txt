Governor Cuomo Announces I Love New York Partnership With Delta Air Lines
Press Release
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Sept. 10, 2015
Location: Albany, NY

Governor Cuomo announced a co-branding partnership between Empire State Development's Division of Tourism/ I LOVE NEW YORK and Delta Air Lines. The new co-branded logo will be unveiled outside the New York Stock Exchange today, when Delta will ring the closing bell. In an interactive activation at the exchange, Delta will showcase the new partnership, network and presence, alongside New York's Upstate tourism initiatives.
"When people take the time to experience New York, they fall in love and keep coming back -- and that means more tourism and economic activity for our state," Governor Cuomo said. "This partnership will bring the I LOVE NY brand to new heights, and I am proud that our administration is partnering with Delta Air Lines to promote all that the Empire State has to offer."
