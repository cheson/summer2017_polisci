Governor Cuomo Announces Launch of Common Core Task Force
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Sept. 28, 2015
Location: Albany, NY

Task Force to Perform Comprehensive Review of Learning Standards, Instructional Guidance and Curricula, and Tests to Improve Implementation and Reduce Testing Anxiety
Governor Cuomo: "The State's learning standards must be strong, sensible and fair, and parents and teachers should be able to have faith in those standards. This Task Force will ensure that this becomes the reality."
