Governor Cuomo Announces Construction Has Begun For State-of-the-Art Fruit and Vegetable Processor in Orleans County
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Nov. 5, 2015
Location: Albany, NY

Governor Cuomo announced that work has begun on Pride Pak Canada Ltd.'s new 68,000 square-foot facility on 13 acres in the Medina Business Park. Pride Pak, Canada's largest fresh fruit and vegetable processor, is spending more than $20 million to build a state-of-the-art facility and install machinery and equipment in Orleans County in order to be closer to its U.S. customers. View a rendering here.
"This company will not only bring new jobs to the regions, but will also bolster local farmers and help attract other businesses to the area by being the anchor to this new commerce hub," Governor Cuomo said. "Today's action is more exciting news for the Finger Lakes."
