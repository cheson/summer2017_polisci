Public Statements
Statement from Governor Andrew M. Cuomo
Statement
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: Dec. 22, 2015
Location: Unknown

"Staff Sgt. Joe Lemm was committed to his fellow Americans, and embodied the selflessness and bravery of the U.S. Armed Forces and the NYPD. I join all New Yorkers in mourning his loss, as well as the loss of the other victims of this horrific attack. They will be greatly missed.
