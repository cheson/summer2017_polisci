Statement from Governor Andrew M. Cuomo on Passover
By: Andrew Cuomo
Date: April 10, 2017
Location: Albany, NY

"This evening, members of the Jewish community will gather to begin the Holiday of Passover.
"The overarching message of this festive holiday is that through resiliency, faith and trust in each other, we can come together and beat back oppression, slavery and bondage.
"In New York, our greatest strength is the vibrancy and diversity of our communities.
"As we gather with our families and loved ones, let us remind ourselves to recommit to making New York the most fair, just, secure and equal state for all New Yorkers. My best wishes for a Chag Sameach."
