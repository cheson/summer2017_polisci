Gov. Edwards' Statement on 2017 NBA All-Star Game
Statement
By: John Bel Edwards
Date: July 21, 2016
Location: Baton Rouge, LA

Gov. John Bel Edwards released the following statement on the 2017 NBA All-Star Game:
"Louisiana would be proud to host the 2017 NBA All-Star Game if the opportunity presents itself. It would be an honor to have this event return to New Orleans, and we look forward to sharing our hospitality with basketball fans from around the country. My office will support and assist Tom Benson and the New Orleans Pelicans in any way possible to help bring the NBA All-Stars back. Louisiana is rich in diversity of heritage, culture, cuisine and people, and we believe the NBA could not select a better place for everyone to come and enjoy this spectacular sporting event."
