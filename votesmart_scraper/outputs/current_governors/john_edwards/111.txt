Gov. Edwards Signs Hurricane Preparedness and Public Safety Executive Orders
Statement
By: John Bel Edwards
Date: June 1, 2016
Location: Baton Rouge, LA

Today, on the first day of hurricane season, Governor John Bel Edwards signed three executive orders related to hurricane preparedness and public safety.
"These three orders will ensure that all levels of government will be able to coordinate and to respond quickly and effectively in the event of a natural disaster or terrorist attack," said Gov. Edwards. "They are especially important as we prepare for this upcoming hurricane season. The Governor's Office of Homeland Security and Emergency Preparedness has been working with me, the Louisiana National Guard, and all the other departments of state government to ensure that we can be ready for any emergency."
