Wyoming Capitol Opens on Saturdays for Cheyenne Frontier Days
Press Release
By: Matt Mead
Date: July 18, 2014
Location: Cheyenne, WY

The Wyoming Capitol will open to the public on July 19th and 26th this year. This allows visitors access to the Capitol for tours and sightseeing on the Saturdays of Cheyenne Frontier Days.
"I want to invite anyone in Cheyenne for the Saturday parades to come by their Capitol building and take a tour," Governor Mead said. "This is a great idea the Legislature came up with this year. We get a record number of visitors in Cheyenne for the Daddy of "Em All and we will proudly open our Capitol as a destination for visitors from near and far."
The Capitol is open 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. on the 19th and 26th. The Capitol Information Desk will be staffed and security will be on site.
