Governor Rick Scott and Florida Cabinet Issue Resolution Recognizing Vietnam Veterans Day
Statement
By: Rick Scott
Date: March 30, 2015
Location: Unknown

Today, Governor Rick Scott and the Florida Cabinet issued the following resolution recognizing Welcome Home Vietnam Veterans Day in honor of the 50th anniversary of the Vietnam War.
Governor Rick Scott said, "Our state is home to more than 515,000 Vietnam era veterans whose courageous service has protected the freedoms we enjoy today. As a Navy veteran who served during the Vietnam era, I am proud to sign this resolution to honor all of our Vietnam veterans and to remember those who lost their lives while defending our nation. While we can never fully repay these brave heroes for their sacrifice, I join every Floridian to recognize and thank our Vietnam veterans and their families for their service to our nation."
