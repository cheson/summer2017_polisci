Gov. Scott: The Profound Legacy of Shimon Peres Will Live On
Statement
By: Rick Scott
Date: Sept. 28, 2016
Location: Tallahassee, FL

Today, Governor Rick Scott released the following statement on the passing of Shimon Peres, former president and prime minister of Israel.
Governor Rick Scott said, "Florida has maintained a strong relationship with Israel for many generations, and over the years we have joined together in support of the peace and security Israel's former president and prime minister Shimon Peres worked so hard to secure for families. I had the opportunity to meet him a few years ago and I was truly honored to hear him speak about his unparalleled career and remarkable achievements. While we are deeply saddened by the passing of Shimon Peres, his profound legacy of peace and service to his nation will undoubtedly live on. Our thoughts and prayers are with the Peres family and all of our friends in Israel during this difficult time."
