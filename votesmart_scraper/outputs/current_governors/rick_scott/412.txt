Gov. Scott Honors Florida's Military on Florida Purple Heart Day
Statement
By: Rick Scott
Date: Aug. 7, 2015
Location: Tallahassee, FL

Today, Governor Rick Scott recognizes Florida Purple Heart Day, and calls upon Floridians to pay special tribute to those members of the Armed Forces who have received the Purple Heart, while also honoring the families of the fallen.
Governor Rick Scott said, "We are thankful for the sacrifices that our brave military men and women make to protect our country, especially those who have bravely risked their lives in the line of duty. I encourage Florida families to recognize this day to honor our soldiers and their loved ones, and thank them for their brave service."
