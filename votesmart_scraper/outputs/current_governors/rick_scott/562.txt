Governor Scott to Attend VICA Leaders Forum in California on Monday
Press Release
By: Rick Scott
Date: April 10, 2015
Location: Unknown

Governor Rick Scott today announced he will be attending the Valley Industry and Commerce Association (VICA) Leaders Forum during his upcoming trade delegation to California on Monday. During the forum, Governor Scott will discuss how Florida is creating an environment where families and job creators can succeed and why businesses in California should move their operations to Florida.
Governor Scott said, "I am excited to visit California and share our vision of making Florida the number one place in the world for business. We are continuing to cut taxes and eliminate burdensome regulations to make it easier for families and businesses to succeed in Florida."
