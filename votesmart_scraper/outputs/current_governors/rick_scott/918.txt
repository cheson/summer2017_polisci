Gov. Scott: It's Disappointing the US House Rejected Proposal to Stop NFIP Rate Hikes
Statement
By: Rick Scott
Date: Feb. 5, 2014
Location: Unknown

"It's disappointing the US House of Representatives rejected a proposal to delay the unfair national flood insurance rate hikes yesterday. President Obama needs to act immediately to undo these outrageous flood insurance hikes he forced on Floridians."
