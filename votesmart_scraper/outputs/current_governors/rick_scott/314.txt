Governor Rick Scott Announces License-Free Saltwater Fishing Day on Nov. 28
Statement
By: Rick Scott
Date: Dec. 1, 2015
Location: Tallahasse, FL

Today, Governor Rick Scott announced that Saturday, November 28, will be a license--free saltwater fishing day for Florida residents and visitors.
Governor Rick Scott said, "People from all over the world come to Florida to fish in our beautiful waters. We hope families and visitors who are spending time with loved ones this Thanksgiving take advantage of license-free saltwater fishing on Saturday. I look forward to fishing with my grandsons this weekend."
This free day in November is a part of the eight total license-free fishing days the FWC offers each year. All bag limits, seasons and size restrictions apply on these dates. To learn more about license-free fishing days, visit MyFWC.com/License. For fishing tips, go to MyFWC.com/Fishing and select "Saltwater Fishing."
