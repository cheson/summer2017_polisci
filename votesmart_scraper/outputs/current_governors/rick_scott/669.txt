Gov. Scott: Ann and I Wish All Families a Happy Thanksgiving
Statement
By: Rick Scott
Date: Nov. 26, 2014
Location: Tallahassee, FL

Governor Rick Scott issued the following statement for Thanksgiving:
Governor Scott said, "Ann and I join all families across Florida to give thanks for the many blessings our state has received this year. As families gather with their loved ones to celebrate Thanksgiving, I encourage everyone to take time to remember the brave men and women who are defending our nation to protect our freedom. Many of these heroes will not be able to celebrate the Thanksgiving holiday with their loved ones, and our thoughts and prayers are with them during this holiday season."
