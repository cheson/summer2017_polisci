Governor Rick Scott's Statement on the Passing of Rear Admiral Kevin Delaney
Press Release
By: Rick Scott
Date: April 8, 2015
Location: Unknown

Today, Governor Scott made the following statement in regards to the passing of Rear Admiral Kevin Delaney (Ret.).
Governor Scott said, "Ann and I were saddened to learn of the passing of Rear Admiral Kevin Delaney, and send our thoughts and prayers to Kevin's wife, Pat, and all of the Delaney family.
"Kevin was an inspirational leader during his service in the Navy, and he continued to serve our state after his retirement, including as a member of the Florida Defense Support Task Force. I was honored to join Kevin to meet with some of America's brave wounded warriors, and saw firsthand his life-long dedication to serving his fellow military men and women. Kevin was a true hero to our country and the state of Florida."
