Gov. Scott: The VA's Lack of Urgency is Concerning
Statement
By: Rick Scott
Date: April 14, 2014
Location: Unknown

Governor Scott said, "We were told last week that the requested information was forthcoming; this week, we still do not have it. Through the Agency for Health Care Administration's inspections, we are working to bring transparency to the processes of federal VA hospitals because our brave veterans deserve quality health care. The VA's lack of urgency is concerning."
