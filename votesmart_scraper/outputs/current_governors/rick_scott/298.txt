Gov. Scott: Showroom Logic Chooses Miami for Headquarters Over Texas
Press Release
By: Rick Scott
Date: Dec. 15, 2015
Location: Miami, FL

Today, Governor Rick Scott announced that Showroom Logic recently decided to consolidate operations and name Miami as their headquarters location over Texas. Showroom Logic has created 58 new jobs for local families in the past two years, and currently employees 74 Floridians. Showroom Logic is a provider of digital marketing technology and services to automotive manufacturers and works directly with thousands of dealerships across the United States and Canada.
Governor Scott said, "We are proud to announce that Showroom Logic has named Miami for their headquarters over Texas and they have added 58 new jobs in Florida over the past two years. Job creators like Showroom Logic are taking note of our ongoing efforts to cut taxes and make Florida more competitive, and we will continue to do everything possible to overtake Texas to become first for job growth. I look forward to Showroom Logic's continuing success in Florida."
