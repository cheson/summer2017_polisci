Gov. Scott Statement for Yom Kippur
Press Release
By: Rick Scott
Date: Oct. 3, 2014
Location: Unknown

Today, Governor Rick Scott released the following statement for Yom Kippur:
"Yom Kippur is a time of contemplation and prayer and a reminder for all of us to reflect on what we can do to make our state and nation a better place for every family. Ann and I wish our Jewish friends and neighbors a meaningful fast and that they be sealed in the Book of Life."
