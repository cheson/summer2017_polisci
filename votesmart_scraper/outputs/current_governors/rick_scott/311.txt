Florida Leaders Endorse New Florida Enterprise Fund to Create More Jobs
Statement
By: Rick Scott
Date: Dec. 3, 2015
Location: Tallahasse, FL

Local leaders across Florida have voiced their support for the new Florida Enterprise Fund which will create more jobs and diversify Florida's economy. We look forward to more leaders signing on in the upcoming days.
Governor Rick Scott said, "The support we've received from across the state for the new Florida Enterprise Fund has been tremendous. The endorsements from local leaders proves that Florida is focused on creating more jobs and diversifying our economy so our children and grandchildren can find great careers in Florida. I look forward to working with the Legislature during the upcoming legislative session to create the new Florida Enterprise Fund."
