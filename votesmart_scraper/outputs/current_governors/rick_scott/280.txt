Governor Scott Announces R+L Global Logistics Expansion Will Create More Than 80 New Jobs
Press Release
By: Rick Scott
Date: Jan. 4, 2016
Location: Fort Myers, FL

Today, Governor Rick Scott announced that R+L Global Logistics will be expanding to create more than 80 new jobs in Fort Myers over the next three years. The company has already created 88 jobs in Fort Myers, including 31 new jobs in 2015 alone. R+L Global Logistics is a domestic and international transportation company headquartered in Ocala.
Governor Scott said, "Florida companies like R+L Global Logistics have helped create more than one million jobs across our state in just five years. I am proud to highlight their recent growth in Fort Myers and announce their plans to create more than 80 new jobs for families today. We will continue to support Florida job creators by cutting $1 billion in taxes and creating the Florida Enterprise Fund so we can diversify our economy and make Florida first for jobs."
