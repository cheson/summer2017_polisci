Governor Scott Recognizes Aleksander Levental with Young Entrepreneur Award
Statement
By: Rick Scott
Date: Dec. 9, 2015
Location: Tallahassee, FL

Today, during a meeting of Florida's Cabinet, Governor Rick Scott recognized University of Florida graduate Aleksander Levental with the Young Entrepreneur Award. Aleksander attended Valencia College and graduated with a double major in physics and mathematics from the University of Florida. In 2012, he co-founded Feathr which develops iOS apps that provide digital marketing and tools for event organizers.
Governor Scott said, "As the graduate of a community college myself, I am proud to recognize Aleksander with the Young Entrepreneur Award today for his hard work to create his successful company. It is exciting to see students in Florida apply what they learned in college and pursue their dreams of starting a business."
