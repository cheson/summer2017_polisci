Governor Rick Scott Applauds Florida Legislature for Opposing President Obama's Policy on Cuba
Statement
By: Rick Scott
Date: April 8, 2015
Location: Unknown

Today, Governor Scott made the following statement in regards to the Florida Legislature Opposing President Obama's Policy on Cuba.
Governor Scott said, "I'm proud of the actions both the Florida Senate and House have taken this Session to oppose President Obama's policy with Cuba. While the Castro regime continues to work against the freedom of the Cuban people, I will continue to stand in solidarity with the people of Cuba and their pro-Democracy movement."
