Governor Scott Awards Tallahassee Small Business with Governor's Business Ambassador Award
Press Release
By: Rick Scott
Date: Dec. 9, 2015
Location: Tallahassee, FL

Today, at a meeting of Florida's Cabinet, Governor Rick Scott recognized Kenny Small, owner of Tallahassee Welding and Machine Shop, Inc., with the Governor's Business Ambassador Award.
Governor Rick Scott said, "It's an honor to recognize a small business in Tallahassee that is committed to providing opportunities and jobs in our local community. Small business is the life-blood of our economy, and I'm proud to recognize Kenny and his team for making a positive impact over the last 60 years."
