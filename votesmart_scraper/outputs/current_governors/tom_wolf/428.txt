Wolf Administration Announces C&S Wholesale Grocers to Create 500 New Jobs in Northampton County
Statement
By: Tom Wolf
Date: Dec. 1, 2015
Location: Harrisburg, PA

Governor Tom Wolf announced today that C&S Wholesale Grocers, Inc. will create at least 500 new jobs with a new expansion project in Bethlehem, Northampton County.
"Today we are pleased to officially announce the creation of 500 new, full-time jobs through C&S's expansion project," said Governor Wolf. "The reuse of this shuttered facility is a huge win for the commonwealth and the Lehigh Valley region, and serves as a prime example of the importance of remaining engaged in securing new employers and opportunities to yield positive economic impacts for Pennsylvania."
