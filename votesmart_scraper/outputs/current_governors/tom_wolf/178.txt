Governor Wolf Announces GO-TIME Savings of $156 Million in 15-16 Fiscal Year
Press Release
By: Tom Wolf
Date: Aug. 2, 2016
Location: Harrisburg, PA

Governor Wolf announced today that state agencies saved over $156 million through the Governor's Office of Transformation, Innovation, Management and Efficiency (GO-TIME), exceeding the $150 million savings goal for the 15-16 fiscal year.
"Across our departments, leaders and employees are working together and sharing ideas to be more efficient, provide better service, and reduce costs," said Wolf. "Many of these improvements will continue to generate savings and other benefits for years to come."
