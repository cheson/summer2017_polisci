House Bill 1436 Becomes Law
Press Release
By: Tom Wolf
Date: June 12, 2016
Location: Harrisburg, PA

Today, the following bill became law:
Act 40 -- House Bill 1436, sponsored by Rep. Godshall, amends Title 66 (Public Utilities), in rates and distribution systems, providing for computation of income tax expense for ratemaking purposes.

Source: https://www.governor.pa.gov/house-bill-1436-becomes-law/
