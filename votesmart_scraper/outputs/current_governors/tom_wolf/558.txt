Governor Wolf Signs State Emergency Operations Plan Executive Order
By: Tom Wolf
Date: April 17, 2015
Location: Harrisburg, PA

Yesterday, Governor Tom Wolf signed Executive Order 2015-06, which directs that the Pennsylvania Emergency Management Agency (PEMA) develop and manage the Pennsylvania State Emergency Operations Plan.
The plan will allow PEMA to prepare for any potential danger to the commonwealth or its people. The Governor's executive order is effective immediately and the plan will supersede all previous emergency management plans in their entirety and will be implemented consistent with applicable law.
