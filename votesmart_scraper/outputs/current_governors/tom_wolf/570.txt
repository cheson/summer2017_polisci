At Scranton Plastics Plant, Governor Wolf Focuses on Rebuilding the Middle Class, Creating Jobs
Press Release
By: Tom Wolf
Date: March 27, 2015
Location: Scanton, PA

Governor Tom Wolf today visited Quadrant EEP's plastics plant to highlight his proposals to reinvigorate Pennsylvania's manufacturing sector and ensure more workers have the skills that employers demand. Governor Wolf's budget makes significant new investments in manufacturing, economic development and workforce training aimed at rebuilding Pennsylvania's middle class while reducing the Corporate Net Income Tax by 40 percent immediately.
