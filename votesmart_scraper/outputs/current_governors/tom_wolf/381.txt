Governor Wolf Announces Avoided Costs, Waste Prevention as Result of Significant Reduction in SNAP Error Rate
Press Release
By: Tom Wolf
Date: Jan. 20, 2016
Location: Lancaster, PA

Governor Tom Wolf joined the Pennsylvania Department of Human Services (DHS) today to announce a significant reduction in the error rate of the administration of the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP). This is the latest success in the Wolf Administration's "Government that Works" effort to spearhead initiatives that save costs, reduce waste, improve efficiency, and reform government programs.
