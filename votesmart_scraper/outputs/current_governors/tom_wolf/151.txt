Governor Wolf Statement on PennDOT Plan for I-76 Corridor Road, Rail and Trail Improvement
Statement
By: Tom Wolf
Date: Sept. 27, 2016
Location: Harrisburg, PA

Governor Tom Wolf today released the following statement in support of PennDOT's plan to improve travel between King of Prussia and Philadelphia by road, rail and trail:
"The plan announced today to improve transportation by road, rail and trail in Philadelphia's Interstate 76 corridor is an important step forward towards improving infrastructure, traffic management and accessibility no matter how you commute and travel. This roadway and the entire corridor are vital to the economic viability of the region and my administration will continue to prioritize improvements that help drivers, riders and bicyclists move more efficiently."
