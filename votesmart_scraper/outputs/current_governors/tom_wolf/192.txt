Governor Wolf Orders Flags to Remain at Half-Staff to Honor the Victims of the Attack in Baton Rouge, Louisiana
Press Release
By: Tom Wolf
Date: July 19, 2016
Location: Harrisburg, PA

In accordance with the President Barack Obama's proclamation, Governor Tom Wolf has ordered all United States and Commonwealth flags at the Capitol Complex, Commonwealth facilities, and all public buildings and grounds throughout the state to remain lowered to half-staff to honor the victims of the July 17, 2016 attack in Baton Rouge, Louisiana.
Flags will return to full-staff at sunset on July 22, 2016 per the President's order.
