New Ad: Wolf Campaign Releases New Television Ad, Highlights Manufacturing Plan
Press Release
By: Tom Wolf
Date: Aug. 7, 2014
Location: York, PA

Today, as Tom Wolf tours Northeast Building Products in Philadelphia, the Tom Wolf for Governor campaign released a new television ad highlighting his plans to grow Pennsylvania's economy and manufacturing sector. Since the primary, Tom Wolf has been touring the Commonwealth highlighting his "Made in Pennsylvania" manufacturing plan that will improve workforce development and provide incentives for companies to create good manufacturing jobs in Pennsylvania.
