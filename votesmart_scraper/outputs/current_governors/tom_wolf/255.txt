Governor Wolf Statement on School Code
Statement
By: Tom Wolf
Date: May 25, 2016
Location: Harrisburg, PA

Governor Wolf released the following statement regarding the School Code:
"I am pleased to have worked closely with the legislature to begin addressing distressed school districts and to take an important step to ensure the basic education fair funding formula is permanent. I look forward to continue working with leaders in the coming weeks to further address our challenges and reach agreement on a budget that is balanced, fixes the deficit and invests in education at all levels."
