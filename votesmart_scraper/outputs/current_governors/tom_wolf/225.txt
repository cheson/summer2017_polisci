Governor Wolf Resting at Home After Successful Procedure
Statement
By: Tom Wolf
Date: June 14, 2016
Location: Harrisburg, PA

Jeff Sheridan, Governor Wolf's press secretary, released the following statement:
"Governor Wolf is resting at home today after a successful outpatient procedure as part of his previously announced prostate cancer treatment. He will return to the office on Thursday as planned."
