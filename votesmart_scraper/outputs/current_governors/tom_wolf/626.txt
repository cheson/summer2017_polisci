Blog: Fresh Start Bus Tour Rolls Into Central and Western Pennsylvania
Press Release
By: Tom Wolf
Date: Oct. 26, 2014
Location: Unknown

Tom Wolf is traveling Pennsylvania on the Fresh Start Bus Tour in the closing days of this election, making over 30 stops throughout the commonwealth to share his vision for the future.
Yesterday, Tom Wolf continued his statewide tour at grassroots events in Johnstown, Indiana, Leechburg, Greensburg, Uniontown, and Waynesburg.
