Continuing the Fight Against Pennsylvania's Opioid Epidemic
Press Release
By: Tom Wolf
Date: July 18, 2016
Location: Harrisburg, PA

Working with the legislature, Governor Wolf secured critical funding in the budget to establish new treatment centers and improve treatment options for Pennsylvanians struggling with substance abuse disorder.
In 2015, more than 3,300 Pennsylvanians died from a drug overdose. Heroin and opioid overdose are the leading cause of accidental death in Pennsylvania, killing more individuals each year than motor vehicle accidents.
Our fight against this epidemic is not finished.
