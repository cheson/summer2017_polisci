Governor Wolf Statement on House Passage of Pension Reform
Statement
By: Tom Wolf
Date: June 14, 2016
Location: Harrisburg, PA

Governor Tom Wolf today released the following statement on the House passage of pension reform:
"Today, in a bipartisan vote, the House overwhelmingly passed a pension reform compromise that will save billions of dollars while also reducing risk to the taxpayers of Pennsylvania.
"I want to congratulate both Republicans and Democrats for coming together to find common ground on this issue, and I urge the Senate to move quickly to consider this important legislation."
