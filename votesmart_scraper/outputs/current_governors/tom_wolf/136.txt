Governor Wolf Announces Agencies to Accept Unwanted or Expired Prescription Drugs on Saturday, Oct. 22
Press Release
By: Tom Wolf
Date: Oct. 21, 2016
Location: Philadelphia, PA

Governor Tom Wolf today announced that on Saturday, October 22, from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM the Pennsylvania Department of Drug and Alcohol Programs (DDAP), the Pennsylvania State Police (PSP) and the Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) will give the public its 12th opportunity in six years to prevent pill abuse and theft by ridding their homes of potentially dangerous expired, unused, and unwanted prescription drugs as part of National Prescription Drug Take Back Day.
