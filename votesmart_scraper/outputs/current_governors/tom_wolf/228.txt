Statement Regarding Governor Wolf's Prostate Cancer Treatment
Statement
By: Tom Wolf
Date: June 13, 2016
Location: Harrisburg, PA

Jeff Sheridan, Governor Wolf's press secretary, released the following statement:
"Tomorrow, Governor Tom Wolf will have an outpatient procedure as part of his previously announced prostate cancer treatment. He will be spending Tuesday and Wednesday at his home and return to the office on Thursday."

Source: https://www.governor.pa.gov/statement-regarding-governor-wolfs-prostate-cancer-treatment/
