Governor Wolf Orders Flags at Half-Staff to Honor the Victims of the San Bernardino Shootings
Statement
By: Tom Wolf
Date: Dec. 3, 2015
Location: Harrisburg, PA

In accordance with the President's proclamation, Governor Tom Wolf has ordered all United States and Pennsylvania flags at the Capitol Complex, Commonwealth facilities and all public buildings and grounds throughout the state lowered to half-staff effective immediately. This is a mark of respect for the victims of gun violence that took place on December 2, 2015, in San Bernardino, California.
"Pennsylvanians thoughts are with the victims, their families, and the entire San Bernardino community following tragic mass shooting there," said Governor Tom Wolf. "This violence is absolutely senseless."
