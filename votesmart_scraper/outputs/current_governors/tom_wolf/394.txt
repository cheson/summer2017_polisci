Governor Wolf Statement on Ambushed Philadelphia Police Officer Jesse Hartnett
Statement
By: Tom Wolf
Date: Jan. 8, 2016
Location: Harrisburg, PA

Governor Wolf released the following statement:
"We are thankful that Officer Hartnett is alive and not facing life-threatening injuries after being ambushed. We wish him and his family the best during his recovery. This alleged intentional act of violence against an officer seeking to help a fellow citizen is horrifying and has no place in Pennsylvania."
