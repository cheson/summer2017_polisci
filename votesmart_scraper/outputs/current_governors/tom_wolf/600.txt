Governor Wolf Appoints Six to Delaware River Port Authority
Press Release
By: Tom Wolf
Date: March 4, 2015
Location: Harrisburg, PA

Governor Tom Wolf announced the appointment of six professionals from Philadelphia and the surrounding counties to six year terms on the board of the Delaware River Port Authority.
"These qualified appointees will bring important expertise and diverse perspectives to the Delaware River Port Authority," Governor Wolf said. "DRPA's work is vital to our economic success and these professionals will help the Commonwealth create jobs that pay for middle-class families."
Governor Appointees:
Elinor Haider, Philadelphia
Vice President Market Development, Veolia North America
Antonio Fiol-Silva, Philadelphia
Principal, Wallace Roberts & Todd
Ryan Boyer, Philadelphia
Business Manager, Laborers' District Council of Metropolitan Area of Philadelphia and Vicinity
Rohan K. Hepkins, Delaware County
Mayor, Borough of Yeadon
Marian Moskowitz, Chester County
Owner, Franklin Commons
Whitney R. White, Philadelphia
Chairman, The Global Institute for Strategic Investment
