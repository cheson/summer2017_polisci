Gov. Fallin Welcomes Approximately 900 Boeing Employees to Oklahoma
Press Release
By: Mary Fallin
Date: Sept. 29, 2014
Location: Oklahoma City, OK

Governor Mary Fallin today released the following statement after Boeing announced it would be bringing approximately 900 employees to its Oklahoma City operations. (Read more here: http://boeing.mediaroom.com/9-29-14-Boeing-to-Consolidate-Defense-Services-and-Support-Work-in-Oklahoma-City-and-St-Louis)
"Oklahoma looks forward to welcoming 900 new Boeing employees to our community. We will work to ensure this transition goes smoothly for the families who are affected. I am certain they will enjoy Oklahoma's community-focused environment and our great quality of life.
"Oklahoma looks forward to continuing a long and successful partnership with Boeing and its employees." -- Governor Mary Fallin
