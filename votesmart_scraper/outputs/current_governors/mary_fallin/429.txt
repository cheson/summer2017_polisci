Gov. Fallin Comments on Veterans Day
Statement
By: Mary Fallin
Date: Nov. 11, 2013
Location: Oklahoma City, OK

Governor Mary Fallin released the following statement concerning Veterans Day:

"We owe our veterans and active-duty military men and women a huge debt for their courage and sacrifice. They protect our freedoms and keep us safe, and they do so at a great cost to themselves and their families.

"This November 11, I'm asking all Oklahomans to join me in thanking our veterans for everything they have done for this country and for the people of Oklahoma. We should never forget their dedication and their service.

"Please join me in saying a prayer for our men and women in the military and in setting aside time to remember our fallen heroes."
