Gov. Fallin Issues Statement on the Upcoming Memorial Day Holiday
Statement
By: Mary Fallin
Date: May 23, 2014
Location: Oklahoma City, OK

Governor Mary Fallin today issued the following statement on the Memorial Day Holiday:
"As we celebrate this Memorial Day weekend, I encourage Oklahomans to take a moment to remember and honor those who have fought and died to defend the freedom we continue to enjoy every day.
"Their courage and sacrifice reminds us that freedom comes with a cost, and we are blessed to have so many patriots who are willing to fight and sacrifice for this country and the freedom and liberty it represents.
"I'm wishing Oklahomans a safe and fun Memorial Day weekend. I know they will join me in keeping our former and current military personnel in their thoughts and prayers."
