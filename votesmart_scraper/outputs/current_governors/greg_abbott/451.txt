Issue Position: Promoting Self-Sufficiency Over Government Welfare
Issue Position
By: Gregory Abbott
Location: Unknown

General Abbott's office has collected over $13,816,024,291 in child support. These funds help provide basic necessities for children allowing them to flourish and succeed in life. The funds collected by the Attorney General's Office also reduce the chance of children or their families enrolling in welfare.
