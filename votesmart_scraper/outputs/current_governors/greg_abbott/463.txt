Issue Position: Helping Foster Safety on our Streets and Online
Issue Position
By: Gregory Abbott
Location: Unknown

Launching units focused on both Fugitive and Cyber Crimes, General Abbott has made removing dangerous individuals from our communities a top priority. Over 1400 fugitives, sex offenders, and parole violators have been arrested as a result of the Attorney General's Fugitive and Cyber Crimes Units. 1075 were related to child sex offenses.
