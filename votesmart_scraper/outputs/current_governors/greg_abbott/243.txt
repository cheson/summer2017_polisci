Governor Abbott Statement On Texas Voter ID Law
Statement
By: Gregory Abbott
Date: Aug. 5, 2015
Location: Austin, TX

Governor Greg Abbott today released the following statement after the Fifth Circuit Court issued its decision on Texas' voter ID law:
"In light of ongoing voter fraud, it is imperative that Texas has a voter ID law that prevents cheating at the ballot box. Texas will continue to fight for its voter ID requirement to ensure the integrity of elections in the Lone Star State."

Source: http://gov.texas.gov/news/press-release/21284
