Governor Abbott Meets With Nuevo León Governor Jaime Rodríguez Calderón
Statement
By: Gregory Abbott
Date: March 31, 2016
Location: Austin, TX

Governor Greg Abbott today hosted Nuevo León Governor Jaime Rodríguez Calderón at the Texas Capitol to discuss collaboration on energy, trade, transportation and border security.
During the meeting, the Governors discussed transnational energy infrastructure and collaboration on strengthening trade and commercial ties between Texas and Nuevo León. Governor Abbott thanked Governor Rodríguez for engaging on issues crucial to citizens on both sides of the border and expressed his sincere hope to continue advancing Texas' partnership with Nuevo León.
