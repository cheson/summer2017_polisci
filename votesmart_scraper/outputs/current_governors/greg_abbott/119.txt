Governor Abbott Statement On South Texas Bus Crash
Statement
By: Gregory Abbott
Date: May 14, 2016
Location: Austin, TX

Governor Greg Abbott today issued the following statement after a charter bus overturned in Webb County:
"Cecilia and I extend our deepest condolences to those affected by the tragic accident in South Texas today. I ask the entire State of Texas to keep the families of loved ones lost today, as well as the Webb County community, in its thoughts and prayers during this difficult time."

Source: http://gov.texas.gov/news/press-release/22313
