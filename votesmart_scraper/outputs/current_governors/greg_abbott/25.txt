Governor Abbott Statement On Terror Attacks In London
Statement
By: Gregory Abbott
Date: June 3, 2017
Location: Austin, TX

Governor Greg Abbott today released the following statement on the terror attacks in London this evening:

"Cecilia and I ask that all Texans join us in praying for the victims of the attacks in London this evening. As a nation, we must continue to do everything we can to root out and defeat terrorists who aim to destroy our values of freedom and democracy. Texas offers its condolences and full support for those affected by this tragedy. "
