Governor Abbott Meets With Security And Energy Officials; Gives Keynote Address In Mexico City
Press Release
By: Gregory Abbott
Date: Sept. 7, 2015
Location: Austin, TX

Governor Greg Abbott spent Monday meeting with several high-ranking Mexican Government officials and delivering a keynote address at an economic development luncheon:
"Opportunities to advance the Texas-Mexico relationship are plentiful, and by meeting with high ranking Mexico officials today, we have successfully identified areas that will deepen our partnership," said Governor Abbott. "By enhancing security on both sides of the border, diversifying our economies and investing wisely in shared energy and water resources, I'm confident the State of Texas and Mexico can create more opportunity and an even better future for all."
