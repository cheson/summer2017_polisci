FEMA Disaster Declaration Granted For Additional Texas Counties
Statement
By: Gregory Abbott
Date: Dec. 24, 2015
Location: Albany, NY

On Thursday, December 24th, FEMA granted Governor Greg Abbott's request for Public Assistance for 16 counties affected by severe weather.
"I want to thank FEMA for their prompt response to Texas' request for Public Assistance," said Governor Abbott. "Texas is working with our partners at FEMA to ensure that all Texans receive the assistance they need to continue to rebuild their lives following the devastating floods earlier this year."

Source: http://gov.texas.gov/news/press-release/21790
