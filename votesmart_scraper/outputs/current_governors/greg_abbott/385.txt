Web Ad: Get Out And Vote
Press Release
By: Gregory Abbott
Date: Oct. 30, 2014
Location: Austin, TX

As Texans enter the final days of early voting and Greg Abbott heads to the polls today, Texans for Greg Abbott released a new web ad featuring supporters across the state encouraging their fellow Texans to get out the vote for Greg Abbott.
"Election Day is happening right now -- if you haven't voted, go vote, and make sure that your family, friends and neighbors vote," said Greg Abbott. "I am asking for every Texan to get out and vote so we can keep Texas the best state in the country -- and build an even better future for our great state."
