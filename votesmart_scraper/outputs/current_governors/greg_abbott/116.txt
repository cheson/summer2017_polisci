Governor Abbott Sets Date For Special Runoff Election In HD 120
Press Release
By: Gregory Abbott
Date: May 18, 2016
Location: Austin, TX

Governor Greg Abbott today issued a proclamation setting August 2, 2016 as the date for the special runoff election to fill the Texas House District 120 seat in Bexar County being vacated by Representative Ruth Jones McClendon.
The early voting period for this runoff election will be held from July 25, 2016 to July 29, 2016.
