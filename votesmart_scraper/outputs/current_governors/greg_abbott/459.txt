Issue Position: Defending Gun Owner Rights
Issue Position
By: Gregory Abbott
Location: Unknown

On behalf of 31 states, Attorney General Abbott helped champion the defense of citizens' right to keep and bear arms before the United States Supreme Court. The landmark US Supreme Court decision affirmed individuals' constitutional right to bear arms and struck down the District of Columbia's handgun ban.
