Issue Position: Safeguarding Consumers
Issue Position
By: Gregory Abbott
Location: Unknown

From malicious internet scams to deceptive financial services, the Office of the Attorney General has litigated and enforced Texas statute to protect Texas consumers. During times of natural disaster, the Office has aggressively sought to prevent disaster scams such as price gouging, door-to-door clean up and repair, and reprehensible fake charity solicitations.
