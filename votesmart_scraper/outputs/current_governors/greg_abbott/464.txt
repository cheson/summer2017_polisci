Issue Position: Ensuring Affordable, Quality Healthcare
Issue Position
By: Gregory Abbott
Location: Unknown

Stopping fraudulent, deceptive, and illegal practices within the healthcare system has been a hallmark of General Abbott's tenure. The Attorney General has taken strong action to 1) stop fraudulent health discount cards from being sold to Texans without health insurance, 2) end deceptive marketing of unproven dietary supplements, and 3) promote fair competition by health care providers.
