Issue Position: Assisting Texas Children and Families
Issue Position
By: Gregory Abbott
Location: Unknown

One of the most important aspects of the Attorney General's responsibilities is the collection of child support. Child support directly benefits the children and saves taxpayer money by avoiding additional state welfare. Since taking office, Attorney General Abbott has collected over $13,816,024,291 for Texas children.
