Issue Position: Defending Texas Jobs
Issue Position
By: Gregory Abbott
Location: Unknown

When federally controlled General Motors sought to gut Texas' law and put Texas car dealerships and their employees at risk, Attorney General Abbott objected in GM's federal bankruptcy proceedings. 415 dealerships and 27,000 Texans are potentially affected by GM's proposed course of action.
