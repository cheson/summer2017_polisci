Statement On Governor Abbott's Call With President Obama
Press Release
By: Gregory Abbott
Date: July 9, 2016
Location: Austin, TX

Governor Greg Abbott today spoke with President Obama who called to offer his condolences for the five officers killed in Dallas on Thursday night. The governor thanked the President for the call and reiterated the need for Americans to come together and unite in the wake of this tragedy.
