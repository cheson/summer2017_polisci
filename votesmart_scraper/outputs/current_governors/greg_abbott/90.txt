Federal Disaster Declaration Granted For Additional Texas Counties
Press Release
By: Gregory Abbott
Date: July 8, 2016
Location: Austin, TX

The federal government today granted Governor Greg Abbott's federal disaster request for Public Assistance for Bandera, Bastrop, Brown, Caldwell, Eastland, Fayette, Fort Bend, Grimes, Hood, Houston, Jasper, Kleberg, Polk, San Jacinto and Washington Counties, amending the June 11th disaster declaration following severe flooding across the State of Texas in May.

Additional counties may be added to the Governor's request for assistance once they meet the threshold required by the federal government to request federal disaster aid.
