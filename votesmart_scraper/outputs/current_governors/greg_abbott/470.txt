Issue Position: Helping Homeowners, Fighting Mortgage Fraud
Issue Position
By: Gregory Abbott
Location: Unknown

Attorney General Abbott has successfully litigated against large mortgage companies to help homeowners avoid foreclosure and shore up communities and markets that have been affected by the residential mortgage lending crisis. Additionally, General Abbott has worked cooperatively with federal, state, and local officials to proactively crack down on mortgage fraud related to falsification of loan documentation, fraudulent foreclosure scams, and deceptive lending practices. His efforts have led to the successful prosecution and lengthy prison terms for perpetrators.
