Governor Abbott Statement On Vandalism Of San Antonio Synagogue
Statement
By: Gregory Abbott
Date: Aug. 17, 2015
Location: Austin, TX

Governor Greg Abbott today issued a statement following the second instance of anti-Semitic and racially-motivated vandalism at a synagogue in San Antonio:
"Today's second instance of anti-Semitic vandalism in San Antonio is an offensive and disturbing attack on people of all faiths. Religious discrimination has no place in the State of Texas and I have faith that law enforcement spearheading this investigation will redouble their efforts to swiftly bring those responsible to justice."
