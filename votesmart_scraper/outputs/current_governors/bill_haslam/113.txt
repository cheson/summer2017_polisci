Statement of Gov. Bill Haslam on The Retirement of Tennessee Supreme Court Justice Gary Wade
Statement
By: Bill Haslam
Date: July 24, 2015
Location: Unknown

"I want to thank Justice Wade for his years of service to Tennessee as a dedicated member of the judiciary. I greatly appreciate Gary's commitment to justice and his love for our state. Tennessee will miss his service on the Supreme Court, and I am grateful for his good work."
