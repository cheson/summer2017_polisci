Public Statements
Issue Position: Fiscal Strength and Efficient Government
Issue Position
By: Bill Haslam
Date: Jan. 1, 2017
Location: Nashville, TN

Governor Haslam's Goal: To be the best managed state, providing high-quality services at the lowest possible price to taxpayers.
Tennessee has a proud tradition of being a fiscally conservative state, and Governor Haslam is working hard to ensure state government continues to be well managed with the lowest possible tax burden to residents. The Haslam administration is building off the state's record of good fiscal management by simultaneously building reserves, cutting taxes, and eliminating waste and inefficiencies from state government. Governor Haslam believes taxpayers are the state's customers, and he is striving to change the culture within state government and make Tennessee the best managed state in the country. - See more at: https://www.tn.gov/transparenttn/section/Fiscal-Strength-Efficient-Government#sthash.erihIgUB.dpuf
