Governor Dayton Issues Emergency Executive Order 16-04
Press Release
By: Mark Dayton
Date: May 16, 2016
Location: St. Paul, MN

Today, Governor Mark Dayton issued Emergency Executive Order 16-04, directing the Minnesota National Guard's Adjutant General to provide personnel and equipment for Prairie Island Nuclear Generating Plant safety drills and exercises.
