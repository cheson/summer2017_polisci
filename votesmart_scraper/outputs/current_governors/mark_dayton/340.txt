Statement from Governor Dayton on Minnesota Hosting the 2019 NCAA Final Four Tournament
Statement
By: Mark Dayton
Date: Nov. 14, 2014
Location: St. Paul, MN

Today, Minnesota won its bid to host the 2019 NCAA Final Four Tournament. The following is a statement from Governor Mark Dayton.
"Being chosen to host the 2019 NCAA Final Four is great news for Minnesota. That weekend's drama and excitement will bring tens of thousands of fans and millions of dollars in economic benefits to our state."
"I want to thank our Host Committee's leaders, including Co-Chairs Mary Brainerd, President and CEO of Health Partners, and David Mortenson, President of Mortenson Construction, Meet Minneapolis CEO Melvin Tennant, U of M Athletic Director Norwood Teague, and Sports Facilities Chair Michele Kelm-Helgen, for their tremendous work in securing this premier event for our new stadium and our state."
