Governor Dayton Proclaims September 30 Small Business Day in Minnesota
Statement
By: Mark Dayton
Date: Sept. 29, 2015
Location: St. Paul, MN

Governor Mark Dayton has declared Wednesday, Sept. 30, 2015, to be Small Business Day in the state of Minnesota. The Governor will present the proclamation at the premiere of the new documentary called "Small Business Revolution."
"Small businesses are the backbone of Minnesota's economy," said Governor Dayton. "Our small businesses provide high quality services and products, support strong communities, and provide jobs and opportunity for people across Minnesota. I am proud to celebrate their contributions and successes."
