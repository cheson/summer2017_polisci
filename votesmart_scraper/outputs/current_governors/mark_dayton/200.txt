Governor Dayton to Host Fifth Annual Governor's Pheasant Opener in Mankato, Celebrate Highway 14 Expansion, Drop Puck at MSU Mankato Hockey Game
Statement
By: Mark Dayton
Date: Oct. 8, 2015
Location: St. Paul, MN

This weekend, Governor Mark Dayton will travel to Mankato to host the Fifth Annual Governor's Pheasant Hunting Opener. Celebrating the pheasant opener is a long-standing Minnesota tradition -- and one that Governor Dayton has highlighted by hosting Governor's Pheasant Opener events in each of his five years as Governor.
"For sixty years, I have enjoyed pheasant hunting in Minnesota," said Governor Mark Dayton. "Over the past five years, we have held terrific Governor's Pheasant Hunting Opener events in Minnesota. I thank our wonderful hosts in Mankato and the surrounding area for all of their hard work and gracious hospitality."
