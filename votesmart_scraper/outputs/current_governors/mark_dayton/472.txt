Public Statements
Statement from Governor Mark Dayton
Statement
By: Mark Dayton
Date: March 24, 2014
Location: Unknown

Governor Mark Dayton today released the following statement after MNsure announced that Minnesotans who have attempted to enroll in health coverage by the March 31st deadline will be eligible for an extension.
"I strongly support MNsure's decision to extend the enrollment period for Minnesotans who have tried to enroll in health care coverage by the March 31st deadline."
