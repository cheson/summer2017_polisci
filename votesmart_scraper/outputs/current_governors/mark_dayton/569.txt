Governor Dayton Signs Omnibus Bonding Bill
Statement
By: Mark Dayton
Date: May 11, 2012
Location: Unknown

Today, Governor Mark Dayton signed House File 1752, the Omnibus Bonding Bill.
A full list of projects can be found on the Minnesota House of Representatives website here: http://www.house.leg.state.mn.us/fiscal/files/bond12_2.pdf
At an end of session press conference, Governor Dayton made the following remarks in regard to the Bonding Bill:
"I've just signed the bonding bill. It's not as much as I had hoped for, but [...] I signed the bill, and most of them are good and important projects, ones that will benefit the people of Minnesota, and benefit the institutions where they are going, and most importantly, will put thousands of people throughout Minnesota to work, which was the number one priority of this legislative session."
