Governor Mark Dayton Orders Flags Flown at Half-Staff in Honor of the Victims of the Attack in Brussels, Belgium
By: Mark Dayton
Date: March 22, 2016
Location: St. Paul, MN

In accordance with the proclamation issued by President Barack Obama, Governor Mark Dayton has ordered all United States flags and Minnesota flags to be flown at half-staff at all state and federal buildings in the State of Minnesota. As directed by the President, flags will be flown at half-staff beginning immediately, until sunset on Saturday, March 26, 2016, as a mark of respect for the victims of the terrible acts of violence perpetrated on March 22, 2016, in Brussels, Belgium.
