Public Statements
Vote Explanation
By: Mark Dayton
Date: July 25, 2003
Location: Washington, DC

VOTE EXPLANATION
    Mr. DAYTON. Mr. President, yesterday, I was absent from the Senate, attending the funeral of Kenneth N. Dayton, my uncle. If I had been present, I would have voted "aye" on the motion to waive the Budget Act for Senator Dodd's amendment No. 1363, rollcall vote No. 299. I also would have voted "aye" on the motion to waive the Budget Act for Senator Specter's amendment No. 1368, rollcall vote No. 301.
