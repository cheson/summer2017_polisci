Public Statements
Dayton response to Capitol Security Report
Press Release
By: Mark Dayton
Date: April 1, 2011
Location: Unknown

n response to a report prepared by the Committee on Capitol Complex Security, which was released this morning, Governor Mark Dayton said:

"I thank the Committee on Capitol Complex Security for their important work to assure the safety of all Minnesotans who work at or visit our State Capitol. I fully support their recommendations.

I have assigned Minnesota Commissioner of Public Safety Mona Dohman to lead all future Capitol security initiatives, as recommended by the committee."
