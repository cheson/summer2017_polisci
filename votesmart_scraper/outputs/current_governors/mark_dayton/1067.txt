Statement on the National Transportation Safety Board Findings on the Crash of Senator Paul Wellstone's Plane
By: Mark Dayton
Date: Nov. 18, 2003
Location: Unknown

STATEMENT OF SENATOR MARK DAYTON ON THE NATIONAL TRANSPORTATION SAFETY BOARD FINDINGS ON THE CRASH OF SENATOR PAUL WELLSTONE'S PLANE
The terrible airplane crash which took the lives of Senator Paul and Sheila Wellstone, their daughter, and five other Minnesotans becomes even worse after the National Transportation Safety Board ruling today. All extreme factors were eliminated, leaving as the probable cause the serious deficiencies of the pilot.
No findings could restore the lives of Paul and Sheila. Yet, the senselessness of this disaster raises the question, again and again: Why?
