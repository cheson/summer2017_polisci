Statement from Governor Mark Dayton on Supreme Court Decision on Same-Sex Marriage
Statement
By: Mark Dayton
Date: June 26, 2015
Location: St. Paul, MN

Today, Governor Mark Dayton made the following statement after the Supreme Court released its decision to uphold marriage equality across the country:
"What a great day for America!" said Governor Mark Dayton. "This decision is a huge step forward, toward equal rights and guarantees for all citizens under our Constitution."
