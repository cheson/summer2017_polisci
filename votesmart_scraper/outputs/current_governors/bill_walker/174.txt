Alaskans Invited to Holiday Open House
Press Release
By: Bill Walker
Date: Dec. 4, 2015
Location: Juneau, AK

Governor Bill Walker, First Lady Donna Walker, Lieutenant Governor Byron Mallott, and Mrs. Mallott are pleased to invite the community of Juneau to the Governor's annual Holiday Open House on Tuesday, December 8 from 3:00 to 6:00pm at the Governor's House, 716 Calhoun Avenue, in Juneau.
The Governor's House was built in 1912 and the first open house was held by Territorial Governor Walter Eli Clark and his family on New Year's Day 1913. Open houses have been held every year, with the exception of two years during World War II.
"Donna and I are so happy to be a part of this wonderful Alaska tradition," said Governor Walker. "This is such a special time of year because it brings together family and friends, and we look forward to celebrating with our neighbors."
