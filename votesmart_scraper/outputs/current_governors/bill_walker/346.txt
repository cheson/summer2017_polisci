Issue Position: 2nd Amendment
Issue Position
By: Bill Walker
Date: Jan. 1, 2014
Location: Unknown

I believe that the 2nd Amendment is an individual right. I will work to protect and preserve the right of Alaskans to keep and bear arms.
I further support the right of open and concealed carry by law abiding citizens.

Source: http://www.billwalkerforgovernor.com/on-the-issues/2d-amendment/
