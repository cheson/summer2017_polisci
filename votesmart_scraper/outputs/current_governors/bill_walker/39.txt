Governor Walker Applauds Secretary of the Interior Confirmation
Statement
By: Bill Walker
Date: March 1, 2017
Location: Juneau, AK

Governor Bill Walker applauded the confirmation of Secretary of the Interior Ryan Zinke, who was approved by the Senate today in a 68-31 vote. The Governor issued the following statement on Secretary Zinke's confirmation:
"I congratulate Interior Secretary Ryan Zinke on his Senate confirmation. As a state where more than 65 percent of land is controlled by the federal government, it is crucial that the Interior Department understand the nuances of Alaska, and the issues we face. Our administration looks forward to working with Secretary Zinke and members of his department in the years ahead." -- Governor Bill Walker
