Governor Walker Thanks Senate for Passing the Permanent Fund Protection Act
Statement
By: Bill Walker
Date: March 15, 2017
Location: Juneau, AK

Governor Bill Walker issued the following statement on today's passage of the Permanent Fund Protection Act from the Alaska Senate:
"The Senate showed great leadership today in passing the Permanent Fund Protection Act. I thank members of the Senate for once again taking a significant step toward building a more stable future for Alaska. Their courageous actions show we are making progress toward a complete solution. I look forward to working with legislators in both chambers to reach the finish line on fixing Alaska." -- Governor Bill Walker
