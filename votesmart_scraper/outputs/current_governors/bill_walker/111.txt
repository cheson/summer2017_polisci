Public Statements
Alaska Peony Month
Press Release
By: Bill Walker
Date: July 1, 2016
Location: Juneau, AK

WHEREAS, Alaska's emerging peony industry is one of the fastest-growing and most promising agriculture endeavors in our state's history; and
WHEREAS, Alaskan peonies are the only global source of fresh peonies during the months of July, August, and September; and
WHEREAS, in 2011 there were 16,000 harvestable peony stems in the state of Alaska, and the predicted harvest will be over 200,000 stems in 2016, and could grow to well over a million harvestable stems by 2020; and
WHEREAS, the production value of an acre of peonies is $50,000-$100,000; and
WHEREAS, the number of commercial peony growers has increased from nine in 2011 to over 200 in 2016; and
WHEREAS, Alaska peony growers sell to local, state, domestic, and international markets, including Taiwan, Canada, and Korea; and
WHEREAS, the peony industry provides a wide variety of employment opportunities for Alaskans and makes a notable impact on local economies, as well as the state-wide economy.
NOW THEREFORE, I, Bill Walker, GOVERNOR OF THE STATE OF ALASKA, do hereby proclaim July 2016 as:
Alaska Peony Month
in Alaska, and encourage all Alaskans to celebrate the accomplishments of the state's peony growers and recognize the peony as a strong agricultural industry in the state of Alaska.
