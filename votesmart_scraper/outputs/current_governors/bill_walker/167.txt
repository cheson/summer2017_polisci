Four Companies Recognized for Excellence
Press Release
By: Bill Walker
Date: Dec. 15, 2015
Location: Anchorage, AK

Governor Bill Walker announced the winners of the 2015 Governor's North Star Awards for International Excellence. The winners are Korean Air for transportation, Fugro for scientific exchange, Alaska Skylar Travel for foreign investment, and Northern Alaska Tour Company for tourism.
"It's a pleasure to recognize the important work of these businesses that have forged strong commercial ties to other countries," Governor Walker said. "Alaska benefits when we are connected to as many world markets as possible."
