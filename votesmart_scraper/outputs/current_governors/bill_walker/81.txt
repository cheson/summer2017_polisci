Governor Walker Congratulates President-Elect Donald Trump
Statement
By: Bill Walker
Date: Nov. 9, 2016
Location: Anchorage, AK

"As a non-partisan governor, I have enjoyed working with members of the Obama administration on issues important to Alaskans and the nation. Lieutenant Governor Byron Mallott and I look forward to continuing this critical work with President-Elect Donald Trump and the Trump administration as we maintain our pursuit of responsible resource development while balancing the needs of communities, regions and industries." -- Governor Bill Walker
