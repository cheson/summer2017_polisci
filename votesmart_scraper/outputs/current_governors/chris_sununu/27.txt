Governor Chris Sununu Statement on State Senate Vote to Pass Constitutional Carry Legislation
Statement
By: Chris Sununu
Date: Jan. 19, 2017
Location: Concord, NH

Following today's vote in the State Senate to pass Constitutional Carry legislation, Governor Chris Sununu issued the following statement:
"I am pleased that the State Senate today voted to advance common sense legislation in support of a citizen's fundamental right to carry a firearm, joining neighboring states throughout the region and across the country."
