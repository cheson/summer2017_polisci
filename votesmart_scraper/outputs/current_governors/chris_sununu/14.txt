Public Statements
Governor Chris Sununu Statement on SB 191
Statement
By: Chris Sununu
Date: April 25, 2017
Location: Concord, NH

Following the House Education Committee's vote in support of SB 191 with amendment, Governor Chris Sununu issued the following statement:
"Today's actions are a significant step forward for New Hampshire. Full-day kindergarten is good for children, families, and a critical tool in retaining our workforce."
