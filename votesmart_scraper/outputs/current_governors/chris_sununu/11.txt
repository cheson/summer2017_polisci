Governor Chris Sununu Statement on HB 400
Statement
By: Chris Sununu
Date: May 11, 2017
Location: Concord, NH

Following the New Hampshire Senate's actions to pass HB 400, Governor Chris Sununu issued the following statement:
"Improving our state's mental health system and reforming DCYF are not options. They are not areas that we have the luxury of putting off for another day. They are absolute necessities that our state must address immediately. This legislation is a critical step forward."
