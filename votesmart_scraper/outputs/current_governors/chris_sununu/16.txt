Governor Chris Sununu Statement on SB38
Statement
By: Chris Sununu
Date: April 24, 2017
Location: Concord, NH

Governor Chris Sununu's statement on SB 9, an act relative to the admissibility of proffered evidence in sexual assault cases:
"I encourage legislators to support Senate Bill 9, as passed by the Senate, to help ensure another family does not go through the painful and lengthy legal battle the Marriott family has had to undertake to protect their daughter's privacy."

Source: https://www.governor.nh.gov/news-media/press-2017/20170424-sb-9.htm
