Chris Sununu Statement on Operation Granite Hammer
Statement
By: Chris Sununu
Date: July 9, 2016
Location: Unknown

In reaction to the New Hampshire Legislature's vote to approve funding for Operation Granite Hammer, Chris Sununu released the following statement:
"I want to congratulate the House and Senate on its approval of funding for Operation Granite Hammer -- an important program that has helped to provide local communities across New Hampshire with the resources they need to effectively combat the drug epidemic. Addressing complex issues like this crisis requires bold, collaborative leadership and today, in passing this bill, the House and Senate expressed just that."
