Governor Chris Sununu Statement on SB 191
Statement
By: Chris Sununu
Date: June 1, 2017
Location: Concord, NH

Today following the House passage of SB 191, Governor Chris Sununu issued the following statement:
"The House of Representatives deserves high praise for moving full-day kindergarten forward and recognizing that this is a priority for many families in both considering the quality of a community's public education and in their decision-making process when choosing a place to raise their children."
