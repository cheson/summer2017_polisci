Governor Chris Sununu Statement on SB 3
Statement
By: Chris Sununu
Date: June 1, 2017
Location: Concord, NH

Today following the House passage of SB 3, relative to domicile for voting purposes, Governor Chris Sununu issued the following statement:
"This legislation helps protect the integrity of New Hampshire's electoral process. As host of the First in the Nation primary, New Hampshire has the obligation to ensure our system is beyond reproach. This bill does exactly that and as such, I support SB 3 and commend the House of Representatives for their actions today."
