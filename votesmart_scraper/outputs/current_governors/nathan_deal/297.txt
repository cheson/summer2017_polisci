Mando America Facilities Creates 1,086 Jobs & Invests $280M in Meriwether County
Press Release
By: Nathan Deal
Date: July 28, 2014
Location: Unknown

Gov. Nathan Deal celebrated the opening of two Mando America facilities, creating 1,086 jobs & investing $280M in Meriwether County.
"Mando's decision to build and expand in Georgia is an indicator of the strength of our competitive business climate," said Deal. "It is partnerships like this that have enriched and solidified Georgia's status as the No. 1 state in the nation in which to do business."
