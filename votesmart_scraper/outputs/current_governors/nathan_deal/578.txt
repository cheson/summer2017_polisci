"Prosperity" Ad Details Jobs Plan
Press Release
By: Nathan Deal
Date: Sept. 29, 2010
Location: Washington, DC

Republican nominee for governor Nathan Deal is airing a new commercial titled "Prosperity." It gives details of his Real Prosperity plan to bring jobs to Georgia.

The ad, filmed in an industrial location, discusses the recessions severe impact on Georgia's working families and Nathan Deal's Real Prosperity plan to get our economy moving again.
Deal narrates the script himself:
Nathan Deal: "The recession hit Georgia hard. Plants closed. People lost their jobs. Too many others fear they might. I'll bring Real Prosperity to Georgia with tax cuts that create jobs. We'll cut taxes on businesses that already call Georgia home, incentivize private lending pools and cut government red tape so new businesses come here and bring their jobs with them."

The ad is airing in the Atlanta, Columbus, Augusta, Macon, Savannah and Albany media markets.
