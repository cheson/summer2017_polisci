Press Release
By: Rick Snyder
Date: June 13, 2014
Location: Unknown

Gov. Rick Snyder's proposed fiscal year 2015 budget -- the product of three consecutive years of strict fiscal discipline -- continues Michigan's comeback by significantly investing in core priorities that will drive job creation, student success in the classroom, stronger communities, increased public health and safety, and tax relief for low- to middle-income families.
Budget Presentation
FY 2015 Executive Budget
Governor Snyder's fourth budget recommendation is grounded in fiscal responsibility and designed to fuel the state's continued comeback. As Michigan's economic turnaround progresses, the governor remains dedicated to advancing the state's reinvention.
Michigan is back on track with more than 220,000 new private sector jobs since December 2010, is leading the nation in manufacturing job growth, and is seeing rising personal income, home sales and home values.
