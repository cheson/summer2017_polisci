Gov. Rick Snyder's Statement Regarding Unemployment Rates Falling in all Regional Labor Markets
Statement
By: Rick Snyder
Date: May 25, 2017
Location: Lansing, MI

Gov. Rick Snyder issued the following statement regarding today's announcement that unemployment rates have fallen in all regional labor markets:
"For the second month in a row regional unemployment rates have dropped in all 83 counties. This is great news and proves once again that we are creating more and better opportunities for Michiganders in every corner of this state. In order to keep this momentum going, we are working hard and taking the necessary steps to continue on a positive path toward our future," Gov. Snyder said.
