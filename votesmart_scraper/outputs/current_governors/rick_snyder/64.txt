Issue Position: Building Roads for Michigan's Future
Issue Position
By: Rick Snyder
Date: Jan. 1, 2017
Location: Unknown

Governor Snyder has announced a sustainable, long-term solution to improve Michigan's roads and bridges, making them safer for Michigan's families. The plan approved invests $1.2 billion for Michigan's roads and bridges, the largest investment in a half-century. It is a fiscally responsibly plan to continues to protect the key investments necessary to strengthen Michigan's comeback.
