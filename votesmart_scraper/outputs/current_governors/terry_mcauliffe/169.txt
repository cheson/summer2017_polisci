Governor McAuliffe Announces 500 New Jobs in Wise County
Press Release
By: Terry McAuliffe
Date: June 29, 2016
Location: Richmond, VA

Governor McAuliffe today announced that Frontier Secure, a division of Frontier Communications Corporation, will locate a customer care center in Wise County. The facility will provide customer and technical support for its clients. Virginia successfully competed against Kansas, Georgia, and South Carolina for the project, which will create up to 500 new jobs.
"Today is a great day for Southwest Virginia. Frontier Secure's decision to locate its customer care center in Wise County will have a tremendous economic impact on the region and the Commonwealth," said Governor McAuliffe. "This project is a testament to the strong workforce, business climate and strategic investment potential in this region, and in all corners of the Commonwealth. I congratulate Frontier Secure's leaders on their expansion and thank them for their partnership with the Commonwealth in our continuing work to grow, diversify and build a new Virginia economy."
