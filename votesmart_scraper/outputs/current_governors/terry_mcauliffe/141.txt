Governor McAuliffe Announces Regional Sessions to Improve Police-Community Relations
Press Release
By: Terry McAuliffe
Date: July 21, 2016
Location: Richmond, VA

Governor Terry McAuliffe and Secretary of Public Safety and Homeland Security Brian Moran announced today that as part of the McAuliffe administration's continued efforts to foster positive police and community relationships, the administration will host a series of listening sessions with law enforcement and community partners to solicit recommendations on ways to continue strengthening and sustaining their relationships.
