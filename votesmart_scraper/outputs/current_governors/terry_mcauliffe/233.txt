Governor McAuliffe Statement on the Passing of Johnny Joannou
Statement
By: Terry McAuliffe
Date: May 6, 2016
Location: Richmond, VA

Governor Terry McAuliffe released the following statement on the passing of former Virginia Delegate and Senator Johnny Joannou:
"I am saddened by the passing of former Senator and Delegate Johnny Joannou. Johnny will be remembered for his decades of devoted service to his community and his Commonwealth as a member of the General Assembly. My thoughts and prayers are with the family and friends who are grieving this loss today."
