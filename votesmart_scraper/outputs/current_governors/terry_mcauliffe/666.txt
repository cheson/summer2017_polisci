Governor McAuliffe to Co-Host 7th Annual Conference on Agricultural Trade March 9-10 in Richmond
Press Release
By: Terry McAuliffe
Date: Feb. 6, 2015
Location: Unknown

GovernorTerry McAuliffe will co-host the 7th Annual Governor's Conference on Agricultural Trade on March 9-10, 2015 at the Richmond Marriot Hotel. The two-day conference, entitled Facilitating New Opportunities in the Global Marketplace, is co-hosted by Virginia Tech's Department of Agricultural and Applied Economics, the Virginia Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services (VDACS), Virginia Farm Bureau Federation, and the Virginia Port Authority (VPA). The event will bring together producers, agribusinesses, entrepreneurs, exporters, foreign ambassadors, embassy ministers, land-grant university leadership, state and federal government officials, and others to discuss challenges and opportunities for global exports. A moderated panel discussion and private meetings regarding the international trade of agricultural goods will be held as well.
