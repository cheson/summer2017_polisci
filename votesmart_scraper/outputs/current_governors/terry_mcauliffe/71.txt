Governor McAuliffe Announces New Exports to Chile and Costa Rica for Virginia Manufacturer
Press Release
By: Terry McAuliffe
Date: Nov. 28, 2016
Location: Richmond, VA

Governor Terry McAuliffe today announced that Grandwatt Electric Corporation, a Suffolk-based manufacturer, has made sales to distributors in Chile and Costa Rica as a result of its participation in the Virginia Economic Development Partnership's (VEDP) international trade programs. After attending the VEDP-led ExpoMin Chile trade show in April of 2016, Grandwatt signed an agreement with DiPERK Power Solutions to distribute its generators in Chile. The initial $400,000 sale to DiPERK is for 40 generators, and the company is projecting sales of 150-200 units per year going forward.
