Governor McAuliffe Announces 800 New Jobs for Arlington County
Press Release
By: Terry McAuliffe
Date: July 21, 2014
Location: Richmond, VA

Governor Terry McAuliffe announced today that CEB, the leading member-based advisory company, will invest $149.7 million in a new headquarters operation to support the company's next-generation workplace plans and accommodate its growing global requirements. The company has signed an Agreement to lease approximately 350,000 square feet of space in The JBG Companies' new Central Place development in Rosslyn, Virginia. CEB will be the marquee tenant in the building to be named "CEB Tower." CEB plans to move to its new global headquarters in 2018. The project will create 800 new jobs.
