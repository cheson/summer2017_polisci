Public Statements
Issue Position: Veterans and Military Families
Issue Position
By: Terry McAuliffe
Date: Jan. 1, 2017
Location: Unknown

Workforce:
Create seamless transitions for Virginia's veterans with high quality education and workforce services that accelerate career opportunities.
Homeless:
Increase affordable housing opportunities and veterans support programs.
BRAC:
Retain and reinforce Virginia's military installations and defense activities.
Family Support:
Ensure that Virginia remains the preeminent state for military personnel, their families and veterans.

Source: https://governor.virginia.gov/policy-priorities/veterans-and-military-families/
