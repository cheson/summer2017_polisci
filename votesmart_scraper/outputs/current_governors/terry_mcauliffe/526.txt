Governor McAuliffe Announces New Craft Distillery in Newport News
Press Release
By: Terry McAuliffe
Date: July 1, 2015
Location: Unknown

Governor Terry McAuliffe announced today that Ironclad Distillery will open a new small-batch bourbon distillery in the City of Newport News. The company, the first craft distillery in revitalized downtown Newport News, will create eight new jobs in the city and source one-hundred percent of its corn, wheat, and rye needs from Virginia agricultural producers. The Commonwealth of Virginia is partnering with Newport News and Ironclad Distillery on this project through the Governor's Agriculture and Forestry Industries Development Fund (AFID).
