Rep. Paul Ryan Stumps for Asa Hutchinson
Press Release
By: Asa Hutchinson Paul Ryan
Date: Sept. 24, 2014
Location: Little Rock, AR

Republican gubernatorial nominee Asa Hutchinson welcomed the former Vice Presidential nominee, Congressman Paul Ryan, chairman of the House Budget Committee, Wednesday evening at a get out the vote rally in Saline County.
Congressman Ryan released the statement below following the news conference:
"Asa Hutchinson has what it takes to get Arkansas working again. His six-point plan will build a healthy economy for working families by lowering the high income tax rate, putting patients in charge of their health care, and helping students prepare for the 21st century economy. He's ready to take on the big challenges, and I'm proud to be in Arkansas to support Asa."
Asa Hutchinson issued the following statement:
"As chairman of the House Budget Committee, Congressman Paul Ryan has shown great leadership in his approach to solving our nation's debt and addressing the issue of poverty throughout the country. I am honored to have him here in Arkansas to help get out the vote.
"As governor, I will lead our state with fiscal responsibility, unafraid of saying no to wasteful spending and maintaining our balanced budget. I have a plan that will actually work for all Arkansans. Together we can create jobs, grow our economy and, with an emphasis in computer science and STEM programs in every state high school, we will lead in technology."
