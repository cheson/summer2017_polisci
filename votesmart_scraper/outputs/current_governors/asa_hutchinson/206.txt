Governor Asa Hutchinson Makes Official Call for Special Legislative Session
Press Release
By: Asa Hutchinson
Date: May 20, 2015
Location: Little Rock, AR

Governor Asa Hutchinson has made the official call to legislators for a special session of the 90th General Assembly that will convene Tuesday, May 26, 2015. Along with the official call, the Governor has announced all items on the special session agenda below.
Governor Hutchinson issued the following statement:
"This limited agenda focuses on job creation and economic development, while highlighting government efficiencies that will ultimately result in savings to all Arkansas taxpayers."
