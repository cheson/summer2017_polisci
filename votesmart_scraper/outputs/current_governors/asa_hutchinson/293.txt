Issue Position: Values
Issue Position
By: Asa Hutchinson
Date: Jan. 1, 2014
Location: Unknown

Pro-life - Asa is pro-life and has consistently received very high ratings from pro-life groups such as Arkansas Right to Life and National Right to Life. As governor, Asa would have signed both the 12 week and 20 week abortion bans passed by the Arkansas General Assembly.
Traditional Marriage - Asa believes that marriage is between one man and one woman.
Faith - Asa relies on his personal faith and strength through prayer as daily reminders that we are not in control of everything in life and that we are all responsible to a divine Creator.
