Side-by-Side Comparison: How House, Senate, and Governor's Budget Proposals Address Needs
By: Roy Cooper III
Date: June 1, 2017
Location: Raleigh, NC

Yesterday, Gov. Cooper shared the following statement on the House Republican budget:
"This House budget shortchanges our state at a time when we don't have to. It falls far short of what is needed on education and jobs, and I urge the House to do better for our families."
Click here for a side-by-side comparison of how budget proposals from Governor Cooper, the NC House, and the NC Senate address needs in education, job growth, good government, public safety, mental health, and the opioid crisis.
