Governor Cooper Issues Statement on Latest Order from Three-Judge Panel
Statement
By: Roy Cooper III
Date: June 9, 2017
Location: Raleigh, NC

Governor Cooper released the following comment on three-judge panel order that shows the court intends to move quickly on redistricting:
"I'm pleased the court intends to act swiftly. There is no reason for Republican legislators to drag their heels in correcting this wrong after the Supreme Court affirmed their map to be unconstitutional."
