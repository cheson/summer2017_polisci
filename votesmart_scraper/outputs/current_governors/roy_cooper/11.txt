Governor Cooper Issues Statement on President Trump's Decision to Withdraw from the Paris Climate Agreement
Statement
By: Roy Cooper III
Date: June 1, 2017
Location: Raleigh, NC

Governor Cooper released the following statement on President Trump's decision to withdraw from the Paris Climate Agreement:
"The United States should be a leader in fighting pollution. Pulling out of the Paris accord is wrong for our country, our children, and the generations to come."
