Gov. Ricketts Visits UNMC for Biocontainment Unit 10th Anniversary Events
Press Release
By: Pete Ricketts
Date: March 27, 2015
Location: Lincoln, NE

Today, Governor Pete Ricketts visited the University of Nebraska Medical Center (UNMC) Biocontainment Unit to recognize its 10th anniversary. The Governor participated in a brief press conference with Dr. Rick Sacra, the first Ebola patient treated in the unit, along with UNMC leadership before delivering remarks at a celebration event and touring the facility.
