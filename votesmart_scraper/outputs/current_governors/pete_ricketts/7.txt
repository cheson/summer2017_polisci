Gov. Ricketts Comments on May Tax Receipts
Statement
By: Pete Ricketts
Date: June 15, 2017
Location: Lincoln, NE

Today, Governor Pete Ricketts issued a statement following news that state general fund tax receipts fell $9 million short of projections during May.
"Today's revenue report is a reminder that uncertain revenues require continued fiscal constraint by state agencies. We will continue to monitor tax receipts closely, and manage our budget carefully to protect taxpayers."
