Gov. Ricketts' Statement on the Death of Tech. Sgt. Joseph Lemm
Statement
By: Pete Ricketts
Date: Dec. 23, 2015
Location: Lincoln, NE

Today, Governor Pete Ricketts issued the following statement honoring Technical Sergeant Joseph Lemm of Beemer who died in a suicide attack in Afghanistan on December 21, 2015.
"Susanne and I join Nebraskans in mourning the death of Technical Sergeant Lemm. We will long remember his bravery and sacrifice for the freedoms that we cherish."
