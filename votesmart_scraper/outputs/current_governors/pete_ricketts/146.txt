Flags to Fly at Half-Staff to Honor Victims of the Terrorist Attack in Brussels
Statement
By: Pete Ricketts
Date: March 23, 2016
Location: Lincoln, NE

oday, Governor Pete Ricketts, in accordance with a proclamation from President Barack Obama, announced that all U.S. and Nebraska flags are to be flown at half-staff in honor of the victims of the terrorist attack in Brussels, Belgium.
