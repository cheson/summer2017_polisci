Gov. Ricketts Promotes Nebraska Beef in New York City
Press Release
By: Pete Ricketts
Date: April 10, 2015
Location: Lincoln, NE

On Thursday and Friday this week, Governor Pete Ricketts visited New York City along with the Nebraska Department of Agriculture, Nebraska commodity group leaders, and Nebraska beef producers to promote Nebraska beef. As a part of this trip, the delegation visited four venues: Empire Steak House, Ottomanelli and Sons Meat Market, Burger & Lobster, and the Bull and Bear Prime Steakhouse. At these venues, the Governor and delegation members met with food journalists, nutritionists, and culinary experts to highlight Nebraska's quality beef products.
