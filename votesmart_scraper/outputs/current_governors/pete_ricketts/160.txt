Gov. Ricketts Comments on the Passing of Justice Scalia
Statement
By: Pete Ricketts
Date: Feb. 16, 2016
Location: Lincoln, NE

Today, Governor Pete Ricketts issued a statement on the passing of United States Supreme Court Justice Antonin Scalia.
"Justice Antonin Scalia was a true public servant. He was a staunch defender of the Constitution, and for that America will be forever grateful."
