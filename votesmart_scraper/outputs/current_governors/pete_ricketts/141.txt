Gov. Ricketts Praises the Legislature's Advancement of Property Tax Relief Measure
Statement
By: Pete Ricketts
Date: April 1, 2016
Location: Lincoln, NE

Today, Governor Pete Ricketts issued a statement on the advancement of LB958, which provides property tax relief. Property tax relief is the Governor's number one priority this legislative session.
"The Legislature's decision to advance LB958 is a good sign that we will be able to deliver much-needed property tax relief to hardworking Nebraskans this year," said Gov. Ricketts. "With two more rounds of debate left, I look forward to continuing to work together with senators to make tax relief a reality."
