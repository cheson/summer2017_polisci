Issue Position: Protecting Our Values
Issue Position
By: Pete Ricketts
Date: Jan. 1, 2006
Location: Unknown

Culture of Life: I am a pro-life conservative.
Marriage: I believe that marriage is between a man and a woman.
Guns: I strongly support the Second Amendment--our right to keep and bear arms.
Judges: I support conservative judicial nominees who understand it is their job to interpret the law, not create it.
