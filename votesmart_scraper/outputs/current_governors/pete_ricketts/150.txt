Gov. Ricketts' Statement on the Advancement of the Transportation Innovation Act
Press Release
By: Pete Ricketts
Date: March 16, 2016
Location: Lincoln, NE

Today, Governor Pete Ricketts issued a statement on the advancement of LB960, also known as the Transportation Innovation Act. LB960 is one of the Governor's major initiatives for the 2016 legislative session.
"I have enjoyed the opportunity to work with the Legislature as we have collaborated together on the Transportation Innovation Act, and my administration will continue to work with senators to prioritize these important infrastructure investments as the legislative process continues."
