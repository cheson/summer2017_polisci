Gov. Ricketts Applauds Revenue Committee's Advancement of Property Tax Relief Bill
Statement
By: Pete Ricketts
Date: March 25, 2016
Location: Lincoln, NE

Today, Governor Pete Ricketts issued a statement following the Revenue Committee's vote to advance LB958, which provides property tax relief. Property tax relief is the Governor's number one priority this legislative session.
"The Revenue Committee has invested significant time and thought in collaborating with my office to assemble a property tax relief package. Their advancement of LB958 for consideration by the full Legislature is the next key step towards bringing property tax relief to hardworking Nebraska families. I look forward to working with the entire Legislature on this important property tax relief proposal in the coming days."
