Governor Malloy on President Obama's Announcement Regarding Marriage Equality
Statement
By: Dan Malloy
Date: May 9, 2012
Location: Washington, DC

Governor Dannel P. Malloy today released the following statement following President Obama's announcement of support for marriage equality:

"I applaud President Obama's support for same-sex marriage. This is an historic day for civil rights.

"Equal rights should be granted to all. We in Connecticut agree."
