Gov. Malloy Statement on Tonight's Vigil in East Haven
Statement
By: Dan Malloy
Date: Aug. 10, 2013
Location: Hartford, CT

Governor Dannel P. Malloy tonight released the following statement on the vigil in honor of the victims of Friday's plane crash in East Haven.

"Tonight's vigil is a profound statement of the ties that bind East Haven and our entire state together as one community. When a family suffers an unimaginable tragedy, we come together and pray that they have the strength they need to carry on.

"Our thoughts and prayers are with all the families tonight who are suffering from grief and loss."
