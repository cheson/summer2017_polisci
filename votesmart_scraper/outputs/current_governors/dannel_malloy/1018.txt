Gov. Malloy Statement on Nevada Middle School Shooting
Statement
By: Dan Malloy
Date: Oct. 21, 2013
Location: Hartford, CT

Governor Dannel P. Malloy released the following statement in response to the middle school shooting earlier today in Sparks, Nevada:

"I spoke with Governor Sandoval this afternoon to express our deepest sympathies and offer him our state's support during the difficult healing process that is about to begin. Our thoughts and prayers are with everyone tonight dealing with this senseless tragedy."
