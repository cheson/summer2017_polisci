Gov. Malloy & Lt. Gov. Wyman Statement on Labor Day
Statement
By: Dan Malloy Nancy Wyman
Date: Sept. 1, 2014
Location: Hartford, CT

Governor Dannel P. Malloy and Lt. Governor Nancy Wyman today released the following statement in honor of Labor Day:

"Today we pause to honor the tremendous contribution that working men and women have made to the building of our state and our nation. What we are today, we are because of the dedication of so many who poured their heart and soul into their labor, doing so because they knew the end result would be stronger communities.

"We thank everyone for their contribution to our state and wish everyone a happy and safe Labor Day."
