Gov. Malloy Statement on Earthquake in Central Italy
By: Dan Malloy
Date: Aug. 24, 2016
Location: Hartford, CT

Governor Dannel P. Malloy released the following statement on the 6.2 magnitude earthquake that struck central Italy and has left dozens killed and missing:
"The devastation and loss of life in central Italy is just heartbreaking. Our thoughts and prayers are with those affected by this terrible tragedy and their loved ones. Today, we are truly mourning Italy's loss."
