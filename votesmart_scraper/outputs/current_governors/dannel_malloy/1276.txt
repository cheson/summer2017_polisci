Gov. Malloy Statement on New Connecticut Whale Management
Press Release
By: Dan Malloy
Date: July 9, 2012
Location: Hartford, CT

Governor Dannel P. Malloy released the following statement regarding AEG Management CT, LLC's announcement this afternoon that the company will manage all business operations, including marketing, sponsorship, ticketing and group sales for the American Hockey League's Connecticut Whale hockey team.

"This is good news for the City of Hartford and for hockey fans across the region. I hope this new relationship with AEG will be productive, one that will ensure the Whale stay in our capitol city for a long time to come."
