Gov. Malloy: Connecticut Has Taken a Major Step Toward Meaningful Teacher Evaluations
Statement
By: Dan Malloy
Date: Jan. 25, 2012
Location: Hartford, CT

Governor Dannel P. Malloy released the following statement:

"I extend my congratulations and my thanks to the PEAC Committee and the State Department of Education on their progress today. Connecticut has taken a major step toward a meaningful teacher evaluation system," Governor Malloy said. "Today's consensus proposal has real potential to increase teacher effectiveness -- and as a result, to elevate student achievement. This is a milestone in what I expect will continue to be a momentous year for education reform in Connecticut."
