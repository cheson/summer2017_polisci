Gov. Malloy: French Flag Flying on State Capitol Dome and at Governor's Residence
Statement
By: Dan Malloy
Date: Nov. 14, 2015
Location: Hartford, CT

Governor Dannel P. Malloy today announced that the flag of France is flying on the dome of the State Capitol building and outside of the Governor's Residence in Hartford in solidarity with the people of France.
"It was an extraordinary tragedy and a truly dark day across the globe. The people of Connecticut stand in solidarity with France. Our thoughts and prayers are with the families who suffered through yesterday's attacks." Governor Malloy said. "We must stand up against terrorism and extremism across the world."
