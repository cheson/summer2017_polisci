Governor Malloy on Black History Month
Statement
By: Dan Malloy
Date: Feb. 1, 2011
Location: Hartford, CT

Governor Dannel P. Malloy today released the following statement commemorating February as Black History Month.

"Black History Month is an opportunity for everyone -- young and old, and of all races -- to pay tribute to and learn more about the long-lasting, significant contributions that African Americans have made to our country and our state," Governor Malloy said. "While the celebration of our nation's heritage should be a year-round commitment, it is important that we observe this month to recognize and raise awareness of the many accomplishments of Connecticut's African American citizens."
