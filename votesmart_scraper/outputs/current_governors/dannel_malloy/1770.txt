MALLOY CALLS FOR CAUTION ON ALITO HEARINGS
Democratic candidate for Governor Dan Malloy, Stamford's Mayor, today issued a letter to Connecticut Senators Dodd and Lieberman expressing his concern over the nomination of Judge Samuel Alito to the United States Supreme Court. Commending them both on their demonstrated leadership, Malloy urged each Senator to conduct a thorough examination of the nominee's record of public service, his judicial philosophy, as well as his personal beliefs. A complete transcript of the letter can be found below.
