Issue Position: Life and Marriage
Issue Position
By: Matt Bevin
Date: Jan. 1, 2014
Location: Unknown

Matt is pro-life and believes we have a duty to protect unborn human life. From 2008-2011, Matt served on the board of A Woman's Choice Resource Center, a pro-life counseling center in Louisville, KY.
Matt is the proud father of nine children, four of whom he and his wife adopted from Ethiopia. He has also been a long-time, significant supporter of orphanages around the world, investing both his dollars and his time.
In the U.S. Senate, Matt will vote against any bill that contains federal funding or other material support for abortions. He also believes we must encourage a culture of life that makes adoption easier.
Matt supports marriage as traditionally defined between a man and a woman.
