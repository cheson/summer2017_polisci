Raimondo Directs U.S. and Rhode Island Flags Lowered
Statement
By: Gina Raimondo
Date: Nov. 16, 2015
Location: Providence, RI

In accordance with a Presidential Proclamation, Governor Gina M. Raimondo has ordered the U.S. and Rhode Island flags to be flown at half-staff at all state facilities and buildings. The flags will remain at half-staff until sunset, November 19, 2015 in honor of the victims in the Paris, France attacks.
"On behalf of all Rhode Islanders, I send my deepest condolences to those impacted by Friday's tragic violence in Paris," said Raimondo. "Our thoughts and prayers are with the French people. We stand together."
