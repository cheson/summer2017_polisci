Raimondo Directs U.S. and Rhode Island Flags Lowered
Statement
By: Gina Raimondo
Date: March 7, 2016
Location: Providence, RI

In accordance with a Presidential Proclamation, Governor Gina M. Raimondo has ordered the U.S. and Rhode Island flags to be flown at half-staff at all state facilities and buildings in memory of former First Lady Nancy Reagan, who passed away this weekend.
"On behalf of all Rhode Islanders, I send my deepest condolences to the Reagan family," Raimondo said. "Nancy Reagan was a tireless advocate for our country, and she will be missed."
Raimondo also asks Rhode Islanders to lower their flags as a mark of respect.
