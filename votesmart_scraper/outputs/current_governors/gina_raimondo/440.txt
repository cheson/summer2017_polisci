Governor Raimondo Hosts First Cabinet Meeting
Press Release
By: Gina Raimondo
Date: Jan. 13, 2015
Location: Providence, RI

Governor Gina M. Raimondo today met with her Cabinet for the first time to lay out the vision of her administration - to create opportunities for all families. Following the meeting, the Governor's office hosted an informal meet and greet for the cabinet with members of the General Assembly.
"My team will be focused on working together to create opportunities for Rhode Islanders by building skills, attracting entrepreneurs, and fostering innovation," Raimondo said. "By working together across state government, we will ignite Rhode Island's comeback."
