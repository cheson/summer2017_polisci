Rhode Island State Police Concludes Its Investigation of a Fatal Motor Vehicle Crash on Route 295 in the Town of Johnston
Press Release
By: Gina Raimondo
Date: June 15, 2016
Location: Unknown

On Friday, June 10, 2016 at approximately 5:00 a.m., Troopers assigned to the Scituate Barracks responded to a reported motor vehicle crash on Route 295 South in the Town of Johnston.
The investigation revealed a GMC SUV was traveling on Route 295 south in the Town of Johnston. At a point south of Greenville Avenue, the GMC struck a deer, subsequently lost control, and rolled-over several times landing on its passenger side. The operator of the GMC, who was not wearing a seatbelt, was ejected and sustained fatal injuries as a result of the crash. The operator is identified as Shawn Riccio, age 36, of 192 Marshall Road, Woonsocket, RI.
