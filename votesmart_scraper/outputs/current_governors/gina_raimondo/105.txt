Hospital Conversion Application of Yale-New Haven Health Services Corporation and Lawrence + Memorial Corporation (Westerly Hospital) Deemed Complete
Press Release
By: Gina Raimondo
Date: June 10, 2016
Location: Unknown

The Rhode Island Department of Attorney General (RIAG) today announced that the Expedited Review Hospital Conversion Initial Application submitted by Yale-New Haven Health Services Corporation, Lawrence + Memorial Corporation, LMW Healthcare, Inc., d/b/a Westerly Hospital, has been deemed complete and accepted for review.
RIAG will now start the formal review and evaluation of the application, and have 90 days to complete this step of the process under the expedited review section of the Hospital Conversions Act.
