Raimondo Marks Peace Officers Memorial Day by Ordering Flags Lowered
Press Release
By: Gina Raimondo
Date: May 13, 2016
Location: Providence, RI

Governor Gina M. Raimondo is directing U.S. and Rhode Island flags at all state agencies and buildings to be flown at half-staff from sunrise to sunset on Sunday, May 15, in honor of Peace Officers Memorial Day. This day recognizes all law enforcement officers who have died in the line of duty.
Raimondo also asks Rhode Islanders to lower their flags to half-staff.
