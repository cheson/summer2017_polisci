Statement by Secretary of State Gorbea on Appointments to the State Board of Elections
Press Release
By: Gina Raimondo
Date: May 3, 2016
Location: Providence, RI

"I am pleased with Governor Raimondo's appointment of Diane Mederos and Isadore Ramos to the State Board of Elections. I have already reached out to Ms. Mederos and Mr. Ramos to congratulate them and offer the assistance of the Department of State in whatever they need. Upon confirmation by the Senate, I look forward to working with these new board members, and the entire Board of Elections, to ensure that elections in Rhode Island are fair, fast, and accurate."
