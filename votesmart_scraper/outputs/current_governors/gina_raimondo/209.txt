Providence County Grand Jury Finding in the March 24th Officer Involved Shooting that Resulted in the Death of Dominique Silva
Press Release
By: Gina Raimondo
Date: May 10, 2016
Location: Unknown

On May 9, 2016, the Providence County Grand Jury completed its investigation into the circumstances surrounding the March 24, 2016 shooting of Dominique Silva by Officer Julianne Borsari of the Pawtucket Police Department. The Grand Jury determined the actions by the Officer were lawful and legally justified.
