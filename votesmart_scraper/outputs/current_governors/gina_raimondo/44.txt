Raimondo's Statement on Presidential Election
Statement
By: Gina Raimondo
Date: Nov. 9, 2016
Location: Providence, RI

Governor Gina M. Raimondo released the following statement today on the results of the Presidential Election.
"The long campaign is over and now it is time to begin the necessary work to unite our nation. My top priority is positioning Rhode Island for success. The people of Rhode Island expect and deserve that we continue working together to create jobs, grow our economy and move our state and our nation forward."
