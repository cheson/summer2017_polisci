Governor Holds Legislative Press Conference
Statement
By: Dennis Daugaard
Date: Feb. 11, 2016
Location: Pierre, SD

Gov. Dennis Daugaard today held his fourth legislative press conference at the Capitol.

In the press briefing, the Governor gave an update on where his Medicaid expansion proposal stands with the federal government and commented on updated revenue projections. The Governor also spoke about the status of his education proposal.
