Gov. Daugaard Orders State Capitol Flags At Half-Staff For Former State Rep. Lowdon Heller
Press Release
By: Dennis Daugaard
Date: May 31, 2016
Location: Pierre, SD

Former state representative Lowdon Heller of Ideal passed away on May 28, 2016. Heller, 92, served in the South Dakota House of Representatives from 1957-61.
Gov. Dennis Daugaard has ordered flags to fly half-staff at the State Capitol on Wednesday, June 1, the day of Heller's funeral.
