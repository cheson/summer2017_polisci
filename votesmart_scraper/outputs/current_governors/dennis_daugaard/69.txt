Gov. Dennis Daugaard's Public Appearances; Public Meetings:

Thursday, June 2 -- Friday, June 3, Pierre -- The South Dakota Lottery Commission will meet at 9 a.m. June 2 in Room 412 of the State Capitol, 500 E. Capitol Ave., for its regular commission meeting. On the agenda is a public hearing on proposed rules for the EZ Match lotto game option, discussion on Lottery requests for proposals, and video lottery, sales and marketing reports. The meeting will recess after the morning session and move to the Highlands Meeting Room of the RedRossa Italian Grille, 808 W. Sioux Ave., in Pierre for a strategic planning meeting. The commission will reconvene at 1:30 p.m. The Friday, June 3 session will begin at 8:30 a.m. View meeting materials at http://lottery.sd.gov/about/commission/ or http://boardsandcommissions.sd.gov/. For more information, contact the South Dakota Lottery at 605-773-5770.

Friday, June 3, 1-3 p.m. CDT, Sioux Falls -- The South Dakota Rehabilitation Center for the Blind (SDRC) invites any South Dakota resident who has a visual impairment or can't see to use technology, to participate in the First Friday Computer and Assistive Technology Training at the SDRC facility located at 2900 W. 11th St. This month's training will focus on the iPhone. There is no cost to attend the class. Registration is required due to limited class size. Register by Wednesday, June 1. Contact JoMarie Laughlin at 1-800-658-5441; 605-367-5260 or JoMarie.Laughlin@state.sd.us.
A walk-in tech support clinic will be held the same day from 8-11 a.m. at SDRC. Experienced staff will be on hand to answer questions.
