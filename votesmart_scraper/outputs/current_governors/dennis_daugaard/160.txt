Governor's Statement On Dave Ruhl
Press Release
By: Dennis Daugaard
Date: Aug. 2, 2015
Location: Pierre, SD

Gov. Dennis Daugaard issued the following statement in honor of U.S. Forest Service Firefighter Dave Ruhl who lost his life last week on an assignment in California:

"This is a tragic loss. We are grateful for Dave Ruhl's courage and for the many firefighters who put themselves at risk to keep us safe."

The Governor will request flags at half-mast on the day of Ruhl's funeral once the date has been set.
