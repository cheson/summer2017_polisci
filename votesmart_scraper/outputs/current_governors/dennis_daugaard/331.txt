Issue Position: Cutting the Red Tape
Issue Position
By: Dennis Daugaard
Date: Jan. 1, 2014
Location: Unknown

The natural tendency of government is to grow, and Dennis has taken proactive steps to reverse that trend. Each year, he introduces bills to repeal unnecessary rules and statutes, so that South Dakota maintains a reasonable regulatory burden.
