Monday, May 2, to Thursday, May 5, Pierre, ClubHouse Hotel and Suites, 808 W. Sioux Ave. -- DENR staff will be among speakers at a Manager of Landfill Operations (MOLO) certification course. The four-day course is being hosted by the South Dakota Solid Waste Management Association (SDSWMA). It is designed for those responsible for day-to-day management of landfill operations, landfill inspectors, solid waste facility foremen, solid waste directors, public works directors, lead equipment operators, landfill designers, landfill permit writers, grant writers and individuals seeking a better understanding of solid waste facility operations. Attendees will learn about issues to be considered when planning, operating and closing a landfill; basic landfill siting restrictions and how to comply with design requirements; compliance inspections and basic regulatory standards; characteristics of solid waste; leachate and landfill gas management; and more. For a complete agenda, visit http://www.sdswma.org/2015/12/26/swana-manager-of-landfill-operations-course-may-2016/. For more information, contact Carolyn Trautman, SDSWMA executive director, at 605-216-3256.
