Public Statements
Governor's Statement on HB1234
Statement
By: Dennis Daugaard
Date: March 7, 2012
Location: Pierre, SD

A statement from Gov. Dennis Daugaard on a decision by the South Dakota Education Association to attempt referral of HB1234, the Governor's education reform proposal:


"This bill will invest $15 million a year in teacher pay, with lots of local control as to how the dollars are spent to advance student achievement."
