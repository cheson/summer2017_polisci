Gov. Daugaard's Workforce Summit Report Announced
Statement
By: Dennis Daugaard
Date: Aug. 6, 2014
Location: Pierre, SD

Gov. Dennis Daugaard will host six meetings across South Dakota to share the final report, findings and results of the recent regional Workforce Summits. Over 1,000 people provided input about workforce needs.

"The business, education, community and government sectors each have a role in developing a unified workforce agenda moving forward," said Gov. Daugaard. "Our efforts need to be persistent and ongoing. The next step is to identify and implement the strategies that will best help create an effective workforce system."
