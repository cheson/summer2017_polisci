Public Statements
8th Circuit Ruling-Governor Response
Statement
By: Dennis Daugaard
Date: July 24, 2012
Location: Pierre, SD

*A statement from Gov. Dennis Daugaard on today's 8th U.S. Circuit of Appeals decision that a 2005 state law regulating abortions is constitutional:

"This is an important ruling that upholds South Dakota's requirement that mothers considering an abortion receive complete information. I commend the Attorney General and those he worked with for obtaining this favorable result."
