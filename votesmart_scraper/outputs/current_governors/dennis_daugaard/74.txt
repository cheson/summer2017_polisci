President Proclaims Peace Officers Memorial Day - Gov. Daugaard Requests Flags At Half-Staff On May 15
By: Dennis Daugaard
Date: May 14, 2016
Location: Pierre, SD

President Barack Obama has proclaimed Sunday, May 15, as Peace Officers Memorial Day.
Gov. Dennis Daugaard is asking that all flags in the state be flown at half-staff from 8 a.m. until sunset Sunday in honor of peace officers' contributions and sacrifices.
