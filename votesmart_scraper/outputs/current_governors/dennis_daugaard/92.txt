Governor's Statement On Charges Filed In SDRC Investigation
Statement
By: Dennis Daugaard
Date: March 31, 2016
Location: Pierre, SD

Gov. Dennis Daugaard issued the following statement today in response to the charges filed against Joop Bollen:
"The wheels of justice turn slowly, but the state continues to pursue this matter in civil and criminal court. I appreciate the Attorney General's efforts in this investigation."
