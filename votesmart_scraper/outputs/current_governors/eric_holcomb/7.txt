Public Statements
Governor Holcomb Statement on Pre-K Expansion
By: Eric Holcomb
Date: April 21, 2017
Location: Unknown

Indiana Governor Eric J. Holcomb offered the following statement on passage of HEA 1004
I'm thrilled our lawmakers are providing thousands of low-income Hoosier families access to high quality early education. This important legislation gives more children in more counties the chance to start their educational journey on the right foot. It will be a joy to sign this bill into law.
