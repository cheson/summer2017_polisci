Governor Holcomb Provides Mid-Session Status Update on Next Level Agenda
Press Release
By: Eric Holcomb
Date: March 3, 2017
Location: South Bend, IN

At a South Bend Chamber of Commerce Luncheon today, Governor Eric J. Holcomb provided a progress report on his Next Level Legislative Agenda at the halfway point of the 2017 legislative session.
"I am encouraged by the collaboration and shared focus we've seen so far in this legislative session," Gov. Holcomb said. "We have made significant progress on the Next Level agenda, and I look forward to working with the Indiana General Assembly to move forward these initiatives."
Next Level Agenda:
Make Indiana a jobs magnet.
Fund a long-term plan for roads and bridges.
Prepare a skilled and ready 21st-century workforce.
Attack the state's drug epidemic.
Deliver great government service.
