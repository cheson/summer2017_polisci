Montana.gov Reboots With New Look
Press Release
By: Steve Bullock
Date: Aug. 5, 2013
Location: Helena, MT

Governor Steve Bullock is declaring the new Montana.gov "open for business."
With a goal of providing Montanans efficient access to online services the new Montana.gov is the official website for the State of Montana.
The website provides access to online services ranging from driver license renewals to job listings.
"The new Montana.gov is open for business and it's built by Montanans to serve the everyday needs of Montanans," said Governor Bullock. "The new website puts all state services just a few clicks away and makes sure the State of Montana is going to work for citizens."
State agencies each host an individual online presence inside the new Montana.gov. Many agencies will transition to the new look in coming months to provide Montanans a uniform look and feel when using online services from the State of Montana.
The website is built and administered by the State Information Technology Services Division based in Helena.
