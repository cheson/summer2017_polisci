Public Statements
Statement on Veteran's Day 2014
Statement
By: Steve Bullock
Date: Nov. 11, 2014
Location: Helena, MT

Governor Steve Bullock released the following statement regarding the celebration of
Veteran's Day in Montana:
"Today must be more than just about remembering and honoring the service and sacrifice of those who wear the uniform. It must also be about keeping the promises we made to ensure our veterans have access to care for their physical or mental wounds, to find a job that allows them to continue to provide for themselves and their families, and have access to education that will help them further their careers.
