Bullock on World Teachers' Day: "Montana Teachers Do Amazing Work That Is Changing the Future of Our State for the Better"
Statement
By: Steve Bullock
Date: Oct. 5, 2015
Location: Helena, MT

Governor Steve Bullock today released the following statement on the occasion of World Teachers' Day:
"As a product of Montana's public schools and a parent of three school-aged kids, I'm eternally grateful for the teachers who instill a life-long love of learning in each of their students.
Montana teachers do amazing work that is changing the future of our state for the better. Every day they're equipping our state's next generation of engineers, nurses, and innovators with the skills to succeed, and inspiring them to meet their full potential."
