Governor Bullock and Lt. Governor Cooney Recognize Montana Schools Leading the Way to Improve Energy Efficiency and Save Taxpayer Dollars
Statement
By: Steve Bullock
Date: April 19, 2016
Location: Helena, MT

Governor Steve Bullock today recognized 13 Montana public schools as the winners of the 2015-16 SMART (Saving Money and Resources Today) Schools Challenges. Through these challenges, schools found ways to improve energy efficiency, promote healthy and environmentally sound practices, and reduce waste by implementing or expanding recycling and composting programs. Each school will receive a $1,000 award to support their ongoing efforts.
