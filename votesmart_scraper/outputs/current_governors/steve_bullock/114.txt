Governor Bullock to Order Flags Flown at Half-Staff in Memory of Senator Conrad Burns on Day of His Interment
Statement
By: Steve Bullock
Date: May 2, 2016
Location: Helena, MT

Governor Steve Bullock will order all flags flown in the State of Montana at half-staff on Friday, May 6th, 2016 for twenty-four hours to honor the memory of United States Senator Conrad Burns.
"I join all Montanans in paying respect and remembering the life of Senator Conrad Burns, who passed away on April 28th, 2016. Senator Burns was a dedicated father, husband, Marine, and public servant. He was a fierce advocate for all of Montana," said Governor Bullock. "His legacy and his work are enshrined in the farms and ranches, cities and towns, and the people and places throughout this great state."
