Governor Christie: The Greatest Sports Spectacle In The World
Statement
By: Chris Christie
Date: Feb. 1, 2014
Location: Unknown

I want to tell everybody out there to thank you very much to all of our partners: the NFL, the team owners. We look forward to handing it over to Arizona. I was proud to be in New Orleans last year to accept the handoff on behalf of the region and we look forward to hosting everybody in this group that's got a ticket over in New Jersey tomorrow to watch the greatest sports spectacle in the world. Congratulations to the NFL and congratulations to the Host Committee. Thank you all very much.
