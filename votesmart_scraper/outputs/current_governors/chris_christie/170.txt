Statement from Governor Brian Sandoval Following the Victory of President-elect Donald J. Trump
Statement
By: Chris Christie
Date: Nov. 9, 2016
Location: Carson City, NV

Governor Brian Sandoval released the following statement after President-elect Donald J. Trump's victory tonight.
"Following a long and spirited election, we must now unite, celebrate the strength of our democracy, and support our new President-elect, Donald J. Trump."
