Governor Christie: A Charter School Education Unleashes Every Child's Potential
By: Chris Christie
Date: June 6, 2016
Location: Unknown

Governor Chris Christie today held a State House meeting with 40 charter school parents from around the state, listening to personal stories about their children's successful educational experiences. The Governor expressed his commitment to expand charter schools and improve New Jersey's public education system to ensure all students have an equal opportunity to reach their full potential.
