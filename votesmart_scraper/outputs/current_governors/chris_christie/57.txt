Statement from Governor Chris Christie on 66th National Day of Prayer
Statement
By: Chris Christie
Date: May 4, 2017
Location: Trenton, NJ

Today, Governor Chris Christie issued the following statement on the 66th Annual National Day of Prayer:
"Prayer is a solemn act, one characterized by humility. May we, as a state and a country take time to pray, not just today, on our National Day of Prayer, but every day. As we seek comfort from a higher power, let us pray with thankfulness for the blessings of liberty we have been entrusted, request mercy despite judgement, peace during conflict, civility in strife, and healing through unity."
Governor Christie also proclaimed May 4, 2017 as "A Day of Prayer" throughout New Jersey.
