Statement from Chris Christie on Moammar Gadhafi's Potential Visit to New Jersey
Statement
By: Chris Christie
Date: Aug. 26, 2009
Location: Parsippany, NJ

Today, Chris Christie released the following statement regarding the potential visit of Moammar Gadhafi to New Jersey:
"Whether it was Hemant Lakhani or the Fort Dix Six, we fought terrorism at the U.S. Attorney's Office for the last seven years. Gadhafi would not set foot in New Jersey in a Christie Administration."
