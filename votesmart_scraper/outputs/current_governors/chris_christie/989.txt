Statement From Governor Chris Christie On Reaching An Agreement With The CWA
Statement
By: Chris Christie
Date: May 4, 2012
Location: Trenton, NJ

Governor Chris Christie released today the following statement on reaching a contract agreement with the Communications Workers of America (CWA):
"I'm pleased to announce that we have reached a tentative agreement with the CWA and its 35,000 employees for a collectively bargained four-year contract. Specifics of the agreement will be released next week by the Administration and the union leadership."
