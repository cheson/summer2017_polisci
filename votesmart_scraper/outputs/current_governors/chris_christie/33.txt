Statement From Governor Chris Christie In Honor Of Memorial Day
Statement
By: Chris Christie
Date: May 29, 2017
Location: Trenton, NJ

In honor of Memorial Day, Governor Chris Christie released the following statement:
"This Memorial Day, we remember the courageous men and women of our military, who made the ultimate sacrifice to defend and protect our freedoms and way of life. Let's fly the stars and stripes today, and every day, in their honor, adorning their memorials with flowers and offering prayers in their names. Their bravery, heroism and unwavering patriotism allow us to humbly reflect and celebrate our liberty on this day. I encourage all New Jerseyans to join me in thanking all military members and their families, for their love of our country and willingness to selflessly serve in protection of all we hold dear."
