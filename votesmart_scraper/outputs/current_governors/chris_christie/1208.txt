Governor Chris Christie Marks Beginning of Black History Month
Statement
By: Chris Christie
Date: Feb. 1, 2011
Location: Trenton, NJ

Today, Governor Christie issued the following statement:

"As we celebrate the beginning of Black History Month, I encourage New Jerseyans -- young and old - to reflect on the contributions African Americans have made to our country and our great State. From the sciences, academia and the arts, to public service, law enforcement and business, African Americans have played vital and important roles in shaping our institutions and our identity. This month, we are proud to celebrate and honor those contributions to our state and nation alike."
