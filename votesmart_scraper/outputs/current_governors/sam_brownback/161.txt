Governor Sam Brownback Issues Statement Addressing KPERS Funding
Statement
By: Sam Brownback
Date: July 18, 2014
Location: Unknown

Governor Sam Brownback today issued the following statement regarding funding for the Kansas Public Employees Retirement System.
"Since taking office, I have made restoring the health of our KPERS system a priority. We are now beginning to see the results of our hard work directly benefiting teachers through a pension system they can rely on."
