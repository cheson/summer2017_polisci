Governor Releases Statement on S Sub for HB 2117
Statement
By: Sam Brownback
Date: March 21, 2012
Location: Topeka, KS

Kansas Governor Sam Brownback released the following statement regarding the Senate's approval of Senate Substitute for HB 2117:
"The Senate took an important step towards growing the Kansas economy and creating new jobs. We are one step closer to hitting the accelerator on the emerging Kansas economy. We must remain focused on the just cause of pro-growth tax reform -- jobs and a brighter future for our children."
