Public Statements
Joint Statement by Governor Sam Brownback and Lt. Governor Jeff Colyer, M.D.
Statement
By: Sam Brownback Jeff Colyer
Date: June 17, 2015
Location: Unknown

Kansas Governor Sam Brownback and Lt. Governor Jeff Colyer today issued the following statement:
We have been advised by legal counsel that the United States Attorney's office has concluded an investigation regarding campaign finance matters, and no charges are to be filed.
As we have stated many times, our campaign finances were conducted in full compliance with applicable law and ethics regulations. We will have no further statements regarding this concluded matter.
