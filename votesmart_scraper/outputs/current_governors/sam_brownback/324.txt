Governor Brownback Supports KPERS Conference Agreement
Statement
By: Sam Brownback
Date: May 15, 2012
Location: Topeka, KS

Kansas Governor Sam Brownback issued the following statement today regarding the agreement reached by members of the KPERS Conference Committee.
"I want to applaud the KPERS Conference Committee for reaching an agreement on how to move our state's employee retirement system forward and to ensure state employees have a pension system that is stable."
