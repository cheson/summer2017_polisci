Governor Sam Brownback Releases Statement Prior to Kansas Supreme Court Ruling
Statement
By: Sam Brownback
Date: March 6, 2014
Location: Unknown

Today Kansas Governor Sam Brownback released the following statement about the Kansas Supreme Court ruling on the Gannon school finance lawsuit.
"I have repeatedly stated and believe funding schools is the most important thing state government does. The centerpiece of my agenda for the 2014 legislative session is to increase Kansas's investment in all-day Kindergarten, which is long overdue and a true path forward."
