Governor Sam Brownback Responds to Kansas Supreme Court Ruling on Gannon V Kansas
Statement
By: Sam Brownback
Date: Feb. 11, 2016
Location: Unknown

Governor Sam Brownback today issued a statement on education funding following the recent Kansas Supreme Court ruling on Gannon v State of Kansas:
"Kansas has among the best schools in the nation and an activist Kansas Supreme court is threatening to shut them down.
"We will review this decision closely and work with the Legislature to ensure the continued success of our great Kansas schools."
