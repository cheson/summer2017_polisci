Brownback Commends President on Endorsement of Federal Marriage Amendment
By: Sam Brownback
Date: Feb. 24, 2004
Location: Washington, DC

WASHINGTON - U.S. Senator Sam Brownback today applauded President Bush's endorsement of a constitutional amendment protecting the sanctity of marriage.
"I commend President Bush for his bold and principled stand in support of a constitutional amendment protecting marriage," Brownback said. "I look forward to working with him closely to enact this important legislation. Marriage is a sacred institution between a man and a woman. Protecting marriage is essential to the long term health of our families and culture."
