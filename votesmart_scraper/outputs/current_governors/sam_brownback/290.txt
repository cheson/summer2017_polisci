Gov. Brownback: Kansans Want to Grow Economy, Not Government
Statement
By: Sam Brownback
Date: Aug. 8, 2012
Location: Topeka, KS

Kansas Governor Sam Brownback released the following statement Wednesday regarding the 2012 Primary Elections:
"I want to thank the thousands of Kansans who voted in yesterday's primary and the election workers across Kansas for their hard work. The people of Kansas have a clear choice: Do we want to grow the government or grow the economy? Most Kansans want to grow the economy and increase the number of private sector jobs in our state. We will continue working every day to help Kansans fulfill their aspirations."

Source: http://governor.ks.gov/media-room/media-releases/2012/08/08/gov.-brownback-kansans-want-to-grow-economy-not-government
