Gov. Walker Releases Statement on Hanukkah
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Dec. 20, 2011
Location: Madison, WI

Governor Scott Walker released the following statement on Hanukkah following the Menorah lighting ceremony at the residence.
"As friends and families gather tonight to light the Menorah and begin Hanukkah we wish them a very happy and blessed holiday. Hanukkah reminds all of us of the power of faith, hope and freedom. Tonette and I hope everyone celebrating the holiday have a wonderful Hanukkah season."
