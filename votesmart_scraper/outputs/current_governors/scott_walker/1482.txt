Americans for Prosperity: No Climate Tax
Pledge
By: Scott Walker
Date: July 26, 2008
Location: Unknown

I, Scott Walker, pledge to the taxpayers of the state of Wisconsin that I will oppose any legislation relating to climate change that includes a net increase in state/local government revenue.

Source: http://www.noclimatetax.com/wp-content/uploads/2008/07/scott-walker.pdf
