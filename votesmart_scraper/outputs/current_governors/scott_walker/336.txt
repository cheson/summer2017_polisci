Governor Scott Walker Speaks with Students at Valley Christian School in Oshkosh
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: Jan. 8, 2016
Location: Oshkoch, WI

Governor Scott Walker spoke with students at Valley Christian School today in Oshkosh about his experiences as Governor and his vision for the state of Wisconsin. The fourth through 12th grade students asked Governor Walker questions following his remarks.
"It is important our children grow up in a Wisconsin that is better than that of generations before them," Governor Walker said. "Students, like those here at Valley Christian School, are our future and we will continue to enact common-sense reforms so they can have the best life possible and possess the skills and knowledge needed to help them live their piece of the American Dream."
