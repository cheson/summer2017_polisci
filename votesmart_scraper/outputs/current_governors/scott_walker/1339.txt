Public Statements
Governor Walker Statement on Legislative Action
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Oct. 25, 2011
Location: Madison, WI

Today Governor Walker released the following statement:
Today the Legislature is going to continue to advance a number of bipartisan proposals, transportation related bills, and legislation I called for in the Back to Work Wisconsin special session.
I remain focused on highlighting bills that both Democrats and Republicans can work together on and legislation aimed at helping improve Wisconsin's economy.
