Governor Scott Walker Honors Fallen Officers at Greater Milwaukee Law Enforcement Memorial
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: May 6, 2015
Location: Milwaukee, WI

Governor Scott Walker delivered remarks today at the Greater Milwaukee Law Enforcement Memorial Service, which commemorates fallen law enforcement officers and honors their loved ones.
"This month, law enforcement memorial ceremonies will be held throughout the state," Governor Walker said. "These ceremonies recognize those who paid the ultimate sacrifice for our safety and well-being. The courageous men and women who commit to protecting Wisconsin communities by serving in law enforcement deserve our utmost gratitude and respect. Today, we honor the memories of those who have fallen and keep them and their loved ones in our thoughts and prayers."
