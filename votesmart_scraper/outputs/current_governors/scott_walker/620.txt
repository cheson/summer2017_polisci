Governor Scott Walker Proclaims, "Hire a Veteran Week"
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Nov. 10, 2014
Location: Madison, WI

Governor Scott Walker today issued a Proclamation in recognition of "Hire a Veteran Week."
"The brave men and women who have served our country have proven skills and unique training, making them a great asset for employers," Governor Walker said. "We need to make sure they have the opportunities they deserve for employment in the public and private sector."
"Hire a Veteran Week" runs November 9-15. Veterans Day is Tuesday, November 11.
