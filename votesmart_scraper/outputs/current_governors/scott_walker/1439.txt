Governor Walker Releases Jobs Hotline
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Jan. 28, 2011
Location: Madison, WI

Governor Scott Walker today released a Jobs Hotline giving job creators a direct line into the governor's office. Businesses that call (855) 854-JOBS looking to create or retain jobs in Wisconsin will receive a call back from the administration within 24-hours.
"Job creation is the top priority for my administration," said Governor Scott Walker. "My office stands ready and willing to help any business that wants to grow in our state. The Jobs Hotline lets businesses know that we are here to assist them so Wisconsinites can get to work."
