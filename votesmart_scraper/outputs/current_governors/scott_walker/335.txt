Wisconsin Governor Scott Walker Makes Friendly Wager with Maryland Governor Larry Hogan and Virginia Governor Terry McAuliffe on NFC Wild Card Game
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: Jan. 9, 2016
Location: Madison, WI

Governor Scott Walker made friendly wagers on Friday night with Maryland Governor Larry Hogan and Virginia Governor Terry McAuliffe in support of his beloved Green Bay Packers as they take on the Washington Redskins at FedEx Field in Landover, Maryland.
"Wisconsin bleeds green and gold, and our whole state is cheering on the Packers as they head to the playoffs this weekend," said Governor Walker. "On behalf of Packer Nation, Go Pack Go!"
