Governor Scott Walker Visits Christ King School for Catholic Schools Week
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: Jan. 30, 2014
Location: Wauwatosa, WI

Governor Scott Walker visited Christ King School today during Catholic Schools Week; the theme for this year's Catholic Schools Week is "Catholic Schools: Communities of Faith, Knowledge, and Service."
"Every student deserves access to a great education, and Catholic schools give parents one more option when deciding which school will best serve their child," Governor Walker said. "Making the right choice is the first step toward helping your child achieve their greatest potential in school and in the future."
More than 59,000 of students in Wisconsin are educated at nearly 300 Catholic schools. The Governor's visit coincides with the national observance of Catholic Schools Week. Christ King is a 4K-8th grade school serving students in the Wauwatosa and surrounding community since 1939.
