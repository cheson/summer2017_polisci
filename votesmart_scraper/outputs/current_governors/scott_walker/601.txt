Governor Scott Walker Kicks Off the 2014 New North Summit
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: Dec. 2, 2014
Location: Sheboygan, WI

Today, Governor Scott Walker stopped by the annual New North Summit to talk with business and education leaders, manufacturers, elected officials, stakeholders, and community members about his priorities to move Wisconsin forward with a growing economy. Governor Walker kicked off the summit with a Q&A session with New North leadership and presented the 2014 New North Workplace Excellence Awards.
"This is an exciting time for Wisconsin and this event is a great opportunity to talk with community leaders and job creators about our plans for moving our state forward," said Governor Walker. "This year's theme is "Every Part Counts.' We know this to be true. Economic development requires input and effort across multiple sectors from jobs creators to educators. We've laid the groundwork for spurring economic development and partnerships with groups, like New North, will help us continue building upon our priorities."
The agenda for this year's summit include discussions about talent development, best practices for recruitment, and business development, as well as panels with industry and education training leaders.
The New North Summit is an annual event that brings together business, education, and community leaders from around the region to share economic development updates and learn about regional growth opportunities.
