First Lady Tonette Walker Releases a Statement Celebrating Wisconsin Fathers
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: June 19, 2016
Location: Madison, WI

First Lady Tonette Walker released the following statement today honoring husbands, fathers, and grandfathers across the state of Wisconsin this Father's Day:
Today, I join Wisconsinites across the state in reflecting on the daily contributions made by the husbands, fathers, and grandfathers in our lives. Words cannot express the sincere gratitude and appreciation we hold for these role models and everything they do to help our children, families, and communities.
To all of Wisconsin's fathers -- thank you for your continued dedication, love, and support. Happy Father's Day!
