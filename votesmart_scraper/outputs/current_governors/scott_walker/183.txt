Governor Walker, Wisconsin Department of Veterans Affairs, and Wisconsin Counties Association Issue Joint Statement
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: June 2, 2016
Location: Madison, WI

Governor Scott Walker, Wisconsin Department of Veterans Affairs (WDVA) Secretary John Scocos, and Wisconsin Counties Association (WCA) Executive Director Mark O'Connell issued a joint statement today following a meeting between representatives from the Governor's Office, WDVA, WCA, and the County Veterans Service Officers Association (CVSOs):
Today's meeting was an opportunity for all parties to come together and reach a consensus. We are pleased to announce we have agreed on a way forward to ensure CVSOs receive the funding they need in order to best serve Wisconsin's veterans. We are committed to an ongoing dialogue between all parties to guarantee our veterans receive the services they have so rightfully earned.
