Governor Scott Walker Announces Appointment of Brian Westfahl as Shawano County Coroner
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: May 28, 2015
Location: Madison, WI

Governor Scott Walker announced today the appointment of Brian Westfahl to serve as the Shawano County Coroner. The appointment follows the resignation of Marcus (Mike) Jesse and is effective June 1, 2015.
"I am confident the skill set that Brian has developed as Shawano County Deputy Coroner will provide a smooth transition to Coroner," Governor Walker said. "Furthermore, his service as an Emergency Medical Technician and volunteer firefighter demonstrates an admirable level of both compassion and professionalism necessary to lead the Coroner's Office."
Mr. Westfahl currently serves as Assistant Fire Chief and EMS Service Director in Pella, Wisconsin. Mr. Westfahl holds EMT certifications from Fox Valley Technical College, Clintonville, and Northeast Wisconsin Technical College (NWTC), Shawano. He also holds certifications from NWTC in firefighting and electrical programs.
