Wisconsin Governor Scott Walker Attends Quad/Graphics' Marketing and Thought Leadership Event
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: April 29, 2015
Location: Milwaukee, WI

Governor Scott Walker today delivered remarks at a multi-day Quad/Graphics event that brought together marketing and publishing leaders from client companies to achieving business goals.
"Quad/Graphics is a great Wisconsin company with a growing workforce," Governor Walker said. "I commend the leaders of the company for their forward-thinking, innovative ideas, which help them grow their business and will help us keep Wisconsin's economy moving in the right direction."
Quad/Graphics, headquartered in Sussex, has added about 3,000 jobs in Wisconsin in just the past 5 years. In 2009, the company employed 10,000 people in the U.S. including 4,280 in Wisconsin. In 2015, the company employs 22,000 in the U.S., including more than 7,200 in Wisconsin.
Since 2010, Quad/Graphics has invested more than $175 million in its Wisconsin plants, making them one of the most efficient and effective in the industry, and has invested more than $10 million in worker training.
