WASDA Survey Results: Myth vs. Reality
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: Nov. 14, 2011
Location: Madison, WI

Last week the Wisconsin Association of School District Administrators released a survey of school districts. 355 district administrators respond, which accounts for 83% of Wisconsin school districts.
"The reforms put in place earlier this year have not only helped schools balance their budgets without massive layoffs or property tax increases, but these innovative changes have also helped improve education, which is good for students all across Wisconsin," said Governor Walker.
