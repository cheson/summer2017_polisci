Governor Walker Signs SB 322
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: March 1, 2012
Location: Madison, WI

Today Governor Walker signed SB 322 which allows local governments to work with forest product producers to determine the appropriate weight limits for vehicles transporting raw forest products on local roads.
"This legislation will help transport raw forest products more efficiently," said Governor Walker. "Thank you to Senator Pam Galloway and Representative Jeff Mursau for seeing this bill through the legislature with bipartisan support."
