Public Statements
Governor Walker Statement on Legislative Action
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Nov. 2, 2011
Location: Madison, WI

Today Governor Walker released the following statement:
Today the State Assembly plans to continue to move forward bipartisan legislation aimed at making it easier for small businesses in Wisconsin to have access to capital. I applaud their willingness to put aside differences and work together to help the private sector create jobs.
