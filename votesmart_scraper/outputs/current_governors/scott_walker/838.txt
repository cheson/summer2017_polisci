Governor Scott Walker Attends 97th Annual Chamber of Manitowoc County Meeting
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Feb. 11, 2014
Location: Manitowoc, WI

Governor Scott Walker traveled to Manitowoc tonight for the 97th annual Chamber of Manitowoc County meeting.
"Wisconsin's economy is strong, thanks to the hard-working employees and job creators here in Manitowoc County and across the state," Governor Walker said. "With my Blueprint for Prosperity, we'll continue to boost our economic growth by developing our workforce, and lowering the tax burden, so you have more money to spend in your communities."
