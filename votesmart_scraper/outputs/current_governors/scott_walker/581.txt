Governor Scott Walker Attends Hobart Crossing Groundbreaking Ceremony
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: Jan. 15, 2015
Location: Hobart, WI

Governor Scott Walker today joined community leaders in celebrating the groundbreaking of Hobart Crossing, the first buildings to be constructed in the planned Centennial Centre at Hobart Downtown Development, a Centennial Centre Development Partners project.
"I'm excited to be celebrating the groundbreaking of this is unique development," Governor Walker said. "The Centennial Centre at Hobart Downtown Development will cater to the diverse needs of the residents of Hobart Crossing and city of Hobart at large, boasting of a grocery store and smaller shops. Not only is this community beneficial to residents of Hobart, it is also a strong indicator that businesses in Wisconsin are confident in our economy and ready to expand."
Hobart Crossing, a $25 million development, will be comprised of 180 units, including studio, 1-, and 2-bedroom leased residences. Fashionably furnished with quality cabinetry, fixtures, appliances, and flooring, the Hobart Crossing development additionally offers underground heated parking, monitored security, pet-friendly units, and a clubhouse with a pool and pool deck for the comfort and relaxation of residents.
Hobart Crossing is the first construction of the $250 million Centennial Centre project that will include commercial, retail, and recreational elements on about 75 acres. The developers say all of the suppliers and subcontractors have facilities or are based in Wisconsin, and 90 percent are from Brown, Outagamie, and Oconto Counties.
