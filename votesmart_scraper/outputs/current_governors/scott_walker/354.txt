Governor Scott Walker Delivers Remarks at Northern Wisconsin Economic Development Summit
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Dec. 1, 2015
Location: Trego, WI

Governor Scott Walker delivered remarks at the Northern Wisconsin Economic Development Summit in Trego today. The conference included keynote speakers and breakout sessions, which focused on access to capital for community and economic development, recreational trail development and promotion, workforce development, entrepreneurial assistance, and the expansion of broadband internet.
"This region is critical to our state's success," Governor Walker said. "Several of our key industries thrive in Wisconsin's Northwoods, including forestry, tourism, and manufacturing. These industries and others play a major role in the prosperity of our state as a whole, and today's summit helps us to better understand the specific needs of local business and industry leaders so we can better serve the region and ensure its' continued economic success."
