A Better Bottom Line: Governor Scott Walker Delivers Remarks at Wisconsin Employment First Conference
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: April 3, 2014
Location: Baraboo, WI

Governor Scott Walker delivered remarks today at the Wisconsin Employment First Conference. People First Wisconsin, the Wisconsin Association for Persons Supporting Employment First, and the Wisconsin Board for People with Developmental Disabilities hosted the conference themed "We Can Get the Job Done!"
"Individuals with unique abilities have great talents and dedication, and the desire to bring that to the workplace," Governor Walker said. "The Year of A Better Bottom Line is all about highlighting the advantages of hiring people with disabilities and removing their barriers to employment."
The Wisconsin Employment First conference features presentations on how to expand integrated employment for people with disabilities and offers resources and strategies to businesses looking to expand their employment. Sessions included Fostering Self-Determination with Employment, Family Involvement and Person-Centered Planning, The Fundamentals Employment Supports, and Building Communities that Support Transition. Attendees also had the opportunity to hear from individuals with disabilities about the impact getting a job had on their lives.
