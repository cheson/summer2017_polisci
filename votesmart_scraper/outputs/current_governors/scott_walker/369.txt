Governor Scott Walker Tours Turningpoint for Victims of Domestic and Sexual Violence Center
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Oct. 26, 2015
Location: River Falls, WI

Governor Scott Walker toured the Turningpoint for Victims of Domestic and Sexual Violence Center in River Falls today as a part of Domestic Violence Awareness Month. Turningpoint is a nonprofit organization that has been providing services to victims of domestic and sexual violence in Pierce and St. Croix counties for over 30 years.
"October is Domestic Violence Awareness Month, and it's an important opportunity for us to increase awareness about protecting the state's most vulnerable," Governor Walker said. "Domestic violence affects thousands of women, children, and men in Wisconsin every year. With the help of organizations like Turningpoint, we will continue our work to safeguard and advocate for victims and their children."
