Governor Walker Addresses Eau Claire Chamber of Commerce at Business Luncheon
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: July 11, 2016
Location: Eau Claire, WI

Governor Scott Walker joined local business leaders today at the Eau Claire Chamber of Commerce business luncheon to provide updates to members of the Eau Claire Community.
"The growth of our economy is fueled by local businesses, and by addressing the strengths and weaknesses of each region, town, and village in the state, we can ensure a bright economic future for Wisconsin," Governor Walker said. "Regional establishments like the Eau Claire Chamber of Commerce play an important role in promoting a healthy local business climate, which is vital to the development of Wisconsin's next generation of business leaders."
The Eau Claire Chamber of Commerce has been a part of the Eau Claire community for more than 100 years, with more than 1,200 businesses and organizations holding membership. The Eau Claire Chamber of Commerce is committed to championing a strong local and regional economy by providing a compelling portfolio of products and services to meet the needs of the business community.
