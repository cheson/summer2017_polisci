Governor Scott Walker Delivers Remarks at the Waukesha County Business Alliance Program
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Dec. 11, 2015
Location: Pewaukee, WI

Governor Scott Walker joined business and community leaders at the Waukesha County Business Alliance program in Pewaukee today. This years' program "Key Industries for Wisconsin 2016 and Beyond" included a panel of industry-leading experts that made recommendations for bolstering growth in industries, such as manufacturing, international trade, commercial development, retail and entertainment, and information technology.
"Today, we had the chance to focus on some of our key industries that are crucial in driving Wisconsin's economy," Governor Walker said. "Today's conversations provide a great platform for us to listen and collaborate with industry experts. Our future success requires developing new strategies to ensure continued industry growth in Wisconsin."
