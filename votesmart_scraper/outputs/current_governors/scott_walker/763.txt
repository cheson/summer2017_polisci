Growing Our Economy: Governor Scott Walker Delivers Remarks at Allen Edmonds
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: April 21, 2014
Location: Port Washington, WI

Governor Scott Walker joined workers and company leaders at Allen Edmonds today to tour the facility and to deliver remarks about growing business and the economy in Wisconsin.
"We are committed to encouraging business growth and development in Wisconsin, especially with established manufacturers like Allen Edmonds," said Governor Walker. "I am encouraged by their desire to grow and continue their business in Wisconsin. We're proud to be home to the headquarters of a truly Wisconsin-born brand."
Allen Edmonds, founded in 1922 in Belgium, Wisconsin, is a retailer of premium men's footwear, apparel, leather goods, and accessories with a focus on American manufacturing. Allen Edmonds handcrafts its famous Goodyear welted shoes in Port Washington.
