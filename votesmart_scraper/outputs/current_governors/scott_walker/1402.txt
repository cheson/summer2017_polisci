Governor Walker Statement on Passing of Balanced Budget Legislation
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: April 5, 2011
Location: Madison, WI

Governor Scott Walker today issued the following statement on the Assembly and Senate passing legislation that allows the state to end the year with a balanced budget, protects middle-class taxpayers, and keeps healthcare coverage for the state's most vulnerable citizens.
"I commend the legislature, both Democrats and Republicans, for working quickly to allow us to finish this year with a balanced budget and protect middle-class taxpayers," said Governor Scott Walker. "In addition, this legislation allows us to continue to provide healthcare for the poor."
