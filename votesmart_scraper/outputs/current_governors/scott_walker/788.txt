Blueprint for Prosperity: Governor Scott Walker Talks Real Tax Relief and Celebrates Badger Alloys' 48th Anniversary
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: April 1, 2014
Location: Milwaukee

Today, Governor Scott Walker made a stop at Badger Alloys in Milwaukee to celebrate the company's 48th anniversary. Governor Walker discussed his Blueprint for Prosperity and highlighted the changes to state income tax withholdings that go into effect today.
"Gone are the days of billion dollar deficits and double digit tax hikes. We're using the state surplus to return money to the hard-working taxpayers of the state," Governor Walker said. "We're building the state's economy through real government reform and tax relief so that great company's like Badger Alloys can build on their success in the future."
