Governor Walker Signs Bill into Law to Increase Funding for Recycling Grants to Local Governments
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: April 26, 2016
Location: Osceola, WI

Governor Scott Walker signed a bill into law today at J & S General Contracting to increase funding for recycling grant funding.
Assembly Bill 515, now Act 392, increases recycling grant funding provided to the Department of Natural Resources (DNR) for issuance to responsible units of government by $3 million for fiscal year 2015-16, which will positively impact the day-to-day operations of local government recycling programs. The bill was authored by Representative Paul Tittl (R -- Manitowoc) and Senator Robert Cowles (R -- Green Bay).
