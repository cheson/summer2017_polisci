Governor Walker Statement on Senate Passage of Wisconsin Working Legislation with Bipartisan Support
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Jan. 17, 2012
Location: Madison, WI

Today the State Senate passed Senate Bill (SB) 338, which is a part of the Wisconsin Working plan. This legislation waives an initial state license fee for certain veterans. The legislation passed 31 to 0.
"The State Senate's action today will make it easier for veterans to obtain professional occupational licenses," said Governor Walker. "I am pleased that the Legislature is moving forward with this bill, which is a part of our Wisconsin Working plan. I thank Senator Pam Galloway for her leadership on this bill."
