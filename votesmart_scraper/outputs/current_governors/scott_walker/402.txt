Governor Scott Walker Orders Flags Lowered in Honor of Sergeant Einar H. Ingman, Jr.
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Sept. 16, 2015
Location: Madison, WI

Today, Governor Scott Walker issued Executive Order #174 to lower the Flag of the United States of America and the Flag of the State of Wisconsin be flown at half-staff to honor Sergeant Einar H. Ingman, Jr., Wisconsin's last surviving Korean War recipient of the Medal of Honor.
"Sergeant Ingman went above and beyond the call of duty, demonstrating courage and leadership in his service during the Korean War," Governor Walker said. "Today, we honor this American patriot by flying the flags of the United States and State of Wisconsin at half-staff. My heartfelt condolences go out to the family and friends of Sergeant Ingman, and he will remain in our thoughts and prayers."
Sergeant Einar H. Ingman, Jr., passed away on September 9, 2015 at the age of 85. He will be laid to rest today with full military honors following a funeral service.
