Governor Scott Walker Visits JG Development, Inc.
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: Feb. 29, 2016
Location: Madison, WI

Governor Scott Walker was joined by staff and community leaders at JG Development, Inc. today at their Blue Mounds corporate office. JG Development, Inc. also has a second office in Madison.
"We are happy to be here at JG Development, Inc. to speak with employees as we continue to travel the state to hear from the people," Governor Walker said. "Wisconsin is in the top ten states in America when it comes to our Labor Force Participation rate, and companies continue to tell us they need more skilled workers to fill their open positions. We continue to put forward initiatives and reforms to provide our workers with the training and experience they need to obtain good-paying jobs in Wisconsin."
Founded by Jeff Grundahl in 1990, the company was first operated out of his home. JG Development, Inc. has been in business for over 25 years and now employs 41 employees between the Blue Mounds corporate office and the downtown Madison location. The company builds homes, commercial structures, places of worship, and more through two commercial entities, National Construction and NCI-Roberts, and residential home building and remodeling divisions Brio Design Homes and Vive Home Transformations.
