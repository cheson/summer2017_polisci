Governor Scott Walker Tours Create-A-Pack Foods, Inc.
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Nov. 2, 2015
Location: Ixonia, WI

Governor Scott Walker met with Create-A-Pack Foods, Inc. employees today in Ixonia. Governor Walker toured their current facility and joined in celebrating the growth of the company. Create-A-Pack Foods, Inc. is currently constructing an 114,000 square-foot addition to their existing facility.
"It's great to be here at Create-A-Pack Foods today to celebrate their expansion," Governor Walker said. "This is a major accomplishment for them, and we're happy to see expansions like this throughout the state as well. Businesses are thriving in Wisconsin, but our work is far from over, and we will continue to work to develop the skilled workforce that employers need to fill their open positions."
