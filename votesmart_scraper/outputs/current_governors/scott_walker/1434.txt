Governor Walker Statement on Passage of Tax Limitation Legislation
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Feb. 8, 2011
Location: Madison, WI

Governor Scott Walker issued the following statement today on the passing of legislation that requires a 2/3rds vote for any tax increase to pass the legislature. The legislation received bipartisan support.
"This legislation sends a signal to employees that we will tackle our state budget challenges without raising taxes," said Governor Scott Walker. "It also sets a precedent for the future that difficult decisions must be made so that Wisconsin's economy can grow. Thanks to Representatives August and Kerkman, and Senators Vukmir and Zipperer for their support."
