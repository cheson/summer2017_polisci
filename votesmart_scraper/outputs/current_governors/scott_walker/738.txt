Governor Scott Walker Celebrates Armed Forces Week
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: May 16, 2014
Location: Delafield, WI

Today, Governor Scott Walker delivered remarks at St. John's Northwestern Military Academy recognizing and thanking all members of the military. He also issued a proclamation declaring this week Armed Forces Week (attached).
"I am profoundly thankful to all the men and women who have served and are currently serving in the Armed Forces," said Governor Walker. "Every day, they are defending the freedoms enjoyed by the citizens of our great state and the United States of America. I encourage everyone to join me in thanking all our veterans and military members for their service, dedication, and commitment."
Governor Walker also presented the Governor's Medal to Cadet First Sergeant Paddy Zheng. The Governor's Medal is awarded each year to a member of the Junior Class that best embodies outstanding patriotism, fulfills his duties, and upholds the highest academic standards at St. John's.
This year marks the 65th anniversary of Armed Forces Week, which recognizes all the branches of our nation's military -- the United States Army, Navy, Air Force, Marine Corps, Coast Guard, and National Guard.
