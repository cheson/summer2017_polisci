Governor Scott Walker and First Lady Tonette Walker Take Part in Menorah Lighting Ceremony
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: Dec. 16, 2014
Location: Madison, WI

Governor Scott Walker and First Lady Tonette Walker participated in a traditional Menorah lighting ceremony today, signaling the beginning of Chanukah (Hanukkah). Leaders from the Jewish Community and students from Hillel Academy joined the Governor and First Lady at the Executive Residence for this celebration.
"Lighting the Menorah today, and over the course of the next eight nights, serves to remind us of the power and courage that comes from faith," Governor Walker said. "On behalf of Tonette, Matt, and Alex, I wish everyone celebrating the Festival of Lights a very blessed and happy holiday."
Chanukah's eight-day celebration begins on December 16th and concludes on December 24th.
