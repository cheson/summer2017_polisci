Governor Scott Walker Delivers Remarks at the Wisconsin Mortgage Bankers Association's 40th Annual Real Estate Finance Conference
Press Release
By: Scott Walker
Date: April 2, 2014
Location: Pewaukee, WI

Governor Scott Walker delivered remarks today at the Wisconsin Mortgage Bankers Association's (WMBA) 40th annual Real Estate Finance Conference. WMBA has more than 900 individual members; their spring conference is a chance for members to hear from speakers regarding the real estate finance industry.
"We're paving the way to prosperity with real reform and tax relief," Governor Walker said. "Whether you are looking at unemployment insurance claims, net new business formations, or job growth, the signs all say Wisconsin is open for business."
