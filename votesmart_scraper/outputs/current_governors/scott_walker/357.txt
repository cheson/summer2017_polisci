Governor Scott Walker Announces Northern Wisconsin Economic Development Summit
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Nov. 19, 2015
Location: Madison, WI

Governor Scott Walker announced that registration is open for the ninth annual Governor's Northern Wisconsin Economic Development Summit, held on December 1, 2015 in Trego. The summit will include remarks from Governor Walker and Lt. Governor Rebecca Kleefisch, as well as an opportunity to interact with residents and businesses in northern Wisconsin, and includes information on access to capital, workforce development, entrepreneurial assistance, and the expansion of broadband internet.
"We've made significant investments in worker training, broadband expansion grants, and other programs to support an economic climate that creates private-sector jobs throughout the state," Governor Walker said. "Our administration has focused on key issues that benefit residents and businesses throughout northern Wisconsin, and we look forward to the Northern Wisconsin Economic Development Summit, where we'll discuss how we can continue to move Wisconsin and this part of the state forward."
