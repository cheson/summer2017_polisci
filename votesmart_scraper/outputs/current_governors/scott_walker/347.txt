Governor Scott Walker Tours Marquis and Carver Yachts in Pulaski
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Dec. 10, 2015
Location: Pulaski, WI

Governor Scott Walker joined executives and employees today at Marquis and Carver Yachts in Pulaski for a tour of their facilities and a meeting with employees. Marquis Yachts, LLC is an independent and privately-owned company specializing in 34- to 72-foot boats under the brand names of Marquis and Carver Yachts.
"Across the state, more people are working and our economy is stronger than it was when we took office," Governor Walker said. "The state's unemployment rate is down to 4.3 percent, and the unemployment rate here in Brown County is even lower at 3.2 percent. Companies like Marquis and Carver Yachts have seen significant growth in the past few years, and we remain dedicated to growing our economy to help create jobs across the state."
