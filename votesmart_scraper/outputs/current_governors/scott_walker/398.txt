Governor Scott Walker Celebrates Apache Stainless Equipment Corporation's 40th Anniversary
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Sept. 25, 2015
Location: Madison, WI

Governor Scott Walker joined employees and members of the community in celebrating the 40th Anniversary of Apache Stainless Equipment Corporation today at their open house in Beaver Dam. Apache Stainless Equipment Corporation is a manufacturer of commercial industrial and wholesale capital equipment.
"Congratulations to Apache Stainless Equipment Corporation on their 40th Anniversary. It is a pleasure to join them in celebrating this accomplishment," Governor Walker said. "Apache employs 175 experienced Wisconsinites as craftsmen, technicians, engineers, and managers. They are great supporters of our workforce, investing in our state and growing jobs in Wisconsin. My thanks to Apache for helping us to grow our economy and to continue moving our state forward."
