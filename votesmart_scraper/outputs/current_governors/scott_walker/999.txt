Governor Scott Walker Signs Bill Giving Milwaukee County Voters a Voice in Reforming Government
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: May 31, 2013
Location: Milwaukee, WI

Governor Scott Walker released the following statement today after he signed Assembly Bill 85, which makes a number of commonsense reforms to Milwaukee County government and calls for a binding referendum to determine salaries for members of the Milwaukee County Board:
"These long-awaited reforms will make Milwaukee County government more efficient and will save money for its hard-working taxpayers. The people of Milwaukee County will finally have their voices heard through a vote in 2014. I thank Senator Alberta Darling (R-River Hills) and Representative Joe Sanfelippo (R-West Allis) for their leadership on this bill."
