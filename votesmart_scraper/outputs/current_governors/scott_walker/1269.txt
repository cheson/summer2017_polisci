Governor Walker's Statement on Senate Passage of Family Care Expansion
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Feb. 14, 2012
Location: Madison, WI

Today the State Senate passed Senate Bill 380, which allows all Wisconsin counties to participate in Family Care and lifts the cap on the number of enrollees. SB 380 passed with a final vote of 33-0.
"I applaud the State Senate for passing the Family Care expansion," said Governor Walker. "I would like to thank State Senator Terry Moulton and Leah Vukmir for providing leadership on this bill as it made its way through the State Senate."
