Public Statements
National Wear Red Day -- February 7, 2014
Statement
By: Scott Walker
Date: Feb. 6, 2014
Location: Madison, WI

Governor Scott Walker released the following statement today encouraging Wisconsinites to wear red tomorrow for National Wear Red Day:
Every February, the American Heart Association celebrates Heart Month to raise awareness about the number one killer of women in the United States, heart disease. All around the country, people are joining together to educate others about the risks of heart disease and learn about the warning signs of the disease. I hope you will join Tonette and me as we wear red for women.
