Public Statements
Statement On The Presidential Inauguration
Statement
By: Doug Ducey
Date: Jan. 20, 2017
Location: Washington, DC

"This morning I am thrilled to congratulate Donald Trump and Mike Pence as they officially take the oath of office to lead our nation as the 45th and 48th president and vice president of the United States.
"Every presidential inauguration is special, historic and a reminder to all of us why America is exceptional.
"It's an honor to be here with Americans from all corners of our nation to witness the peaceful and seamless transition of power.
"On behalf of Angela, myself and Arizonans everywhere, we offer our congratulations, our prayers, and our support for President Trump and Vice President Pence as they assume the awesome responsibility of guiding America and meeting the challenges facing us."

Source: http://azgovernor.gov/governor/news/2017/01/statement-presidential-inauguration
