Governor Doug Ducey To Hold Statewide Leadership Summit
Press Release
By: Doug Ducey
Date: April 28, 2015
Location: Phoenix, AZ

Governor Doug Ducey announced today that he will host economic development, education, business and elected leaders from all corners of Arizona for a leadership summit on May 14, 2015 at University of Phoenix Stadium. The event will allow leaders from across the state to convene for a discussion about how to make Arizona the best place in America to work, do business and get an education.
Speakers at the Governor's Leadership Summit will include:
Arthur Brooks, President of the American Enterprise Institute
An Education Panel featuring Joel Klein, former Chancellor of the New York City Department of Education and
Paul Pastorek, former Louisiana Superintendent of Public Instruction
Frank Luntz, Author, pollster, and leading communications consultant
"I'm excited to announce this event and look forward to bringing together professionals from throughout Arizona -- from all backgrounds, sectors and areas of expertise," said Governor Ducey. "We've made some remarkable progress in Arizona in just a few short months -- and as we look ahead to the next few years, we're going to remain steadfastly committed to educational excellence and economic opportunity for all in our state. I'm excited to work with and learn from other passionate leaders as we chart a prosperous course."
The Governor's Leadership Summit will take place from 1:30 - 4:30 p.m. on Thursday, May 14, at University of Phoenix Stadium.
Details to follow for media interested in covering this event.
