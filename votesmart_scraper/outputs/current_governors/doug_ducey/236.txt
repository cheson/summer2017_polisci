Governor Doug Ducey To Attend WGA Annual Meeting
Press Release
By: Doug Ducey
Date: June 19, 2015
Location: Phoenix, AZ

Governor Doug Ducey next week will head to Lake Tahoe, Nevada, for the annual meeting of the Western Governors' Association (June 24-26, 2015).
The governor will join several western governors to address a variety of significant regional issues, including transportation infrastructure, water and drought policy, wildfire management and more.
"As a western state, Arizona is constantly dealing with complex and challenging issues unique to our region -- the most urgent being water and wildfire management," said Governor Ducey. "This meeting presents an important opportunity for western governors to come to the table, address these issues of pressing importance, identify best practices and develop sound strategy and policy. I look forward to working with my fellow governors to ensure the long-term sustainability and economic prosperity of our shared region."
