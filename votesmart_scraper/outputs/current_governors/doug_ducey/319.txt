Governor-Elect Doug Ducey Congratulates Congresswoman-Elect McSally On Her Victory
Statement
By: Doug Ducey
Date: Dec. 17, 2014
Location: Phoenix, AZ

Arizona Governor-elect Doug Ducey today released the following statement regarding Congresswoman-elect Martha McSally's victory in Arizona's Second Congressional District recount:
"I couldn't be more excited that Martha McSally will be representing Southern Arizona in Congress," Ducey said. "This election has been a long road, but with this outcome, voters now have finality. No one will work harder than Congresswoman-elect McSally to fight for Southern Arizona. I can't wait to work with her and hit the ground running on behalf of our great state."
