Governor Doug Ducey to Hold Tele-TownHall on Education
Press Release
By: Doug Ducey
Date: Feb. 5, 2015
Location: Phoenix, AZ

Governor Doug Ducey this evening will invite Southern Arizonans to participate in a tele-town hall meeting focused on K-12 education. The governor will be joined by former Arizona Superintendent of Public Instruction, Lisa Graham Keegan, to discuss and answer questions.
Governor Ducey announced in his State of the State address a Classrooms First Initiative, also reflected in his budget proposal, which proposes increasing classroom funding by $134 million.
"I'm excited to hear from Southern Arizonans as we look for ways to get more money in the classroom and increase educational excellence in all our public schools," said Governor Ducey. "This forum provides a great opportunity to connect directly with Arizonans on an issue that affects every family in every region of our state, and I'm looking forward to it."
