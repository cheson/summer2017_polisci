Governor Doug Ducey's Statement Regarding Kearny Fire
Press Release
By: Doug Ducey
Date: June 17, 2015
Location: Phoenix, AZ

The Governor's Office is in close communication with the Arizona Department of Emergency Management and State Forestry as they provide support to contain the fire in Kearny. Arizona Department of Transportation crews have also been dispatched to redirect traffic.
Governor Ducey continues to be briefed on the situation and offered the following statement:
"My office is in close communication with emergency management officials as they work to contain and extinguish this fire. My thoughts and prayers are with the firefighters working diligently to contain the fire and the Arizonans who have been displaced. We will continue to monitor the fire and provide any and all resources necessary to extinguish it."
