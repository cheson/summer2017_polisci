Governor Doug Ducey Welcomes Coach Welter To Arizona
Statement
By: Doug Ducey
Date: July 28, 2015
Location: Phoenix, AZ

"I want to be one of the first to welcome Coach Jen Welter to Arizona and to the Cardinals, and congratulate her on making NFL history.
"Arizona is truly proud. I salute Bruce Arians, Michael Bidwill and the entire Cardinals organization for this hire. Coach Welter's experience speaks for itself, and as a Cardinals fan, I could not be more excited that she'll be preparing our favorite team during the preseason."
