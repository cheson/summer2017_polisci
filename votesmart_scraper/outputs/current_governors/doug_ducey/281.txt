Public Statements
Statement from Governor Ducey regarding the Mesa shootings
Statement
By: Doug Ducey
Date: March 18, 2015
Location: Phoenix, AZ

"I'm extremely appreciative of all the state and local law enforcement who put themselves in harm's way today during this horrific shooting, and the first responders who assisted the victims. Arizona is grateful for their swift and diligent efforts. Our hearts remain with all of them, as well as the victims, their families and loved ones as they cope with this awful tragedy."
