Governor Doug Ducey Recognizes Arizona's First Responders
Statement
By: Doug Ducey
Date: Sept. 27, 2015
Location: Phoenix, AZ

Governor Doug Ducey has proclaimed this Sunday, Sept. 27, First Responder Appreciation Day in honor of the first responders who risk their safety to protect and help Arizona citizens.
Our first responders put their lives on the line every day to protect the safety, security and lives of Arizonans," said Governor Doug Ducey. "These heroes stand at the ready 24 hours a day, seven days a week to maintain safety and order, often putting themselves directly in harm's way. I could not be more grateful for their selfless service and sacrifice to the citizens of Arizona.
