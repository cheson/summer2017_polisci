Governor Doug Ducey Statement on Renaming of Douglas Port of Entry in Honor of Late Governor Raul Castro
Statement
By: Doug Ducey
Date: Oct. 14, 2015
Location: Phoenix, AZ

Governor Doug Ducey today issued the following statement in honor of the official renaming of the Douglas Land Port of Entry to the Raul Hector Castro Land Port of Entry, in honor of Arizona's late 14th governor.
"Governor Castro was a devoted civil servant, a beloved family man, a loyal friend and an unforgettable, invaluable part of Arizona history," said Governor Ducey. "He will be remembered not only for making history as our first Mexican-American governor, but more importantly, for the work he did to make Arizona a better place to live. His mission to make our state and nation a "remarkable, hopeful and productive land of opportunity' is an example we strive to follow today. I'm proud Arizona will continue to honor Governor Castro's life and legacy with this fitting and lasting tribute."
