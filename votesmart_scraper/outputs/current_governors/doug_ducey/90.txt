Governor Doug Ducey to Attend NGA Summer Meeting, Republican National Convention
Press Release
By: Doug Ducey
Date: July 14, 2016
Location: Phoenix, AZ

Governor Doug Ducey left today for the National Governors Association (NGA) summer meeting, being held July 14-16 in Des Moines, Iowa.
"These NGA meetings provide an important opportunity to collaborate with governors from across the country on key issues affecting our states and our nation as a whole," said Governor Ducey. "I look forward to discussing the challenges, opportunities and advancements we're making in Arizona, particularly in the work we're doing to combat the opioid epidemic across state lines."
Following the NGA meeting, the governor will travel to the Republican National Convention in Cleveland, Ohio, July 17-22, where he will serve as chairman of the Arizona delegation.
