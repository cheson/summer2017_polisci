Gov. Hickenlooper Statement About Amendment 64 Implementation Task Force Report
Statement
By: John Hickenlooper
Date: March 13, 2013
Location: Denver, CO

Gov. John Hickenlooper released this statement today about the Amendment 64 Implementation Task Force report:

"The commendable work by the task force sets the stage for sensible regulation and enforcement in Colorado. We want to thank Barbara Brohl and Jack Finlaw for their leadership on the task force. The entire group carefully and thoughtfully worked through dozens of issues and ideas. We look forward to now working with the General Assembly to follow through on the task force's recommendations."

The Amendment 64 Implementation Task Force report is available at www.Colorado.gov/revenue/amendment64.
