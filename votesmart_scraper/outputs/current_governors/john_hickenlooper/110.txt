Gov. Hickenlooper Issues Statement on Sentencing in Aurora Theater Shooting Trial
Statement
By: John Hickenlooper
Date: Aug. 7, 2015
Location: Denver, CO

Gov. John Hickenlooper today issued the following statement in response to the sentencing decision in the Aurora theater shooting trial:
"Our thoughts remain with the victims and families who have suffered unspeakable tragedy. No verdict can bring back what they have lost but we hope they begin to find peace and healing in the coming weeks.
