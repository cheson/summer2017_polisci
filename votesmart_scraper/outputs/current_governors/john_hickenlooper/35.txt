Gov. Hickenlooper Orders Flags Lowered to Honor the Victims of the Attack in Nice, France
Press Release
By: John Hickenlooper
Date: July 15, 2016
Location: Denver, CO

Gov. John Hickenlooper today ordered flags to remain at half-staff to honor the victims of the attack in Nice, France, and as proclaimed by President Barack Obama. Flags should be lowered until Tuesday, July 19 at sunset.
