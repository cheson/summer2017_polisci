Gov. Hickenlooper offers Condolences to Colorado Springs Community
Statement
By: John Hickenlooper
Date: Nov. 27, 2015
Location: Denver, CO

Gov. John Hickenlooper issued the following statement as fatalities in the Colorado Springs shooting have been determined:
"Our deepest condolences go out to the families and loved ones of those who were killed, including UCCS Officer Garrett Swasey, and those who were injured. Our thoughts continue to be with law enforcement, the Colorado Springs community, and the staff and patients at Planned Parenthood."
