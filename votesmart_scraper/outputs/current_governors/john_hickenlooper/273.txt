State Capitol Draped in Orange, Blue for the Denver Broncos' Playoff Run
Press Release
By: John Hickenlooper
Date: Jan. 10, 2014
Location: Denver, CO

Gov. John Hickenlooper, Senate President Morgan Carroll and House Speaker Mark Ferrandino today asked all Coloradans to support the Denver Broncos in their sure-to-be-exciting playoff run. The state Capitol will show its "United in Orange" spirit with two Denver Broncos flags draped over the West Balcony as long as the team is in the playoffs.
"John Fox, Peyton Manning and entire team know no quit," Hickenlooper said. "We look forward watching the Denver Broncos' playoff run with the same excitement we had after each of the team's 13 wins during the regular season. Go Broncos!"
"It is great when we can find something that unites us in Colorado," Carroll said.
"I have enjoyed watching the Bronco's season unfold and am excited for the playoff game on Sunday and beyond," Ferrandino said. "In fact, today we have a relaxed dress code for members who are dressed in Broncos colors. Go Broncos
