Press Release
By: Bruce Rauner
Date: May 26, 2016
Location: Unknown

Honoring Illinois' Fallen
United States and Illinois flags at half-staff from Sunrise to Noon on Monday, May 30, 2016.
The Department of Central Management Services has received notice from
Governor Bruce Rauner that all persons or entities covered by the Illinois Flag Display Act are to fly the flags at half-staff:
In Observance of Memorial Day
Please lower the United States flags and the State flags at all buildings occupied by your agency's personnel on:
Monday, May 30, 2016 from Sunrise until Noon
If you have any questions regarding this matter, please contact Jacquelyn Reineke at (312) 485-8227 or via email at jacquelyn.reineke@illinois.gov
More information is available at http://www.illinois.gov/news/Pages/Flag.aspx.
