Press Release
By: Bruce Rauner
Date: May 13, 2016
Location: Unknown

Lowering of Flags -In honor of the Peace Officers Memorial Day
The Department of Central Management Services has received notice from
The President of the United States of America and Illinois Governor Bruce Rauner
that all persons or entities covered by the Illinois Flag Display Act are to fly the flags at half-staff in honor of:
Peace Officers Memorial Day
Please immediately lower the United States flags and the State flags at all buildings occupied by your agency's personnel from:
Sunrise to Sunset on Sunday, May 15, 2016
