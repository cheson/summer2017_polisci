Public Statements
Kasich Signs Bill
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: May 26, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich signed the following bill into law:
S.B. 315 (Manning, G.) makes supplemental appropriations related to transportation for the biennium beginning July 1, 2015, and ending June 30, 2017, and to declare an emergency.
