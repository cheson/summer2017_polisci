Kasich Statement on Appeals Court Ruling in JobsOhio Case
Statement
By: John Kasich
Date: June 14, 2012
Location: Columbus, OH

Ohio Gov. John R. Kasich issued the following statement regarding today's ruling by the Ohio 10th District Court of Appeals upholding the Franklin County Common Pleas Court's dismissal of the ProgressOhio lawsuit over the creation of JobsOhio:
"I am very pleased that the court of appeals agreed with the trial court in rejecting arguments which we have always believed are erroneous. Now JobsOhio can continue to move forward with its important efforts to help create a jobs-friendly climate so more Ohioans can get back to work."
