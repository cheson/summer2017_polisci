Kasich Announces Reappointment to State Board
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: May 17, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following reappointment:
Jessica L. Bumgardner of Scio (Harrison Co.) has been reappointed to the Belmont College Board of Trustees for a term beginning May 17, 2016 and ending May 12, 2019.
