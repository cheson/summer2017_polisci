Kasich Announces Appointments to State Boards
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: May 11, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following appointments:
Lori Barreras of Columbus (Franklin Co.) has been reappointed to the Ohio Collaborative Community-Police Advisory Board for a term beginning May 11, 2016 and ending April 29, 2019.
Marlon R. Moore of Galena (Franklin Co.) has been appointed to the Central State University Board of Trustees for a term beginning May 11, 2016 and ending June 30, 2024.
Reginald L. Fields of Columbus (Franklin Co.) has been appointed to the Central State University Board of Trustees for a term beginning May 11, 2016 and ending June 30, 2021.
