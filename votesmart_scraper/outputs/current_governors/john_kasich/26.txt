Kasich Announces Reappointments to State Council
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: June 10, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following reappointments:
Jeffrey T. Ferriell of Columbus (Franklin Co.) and Larry T. Garvin of Bexley (Franklin Co.) have been reappointed to the State Council of Uniform State Laws for terms beginning June 10, 2016 and ending June 5, 2019.
