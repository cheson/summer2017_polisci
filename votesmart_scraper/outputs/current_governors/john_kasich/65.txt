Kasich Announces Appointment to State Board
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: April 29, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following appointment:
John C. Pascoe of Worthington (Franklin Co.) has been appointed to the Miami University Board of Trustees for a term beginning April 29, 2016 and ending February 28, 2024.
