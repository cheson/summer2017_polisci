Kasich Announces Appointments to State Boards
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: June 3, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following appointments:
Cy L. Prettyman of New Bloomington (Marion Co.) has been appointed to the Ohio Livestock Care Standards Board for a term beginning June 3, 2016 and ending January 15, 2019.
Kimberly M. Corrigan of Strongsville (Cuyahoga Co.), Terrence M. Holecek of Powell (Delaware Co.), and Joanne J. Whiteman of Newark (Licking Co.) have been reappointed to the Board of Executives of Long-term Services and Supports for terms beginning June 3, 2016 and ending May 27, 2019.
