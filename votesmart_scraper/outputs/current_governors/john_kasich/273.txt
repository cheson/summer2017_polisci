Kasich and Taylor Issue Thanksgiving Day Proclamation
Statement
By: Mary Taylor John Kasich
Date: Nov. 27, 2013
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich and Lt. Governor Mary Taylor issued a proclamation in honor of Thanksgiving in which they said:
"Thanksgiving is a time for friends and family to gather together and express gratitude for our blessings, to celebrate the freedoms we enjoy and loved ones who enrich our lives."
