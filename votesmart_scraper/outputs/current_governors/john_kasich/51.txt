Kasich Announces Appointment to State Board
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: May 13, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following appointment:
Ferzan M. Ahmed of Powell (Delaware Co.) has been appointed to the State Board of Registration for Professional Engineers and Surveyors for a term beginning May 13, 2016 and ending September 24, 2016.
