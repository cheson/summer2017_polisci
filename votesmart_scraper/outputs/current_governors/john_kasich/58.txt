Kasich Announces Appointments to State Boards May 6, 2016
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: May 6, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following appointments:
Samuel W. Grooms of Westerville (Franklin Co.) has been appointed to the Youngstown State University Board of Trustees for a term beginning May 6, 2016 and ending April 30, 2025.
Allan K. Metz of Warren (Trumbull Co.) has been appointed to serve as the student trustee member on the Youngstown State University Board of Trustees for a term beginning May 6, 2016 and ending April 30, 2018.
