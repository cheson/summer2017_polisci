Kasich Announces Appointments to State Commission and Board
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: May 16, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following appointments:
Chief Scott M. Reinbolt of Blanchester (Clinton Co.) has been appointed to the Ohio Private Investigation and Security Services Commission for a term beginning May 16, 2016 and ending December 31, 2020.
Lydia A. Lancaster of Columbus (Franklin Co.) has been appointed as the student member of The Ohio State University Board of Trustees for a term beginning May 16, 2016 and ending May 13, 2018.
