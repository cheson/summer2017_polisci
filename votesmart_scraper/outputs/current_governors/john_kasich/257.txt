Kasich and Taylor Issue Proclamation in Honor of Days of Remembrance
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: April 28, 2014
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich and Lieutenant Governor Mary Taylor
issued a proclamation recognizing the week of April 27-May 4, 2014 as Days of
Remembrance, a time dedicated to the memory of the victims of the Holocaust and in
honor of the survivors, rescuers and liberators.
"We should always remember the terrible events of the Holocaust and remain vigilant
against hatred, persecution and tyranny," Kasich and Taylor shared in the resolution.
"We should actively rededicate ourselves to the principles of individual freedom in a just
society."
