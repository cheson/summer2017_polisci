Statement
By: John Kasich

Today, Ohio Governor John Kasich issued the following statement in response to Donald Trump's call for banning all Muslim travel into the U.S.
"This is just more of the outrageous divisiveness that characterizes his every breath and another reason why he is entirely unsuited to lead the United States."
