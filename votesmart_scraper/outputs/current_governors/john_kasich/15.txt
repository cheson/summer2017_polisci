Kasich Statement on Amazon's Growing Investment in Ohio
Statement
By: John Kasich
Date: Oct. 17, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Gov. John R. Kasich issued the following statement regarding Amazon Web Services' launch of their new data centers in Ohio:

"As technology continues to migrate toward greater use of cloud computing, having the world leader in that field--Amazon Web Services-- here in Ohio demonstrates the critical role our state is playing in the future of the world's economy. Ohio is not just embracing the new knowledge economy, we're helping create it."
