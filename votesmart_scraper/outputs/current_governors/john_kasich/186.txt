Public Statements
Letter to Keith Faber, President of the Ohio Senate and Cliff Rosenberger, Speaker of the Ohio House - State of the State
Letter
By: John Kasich
Date: Jan. 30, 2015
Location: Unknown

Dear President Faber and Speaker Rosenberger,
I respectfully write to you to request a joint convention of the 131st
General Assembly on Tuesday, February 24, 2015 at 7:00 p.m. in Wilmington at the Roberts Centre for the purpose of delivering my State of the State address.
Thank you for your attention to this request, and I look forward to your favorable consideration. If you require additional information, please do not hesitate to contact my office for assistance.
Sincerely,
John R. Kasich
Governor
