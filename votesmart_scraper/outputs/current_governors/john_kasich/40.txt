Kasich Announces Appointments to State Boards and Council
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: May 26, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following appointments:
Mark A. Tyler of Kirtland (Lake Co.) has been appointed to the Lakeland Community College Board of Trustees for a term beginning May 26, 2016 and ending October 12, 2017.
Carter P. Lewis of Warren (Trumbull Co.) has been appointed to the Eastern Gateway Community College Board of Trustees for a term beginning May 26, 2016 and ending October 16, 2019.
Beth L. Mowrey of Thornville (Perry Co.) has been appointed to the Materials Management Advisory Council for a term beginning May 26, 2016 and ending July 1, 2016.
