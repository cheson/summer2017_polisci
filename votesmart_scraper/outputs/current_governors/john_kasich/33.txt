Kasich Announces Appointment to State Board
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: June 1, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following appointment:
J. Richard Lumpe of Columbus (Franklin Co.) has been appointed to the State Employment Relations Board for a term beginning June 1, 2016 and ending October 6, 2021.
