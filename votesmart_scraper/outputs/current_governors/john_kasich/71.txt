Public Statements
Kasich Signs Two Bills
Statement
By: John Kasich
Date: April 5, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich signed the following bills into law:
Am. S.B. 124 (Thomas) designates May as "Asthma Awareness Month" and designates May 5 as "Childhood Asthma Awareness Day."'
S.B. 181 (Obhof, Schiavoni) prescribes the fiduciary duties of corporate and limited liability company officers, specifies that officers are not required for limited liability companies, permits a written waiver or elimination of the fiduciary duties of limited liability company members, managers, or officers, clarifies when a limited liability company manager's or officer's duties can be the same as a member's duties, declares the policy of the Limited Liability Company Law generally to give maximum effect to freedom of contract, and makes other changes regarding corporations and limited liability companies.
