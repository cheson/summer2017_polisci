Kasich and Taylor Issue Thanksgiving Day Proclamation
Statement
By: Mary Taylor John Kasich
Date: Nov. 26, 2014
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich and Lt. Governor Mary Taylor issued a
proclamation honoring Thanksgiving, in which they said:
"Thanksgiving is a time for friends and family to gather together and express gratitude
for our blessings, to celebrate the freedoms we enjoy and loved ones who enrich our
lives. We ask all Ohioans to remember the many blessings that we have experienced
throughout the year.
