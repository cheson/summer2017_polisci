Kasich Announces Appointments to State Boards
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: May 24, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following appointments:
Rabbi Avraham L. Goldstein of Columbus (Franklin Co.) has been appointed to the Advisory Board of the Governor's Office of Faith-Based and Community Initiatives for a term beginning May 24, 2016 and ending May 4, 2017.
Lee Ann Alexakos of Sandusky (Erie Co.) has been appointed to the TourismOhio Advisory Board for a term beginning May 24, 2016 and ending September 27, 2018.
