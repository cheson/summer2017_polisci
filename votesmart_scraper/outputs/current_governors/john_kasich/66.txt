Kasich Announces Appointments to State Boards
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: April 28, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following appointments:
Michael C. Aquillo of Powell (Delaware Co.) has been appointed to the Ohio Respiratory Care Board for a term beginning April 28, 2016 and ending March 14, 2018.
Michael R. Pratt of Perrysburg (Wood Co.) has been appointed to the Hearing Aid Dealers and Fitters Licensing Board for a term beginning April 28, 2016 and ending January 25, 2018.
