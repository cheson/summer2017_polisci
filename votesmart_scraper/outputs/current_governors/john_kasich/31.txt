Kasich Announces Appointments to State Boards
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: June 2, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following appointments:
Ross W. McGregor of Springfield (Clark Co.) has been appointed to the State Personnel Board of Review for a term beginning June 2, 2016 and ending February 7, 2019.
Richard E. Dalton of Miamisburg (Montgomery Co.) has been appointed to the Governor's Executive Workforce Board for a term beginning June 2, 2016 and continuing at the pleasure of the governor.
