Kasich Announces Appointments to State Committee and Board
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: May 31, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following appointments:
Alex Johnson of Solon (Cuyahoga Co.) has been appointed to the Community Schools Dropout Recovery Study Committee for a term beginning May 31, 2016 and continuing at the pleasure of the governor.
Todd C. Davidson of Cleveland (Cuyahoga Co.) has been appointed to the Cleveland State University Board of Trustees for a term beginning May 31, 2016 and ending May 1, 2025.
