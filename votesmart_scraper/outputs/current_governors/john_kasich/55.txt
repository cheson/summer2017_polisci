Kasich Announces Appointments to State Boards
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: May 9, 2016
Location: Columbia, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following appointments:
Father J. Kevin Maney of Westerville (Franklin Co.) and Deborah Givens of Columbus (Franklin Co.) have been reappointed to the Advisory Board of the Governor's Office of Faith-Based and Community Initiatives for terms beginning May 9, 2016 and ending May 4, 2017.
Ronald E. Falconi of Brunswick (Medina Co.) has been appointed to the Petroleum Underground Storage Tank Release Compensation Board for a term beginning May 9, 2016 and ending July 10, 2018.
