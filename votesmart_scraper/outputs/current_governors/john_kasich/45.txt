Kasich Announces Appointments to State Board and Council
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: May 20, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following appointments:
S. Craig Beam of Sabina (Clinton Co.) has been appointed to the Ohio Thoroughbred Race Fund Advisory Council for a term beginning May 20, 2016 and ending January 31, 2019.
Paul Schoonover of Columbus (Franklin Co.) has been appointed to the Chemical Dependency Professionals Board for a term beginning May 20, 2016 and ending December 23, 2018.
