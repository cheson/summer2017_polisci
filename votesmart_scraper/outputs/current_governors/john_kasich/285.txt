Kasich Issues Proclamation Honoring Independence Day
Statement
By: John Kasich
Date: July 3, 2013
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich issued a proclamation in honor of Independence Day on Thursday, July 4, 2013, in which he said:
"The United States of America, founded on the principles of life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness, is the product of diversity and fortitude and a beacon of hope and freedom to the world."
