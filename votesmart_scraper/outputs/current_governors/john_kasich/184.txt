Kasich Orders Flags Flown at Half-Staff in Honor of Mayor D. Michael Collins
Statement
By: John Kasich
Date: Feb. 11, 2015
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich ordered that flags continue to be flown at half-staff upon all public buildings and grounds throughout the City of Toledo, Lucas County and at the Ohio Statehouse from sunrise to sunset through February 12, 2015, in honor of Toledo Mayor D. Michael Collins. His order reads:
"In honor of the life and service of Mayor D. Michael Collins, I hereby proclaim, by the authority vested in me as Governor of the State of Ohio by the Ohio Constitution and the laws of the United States of America, that the flags of the United States of America and the State of Ohio shall continue to be flown at half-staff upon all public buildings and grounds throughout the City of Toledo from sunrise to sunset through February 12, 2015, and also throughout Lucas County and at the Ohio Statehouse from sunrise to sunset on February 12, 2015."
