Kasich Signs Five Bills
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: Feb. 14, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich signed the following bills into law:
Sub. SB 2 (Peterson) expressly incorporates changes in the Internal Revenue Code since April 1, 2015, into Ohio law, and declares an emergency.
Sub. SB 60 (Hughes) designates a portion of Interstate Route 670 as the "Dana G. 'Buck' Rinehart Memorial Highway."
HB 17 (Blessing, Landis) provides civil immunity for architects, contractors, engineers, surveyors, and tradespersons providing volunteer services during a declared emergency.
HB 98 (Brown) designates the fourteenth day of May as "Childhood Apraxia of Speech Awareness Day."
HB 121 (Stinziano, Ruhl) designates the last week of July as "Service Dog Awareness Week."
