Public Statements
Kasich Signs Executive Order
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: May 4, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich signed Executive Order 2016-01K.
Executive Order 2016-01K authorizes the State Board of Pharmacy to take emergency action to make a synthetic opioid known as U-47700 a Schedule I controlled substance and subject to criminal drug penalties.
