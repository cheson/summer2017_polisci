Kasich and Taylor Issue Resolution Recognizing Veterans Day
Statement
By: John Kasich Mary Taylor
Date: Nov. 10, 2014
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich and Lieutenant Governor Mary Taylor issued a resolution recognizing November 11, 2014 as Veterans Day and encouraged all Ohioans to join in recognizing the men and women who have courageously served in the military.
"As a reminder of the price we pay for our freedoms, we have an obligation to honor the valor and service of each and every veteran," Kasich and Taylor noted. They encouraged all Ohioans to "display the American flag and attend special ceremonies and parades held throughout the state to honor and thank all veterans for their selfless service to our country."
