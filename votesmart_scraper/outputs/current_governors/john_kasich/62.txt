Kasich Announces Appointments to State Boards
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: May 3, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following appointments:
Richard C. Hylant of Ottawa Hills (Lucas Co.) has been reappointed to the Great Lakes Protection Fund Board for a term beginning May 3, 2016 and ending October 10, 2017.
Nancy P. Hollister, former governor, lieutenant governor and state representative, of Marietta (Washington Co.) has been appointed to represent District 8 on the State Board of Education. She will assume the seat on May 3, 2016, and must run in November 2016 to retain the seat for the unexpired term ending December 31, 2018. Hollister is replacing Dr. Robert E. McDonald Jr., who resigned.
