Kasich Announces Appointments to State Boards
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: May 23, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following appointments:
Ronald A. George of Westerville (Delaware Co.) has been appointed to the Ohio Commission on Fatherhood for a term beginning May 23, 2016 and ending July 30, 2017.
Lora L. Miller of Columbus (Franklin Co.) has been reappointed to the Ohio Retirement Study Council for a term beginning May 23, 2016 and ending June 30, 2016.
Monique R. Hamilton of Pickerington (Fairfield Co.) has been appointed to the Community Schools Dropout Recovery Study Committee for a term beginning May 23, 2016 and continuing at the pleasure of the governor.
