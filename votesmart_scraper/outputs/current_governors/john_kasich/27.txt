Kasich Announces Appointment to State Commission
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: June 9, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following appointment:
William R. Creedon of Upper Arlington (Franklin Co.) has been appointed to the Ohio Public Defender Commission for a term beginning June 9, 2016 and ending January 12, 2020.
