Kasich Announces Reappointments to State Board and Commission
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: May 2, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following reappointments:
Jude S. Foulk of Marion (Marion Co.) has been reappointed to the Marion Technical College Board of Trustees for a term beginning May 2, 2016 and ending April 29, 2019.
John T. Patton III of Spencer (Medina Co.) has been reappointed to the Ohio Athletic Commission for a term beginning May 2, 2016 and ending September 2, 2018.
