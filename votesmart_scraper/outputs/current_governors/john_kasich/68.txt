Kasich Announces Reappointments to State Commission and Board
Press Release
By: John Kasich
Date: April 26, 2016
Location: Columbus, OH

Today Governor John R. Kasich announced the following reappointments:
Sheila N. Fox of Worthington (Franklin Co.) and Brenda Stier-Anstine of Delaware (Delaware Co.) have been reappointed to the Ohio Commission on Service and Volunteerism for terms beginning April 26, 2016 and ending April 21, 2019.
Richard F. Hillis of Powell (Delaware Co.) and Juan P. Cespedes of Columbus (Franklin Co.) have been reappointed to the Capitol Square Review and Advisory Board for terms beginning April 26, 2016 and ending April 22, 2019.
