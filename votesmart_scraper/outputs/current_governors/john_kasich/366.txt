In Case You Missed It: Kasich on Merit Pay for Teachers, 700 WLW
Statement
By: John Kasich
Date: April 26, 2011
Location: Unknown

Earlier this month, Gov. John Kasich spoke with Bill Cunningham of Cincinnati's 700 WLW. Below is a highlight from their conversation:
"We want to say, over time, that we want to pay teachers a lot more money based on performance, Bill, based on performance. We want to develop a criteria based on some testing and based on peer review, principal review and parental review."
