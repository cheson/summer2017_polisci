Governor Brown Orders Flags at Half-Staff in Honor of Memorial Day
Press Release
By: Kate Brown
Date: May 27, 2016
Location: Salem, OR

Governor Kate Brown today ordered flags lowered to half-staff at all public institutions on Monday, May 30 until noon in honor of Memorial Day.
"I appreciate the service of Oregon's military veterans and the support from their families," Governor Brown said. "This Memorial Day, we honor the the brave men and women who made the ultimate sacrifice in service to our nation and remember their courage and dedication."
The full Presidential Memorial Day Proclamation is available on the White House website.
