Governor Brown Submits Appointments for Senate Confirmation
Press Release
By: Kate Brown
Date: May 3, 2016
Location: Salem, OR

Governor Kate Brown today submitted a list of state board and commission appointments to the Oregon Senate. The Senate will consider the nominations for approval during May Legislative Days.
"I appreciate the willingness of so many Oregonians to serve and provide valuable input on an array of issues for our state," said Governor Brown. "Their diverse perspectives are important to improving outcomes and expanding opportunities across Oregon."
