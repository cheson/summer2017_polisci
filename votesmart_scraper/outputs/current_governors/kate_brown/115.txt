Governor Brown Appoints Marijuana Policy Advisor
Press Release
By: Kate Brown
Date: May 31, 2016
Location: Salem, OR

Governor Kate Brown announced today she appointed Jeff Rhoades as Senior Policy Advisor for Marijuana Policy in the Governor's Office.
"Jeff will work on marijuana-related policy issues, helping create responsive regulations in what is still a new industry," Governor Brown said. "As we continue implementation of Measure 91, Jeff will be mindful of my focus on public safety, user awareness, and educating youth as this burgeoning business sector takes root."
Rhoades has served as Deputy District Attorney with the Multnomah County DA's Office since 2011, and worked as Counsel to the Oregon Legislature's House and Senate Judiciary Committees during the 2015 Session. He earned his bachelor's degree in economics at Trinity College and his law degree at Lewis and Clark's Northwestern School of Law.
