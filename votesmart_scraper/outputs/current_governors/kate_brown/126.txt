Governor Brown Orders Flags Lowered to Half-Staff for Peace Officers Memorial Day and Police Week 2016
By: Kate Brown
Date: May 14, 2016
Location: Salem, OR

Today Governor Brown ordered all flags at public institutions throughout Oregon be lowered to half-staff on Sunday, May 15, 2016 for the observance of Peace Officers Memorial Day. Flags should be lowered to half-staff for the entire day on Sunday.
"I extend my gratitude to all peace officers and law enforcement professionals from city, county, state, tribal, and federal agencies," said Governor Brown. "Every day, these dedicated public servants work closely with community partners to keep Oregon safe, and we recognize and honor their service and sacrifice."
