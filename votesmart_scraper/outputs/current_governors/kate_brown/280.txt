Governor Kate Brown Statement on Oregon Supreme Court Decision on PERS
Statement
By: Kate Brown
Date: April 30, 2015
Location: Salem, OR

Governor Kate Brown issued this statement in response to the Oregon Supreme Court ruling on PERS:
"I will be reviewing the ruling and assessing next steps, including the short and long term fiscal needs of PERS, and I will be working with the PERS Board to determine what next steps they will take."
