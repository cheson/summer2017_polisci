Eugene to Host 2021 World Outdoor Track & Field Championships
Press Release
By: Kate Brown
Date: April 16, 2015
Location: Salem, OR

In a special vote at its meetings in Beijing, the IAAF Council awarded the 2021 World Championships for track and field to the United States and the state of Oregon.
USA Track & Field, the national governing body for the sport in this country, the City of Eugene and local organizing committee TrackTown USA will host the meet at the University of Oregon's historic Hayward Field.
"This is spectacular news for Oregon, and a tribute to the University of Oregon's reputation for excellence in track and field," Governor Brown said. "It will be a pleasure and an honor to welcome visitors from around the globe to our state and to the beautiful city of Eugene."
