Public Statements
Issue Position: Marijuana Policy
Issue Position
By: Kate Brown
Date: Jan. 1, 2017
Location: Unknown

Governor Brown is committed to keeping marijuana out of the hands of children while educating adults about the products they use, protecting public safety, and creating sensible regulations for Oregon businesses. Governor Brown's team is committed to continued dialog with the newly developing marijuana business community to ensure that regulations work not just to keep consumers as safe as possible, but also to allow Oregon grown businesses to thrive.

Source: http://www.oregon.gov/gov/policy/Pages/marijuana_policy.aspx
