Governor Kate Brown Statement on December Revenue Forecast
Statement
By: Kate Brown
Date: Dec. 2, 2015
Location: Salem, OR

Governor Kate Brown issued the following statement in response to the December revenue forecast:
"The forecast is more than a number, it directly correlates to state government's ability to support the health of our schools, resiliency of our communities, and growth of our businesses large and small. While the forecast is stable through this biennium, I am watching carefully to make sure we are able to respond nimbly and appropriately should the landscape change."
