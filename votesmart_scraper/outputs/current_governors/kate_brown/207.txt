Governor Brown Praises Guardsman's Courage
Statement
By: Kate Brown
Date: Aug. 22, 2015
Location: Salem, OR

Governor Kate Brown issued the following statement today regarding the involvement of Oregon National Guard Specialist Alek Skarlatos in thwarting a gunman's actions on a train from Amsterdam to Paris on Friday, August 21:
"Specialist Alek Skarlatos is the pride of Oregon. His swift and courageous action against a heavily armed and dangerous gunman saved many lives and earned him the gratitude of the nation and the world. I am extremely proud of this brave Oregonian and serviceman."
