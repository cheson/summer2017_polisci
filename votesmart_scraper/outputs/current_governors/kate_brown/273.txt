Governor Brown Orders Flags Lowered to Half-Staff to Honor National Peace Officers Memorial Day and Police Week
Statement
By: Kate Brown
Date: May 13, 2015
Location: Salem, OR

Governor Kate Brown orders all flags at public institutions throughout Oregon be flown at half-staff from sunrise to sunset on Friday, May 15 in observance of National Peace Officers Memorial Day and Police Week 2015.
"We honor the brave men and women of Oregon who keep our communities safe and bring lawbreakers to justice," Governor Brown said. "Let us keep in our thoughts and prayers those peace officers who have lost their lives in the line of duty."
