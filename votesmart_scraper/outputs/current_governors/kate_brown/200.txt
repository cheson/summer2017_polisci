Governor Brown Orders Flags Lowered to Half-Staff in Remembrance of Sept. 11, 2001
Statement
By: Kate Brown
Date: Sept. 10, 2015
Location: Salem, OR

Governor Kate Brown ordered flags at all public institutions throughout Oregon to be flown at half-staff from sunrise to sunset on Friday, September 11 for Patriot Day and National Day of Service and Remembrance.
"I ask all Oregonians to join me to honor those who lost their lives in the attacks of September 11, 2001, and those who answered the call to service in the days and years following," said Governor Brown.
