Governor names George Naughton Acting Director of DAS
Press Release
By: Kate Brown
Date: March 5, 2015
Location: Salem, OR

Michael Jordan, Chief Operating Officer and Director of the Department of Administrative Services (DAS), has announced his resignation effective April 1, 2015. Governor Brown has named George Naughton, Chief Financial Officer at DAS, to serve as acting director effective immediately until a successor is named.
"We are committed to continuing with the Enterprise Leadership Team and related collaborative initiatives begun under Mr. Jordan's leadership," said Governor Kate Brown. "We are grateful for his years of service to the people of Oregon and the community of state government."
