Governor Brown's 2016 State of the State Address: 'We will protect what makes Oregon great'
Statement
By: Kate Brown
Date: April 8, 2016
Location: Portland, OR

Governor Kate Brown today delivered her 2016 State of the State address at the City Club of Portland's Friday Forum, reporting on her first year as Oregon's 38th Governor and sharing her vision for Oregon's future.
"In my 20-plus years in public office, in the Legislature, as Secretary of State, and now as your Governor, I have never been prouder of my state than I am today," Governor Brown said.
