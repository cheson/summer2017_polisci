Governor Brown Orders Flags at Half-Staff in Honor of Memorial Day
Statement
By: Kate Brown
Date: May 21, 2015
Location: Salem, OR

Governor Kate Brown today ordered flags lowered to half-staff at all public institutions from sunrise to noon on Monday, May 25 in honor of Memorial Day. Flags should be raised to full-staff from noon to sunset.
"On this Memorial Day, let us remember the brave Oregonians and Americans across the nation who have died while serving our country," Governor Brown said. "As we honor our fallen, may we remember their honor, their courage, and their dedication."
On Monday, Governor Brown will take part in a Memorial Day service at Willamette National Cemetery in Portland.
