Governor Kate Brown Orders Flags to Half-Staff Following Terrorist Attacks on Brussels
Statement
By: Kate Brown
Date: March 22, 2016
Location: Salem, OR

Governor Kate Brown today ordered all flags at Oregon public institutions to be flown at half-staff immediately until sunset on Saturday, March 26 as a mark of respect for the victims of the terrorist attacks in Brussels.
"On behalf of all Oregonians, I offer my sincere condolences to the people of Belgium and those who lost family and friends in today's brutal attacks," Governor Brown said.
