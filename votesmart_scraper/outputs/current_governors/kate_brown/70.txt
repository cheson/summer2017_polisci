Issue Position: Higher Education, K-12, and Early Education
Issue Position
By: Kate Brown
Date: Jan. 1, 2017
Location: Unknown

For our children to succeed and thrive, Oregon is building a seamless system of education from cradle to career to ensure every student is ready to learn, on-track and prepared for a future in which they can compete in the global workforce and be economically secure. The education policy advisor represents the Governor on education issues, guiding policy, and priority-setting from early learning through higher education.
