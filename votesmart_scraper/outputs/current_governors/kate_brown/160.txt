Governor Brown Orders Flags to Half-staff in Honor of Justice Antonin Scalia
Statement
By: Kate Brown
Date: Feb. 14, 2016
Location: Salem, OR

Governor Kate Brown today ordered all flags at Oregon public institutions to be flown at half-staff immediately until the day of interment in honor of U.S. Supreme Court Justice Antonin Scalia, who died on Feb. 13.
"Justice Scalia provided many years of dedicated service to one of the most consequential institutions of our nation," Governor Brown said. "On behalf of the State of Oregon, I offer my sincere condolences to the Scalia family."
