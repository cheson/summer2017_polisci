Governor Brown: 'I Commend President Obama's Plan to Curtail Greenhouse Gas Emissions'
Statement
By: Kate Brown
Date: Aug. 3, 2015
Location: Salem, OR

Governor Kate Brown released the following statement regarding President Obama's announcement, http://oregon.us2.list-manage.com/track/click?u=41... , on the EPA's new rule, http://oregon.us2.list-manage.com/track/click?u=41... , to limit emissions from existing energy facilities:
"I commend President Obama's plan to curtail greenhouse gas emissions. The reality of climate change calls on us to address these challenges in an efficient, cost effective, and responsible way.
"The Governor's Office will work with state agencies and stakeholders to learn more about the Clean Power Plan and chart a path forward that works best for Oregon.
"This is in the best interests of Oregon on many fronts. A healthy environment is essential to ensuring the health of Oregonians and protects our quality of life for many generations to come.
"Oregon is well positioned to comply with the Clean Power Plan. We have long been a leader in clean energy development to the benefit of citizens in our state. Making smart energy investment decisions now is critical to building a more resilient and sustainable economy for the future."
