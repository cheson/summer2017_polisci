Governor Brown: 'We Must Assure This Momentum Reaches Every Community'
Statement
By: Kate Brown
Date: April 14, 2015
Location: Salem, OR

Governor Kate Brown released the following statement on Oregon's unemployment rate, which is now below the national average at 5.4 percent.
"As Oregon's economy continues to improve, we must make sure there are opportunities for every Oregonian to get ahead, to make better lives for themselves and their families. As Oregon's economic picture brightens, we must assure this momentum reaches every community across the state."
More information on Oregon's unemployment rate is available from the Oregon Employment Department.
