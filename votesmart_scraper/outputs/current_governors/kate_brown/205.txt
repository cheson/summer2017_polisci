Governor Brown: Oregon's Economic Outlook Positive
Statement
By: Kate Brown
Date: Aug. 26, 2015
Location: Salem, OR

Governor Kate Brown issued the following statement regarding the September 2015 Economic and Revenue Forecast released today:
"I am encouraged that, overall, Oregon's economic outlook continues to be positive and will sustain funding commitments we've made to schools, public safety, and other important state services that support opportunities for working families. I appreciate the foresight and prudence on the part of my legislative colleagues in ensuring a healthy ending balance that can accommodate midstream revenue adjustments."
