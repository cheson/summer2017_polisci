Governor Brown's Statement in Response to Air Quality Concerns in SE Portland
Statement
By: Kate Brown
Date: Feb. 9, 2016
Location: Salem, OR

Governor Kate Brown released the following statement and letter in response to recent reports of high levels of cadmium and arsenic in some areas of SE Portland:
"I am very concerned about the safety of area residents. I have directed the directors of DEQ and OHA to provide me a detailed account of their agency's response and indicate specific next steps to restoring safe air quality."
