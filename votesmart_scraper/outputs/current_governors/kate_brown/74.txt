Governor Brown to Release Recommended Budget for 2017-2019
Statement
By: Kate Brown
Date: Nov. 30, 2016
Location: Salem, OR

Governor Kate Brown on Thursday will hold a press conference to release the Governor's Recommended Budget for 2017-2019. Following the press conference, members of the Governor's staff and State Budget Analysts will be available to reporters for an additional background briefing.
"I believe in a thriving Oregon; one that is resilient and sustains the well-being of current and future generations; where all Oregonians have opportunities to reach our full potential," said Governor Brown. "Achieving this vision requires strong public education, healthy families, careful stewardship of our natural resources, and a robust economy in every corner of the state. These priorities are foundational to Oregon's future."
