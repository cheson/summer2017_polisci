Flags to Fly at Half-Staff in Honor of Memorial Day 2015
Statement
By: David Ige
Date: May 22, 2015
Location: Honolulu, HI

By order of Governor David Ige, the United States flag and the Hawai'i state flag will be flown at half-staff from sunrise to NOON on Monday, May 25, 2015. This action is in honor of Memorial Day, in memory of the brave Americans who sacrificed their lives for the freedom and security of our Nation.
"We pause on this Memorial Day to remember and honor those who answered the call to serve our country and paid the ultimate price to preserve and protect our freedom. I encourage everyone in Hawai"i to take some time this holiday weekend to show your appreciation and gratitude to those who have fought and who continue to fight for our freedom," Governor Ige said.
