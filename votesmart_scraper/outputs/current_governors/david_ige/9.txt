Gov. Ige's Statement Regarding Today's Tentative Agreement on a Four-Year Contract with the Hawaii State Teachers Association
Statement
By: David Ige
Date: April 22, 2017
Location: Unknown

"This contract represents my commitment to excellence in our public schools and the practice of teaching. It says we trust and respect Hawaii's teachers. Together, we will make Hawaii a place our children can choose to call home."
-- Gov. David Y. Ige
