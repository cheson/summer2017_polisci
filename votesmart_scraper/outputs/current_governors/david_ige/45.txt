Flags to Fly at Half-staff in Honor of Officers, Victims of Police Ambush in Dallas
Press Release
By: David Ige
Date: July 8, 2016
Location: Honolulu, HI

At the direction of the President of the United States, the national and state flags will be flown at half-staff at the State Capitol and upon all state offices and agencies as well as the Hawai'i National Guard in the State of Hawai'i as a mark of respect for the victims of the attack in Dallas, Texas.
The flags will be flown at half-staff until sunset on Tuesday, July 12, 2016.
