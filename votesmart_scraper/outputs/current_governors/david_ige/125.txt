Statement from Governor David Ige on the Passing of Outstanding Educator Ron Bright
Statement
By: David Ige
Date: July 8, 2015
Location: Honolulu, HI

Governor David Ige released the following statement about the passing of outstanding educator Ron Bright:
"Ron Bright was one of those teachers who had a unique, gentle, energizing and contagious spirit that inspired his students to strive for excellence and to believe in themselves. He put his students first, and he gave them the confidence to achieve more than they thought they could. He was a role model for all public school teachers. I send my Aloha and condolences to his family, friends and students. He was a great man who we will truly miss."
