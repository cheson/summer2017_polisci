Governor's Office Flag Order: Flags to Fly at Half-Staff in Honor of Victims of Attack in Baton Rouge, Louisiana
Press Release
By: David Ige
Date: July 18, 2016
Location: Honolulu, HI

At the direction of the President of the United States, the national and state flags will be flown at half-staff at the State Capitol and upon all state offices and agencies as well as the Hawai'i National Guard in the State of Hawai'i as a mark of respect for the victims of the attack on police officers on Sunday, July 17, 2016, in Baton Rouge, Louisiana.
The flags will be flown at half-staff until sunset on Friday, July 22, 2016.
