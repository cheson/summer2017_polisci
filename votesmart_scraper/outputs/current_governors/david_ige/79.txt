Governor's Office News Release: Flags to Fly at Half-Staff in Honor of 12 U.S. Marines
Statement
By: David Ige
Date: Jan. 21, 2016
Location: Honolulu, HI

As a salute of honor and respect for the 12 U.S. Marines who went missing after their two CH-53E Super Stallion helicopters impacted the water off the coast of Oahu's Waimea Bay along the North Shore the evening of January 14, Gov. David Y. Ige has ordered the flags of the United States and State of Hawai"i be flown at half-staff. For five days, flags will be flown at half-staff at all state offices and agencies, as well as the Hawai"i National Guard, from sunrise on Friday, January 22, to sunset on Tuesday, January 26th, 2016.
