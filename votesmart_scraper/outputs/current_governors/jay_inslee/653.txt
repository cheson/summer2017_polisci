Public Statements
Senseless Shooting in Arizona
Statement
By: Jay Inslee
Date: Jan. 10, 2011
Location: Unknown

Jay issued the following statement regarding Saturday's tragic events in Arizona:
"My thoughts are with Gabrielle Giffords, her staff, the other victims and their families and I send my heartfelt condolences to those families who suffered an unbearable loss this morning. I grew to know Gabby through our work on clean energy issues from which I experienced first-hand her dedication and vision. I wish for her full and speedy recovery and in the meantime I ask all Washingtonians to join me in supporting the victims and their families."
Jay attended an event shortly after the shooting on Saturday, you can read the Seattle PI's coverage of his remarks here.
