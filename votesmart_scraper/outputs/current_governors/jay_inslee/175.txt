Inslee Appoints Rebecca Pennell to the Division Iii Court Of Appeals
Press Release
By: Jay Inslee
Date: Dec. 31, 2015
Location: Unknown

Gov. Jay Inslee announced the appointment of Rebecca Pennell to the Division III Court of Appeals, District 3. Pennell is a trial attorney with the Federal Defenders of Eastern Washington and Idaho, and has been with the organization since 2000. She replaces retiring Judge Stephen Brown, who served on the bench for nearly 20 years.
"Pennell displays a deep commitment to public service in her role as an attorney and as an active member of her community," said Gov. Inslee. "I am proud to appoint such a bright mind to this position, where I am sure she will excel."
