Public Statements
Letter to Ed Whitfield, Chairman of the Subcommittee on Energy - Additional Days of Hearings
Letter
By: Bobby Rush Jim Matheson Jay Inslee Gene Green Lois Capps Ed Markey Henry Waxman Mike Doyle John Dingell Eliot Engel
Date: April 13, 2011
Location: Unknown

Today Ranking Member Henry A. Waxman and Democratic Members of the Energy and Power Subcommittee sent a letter to Chairman Ed Whitfield requesting at least one additional day of hearings on legislation to amend section 328 of the Clean Air Act. The letter urges Republicans to schedule this hearing before the draft bill is marked up in order to gain insight from Environmental Protection Agency experts regarding the effects of this legislation.
The full text of the letter is below and can be downloaded here.
April 13, 2011
The Honorable Ed Whitfield
Chairman
Subcommittee on Energy and Power
House Committee on Energy and Commerce
2125 Rayburn House Office Building
Washington, DC 20515
Dear Chairman Whitfield:
Pursuant to clause 20)(1) of rule XI of the Rules of the House of Representatives, we are writing to request that at least one additional day of hearings be conducted on the subject of today's hearing on draft legislation to amend section 328 of the Clean Air Act. We further request that this hearing be scheduled before the Subcommittee marks up legislation on this subject.
