Public Statements
Letter to Ed Whitfield, Chairman Subcommittee on Energy and Power - Additional Day of Hearing
Letter
By: Ed Markey Mike Doyle Gene Green John Dingell Henry Waxman Lois Capps Eliot Engel Bobby Rush Jim Matheson Jay Inslee
Date: March 1, 2011
Location: Unknown

Today Ranking Member Henry A. Waxman and Democratic Members of the Energy and Power Subcommittee, sent the following letter to Chairman Ed Whitfield requesting at least one additional day of hearings on the subject of EPA's greenhouse gas regulations in order to hear from scientific experts.
March 1, 2011
The Honorable Ed Whitfield
Chairman
Subcommittee on Energy and Power
U.S. House of Representatives
2125 Rayburn House Office Building
Washington, D.C. 20515
Dear Chairman Upton and Chairman Whitfield:
Pursuant to clause 2(j)(1) of rule XI of the Rules of the House of Representatives, we are writing to request that at least one additional day of hearings be conducted on the subject of today's hearing on EPA's greenhouse gas regulations. We further request that this hearing be scheduled before the Subcommittee marks up legislation on this subject.
