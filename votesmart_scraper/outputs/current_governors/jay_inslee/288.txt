Gov. Inslee statement on UW President Michael Young's departure for Texas A&M
Statement
By: Jay Inslee
Date: Feb. 3, 2015
Location: Unknown

"On behalf of all Washingtonians, I want to thank Michael Young for all he has done for the students and faculty of the University of Washington. He'll be missed on campus and around the state and has left the UW poised for even more great things."
