Inslee Statement on Lamb Weston Announcement of 125 New Jobs in the Tri-Cities
Statement
By: Jay Inslee
Date: June 8, 2016
Location: Washington, DC

Inslee statement on Lamb Weston announcement of 125 new jobs in the Tri-Cities
"I am so pleased to have Lamb Weston reinvesting in Washington with the creation of 125 new jobs. This next generation of food processing in Richland is the perfect match of our agriculture and technology industries, which is why I worked intensively with the company to help them choose our terrific state."
