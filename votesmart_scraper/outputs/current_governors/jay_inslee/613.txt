Letter to Chairman Upton and Chairman Whitfield
Letter
By: Doris Matsui Frank Pallone, Jr. Anna Eshoo Jay Inslee John Dingell Gene Green Tammy Baldwin Bobby Rush Donna Christensen G.K. Butterfield, Jr. John Barrow Eliot Engel Henry Waxman Charlie Gonzalez Mike Doyle Ed Markey Diana DeGette Lois Capps Kathy Castor Jan Schakowsky Ed Towns
Date: July 8, 2011
Location: Unknown

Today, Democratic Members of the Energy and Commerce Committee sent a letter to Chairmen Upton and Whitfield objecting to the placement of H.R. 2417 on the suspension calendar next week. Members requested that the Committee proceed with regular order on the legislation that would repeal provisions in the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007 relating to light bulbs.
July 8, 2011
