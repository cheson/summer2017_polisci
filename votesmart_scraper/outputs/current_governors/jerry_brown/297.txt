Governor Brown Issues Statement on High-Speed Rail Agreement
Statement
By: Jerry Brown, Jr.
Date: April 18, 2013
Location: Sacramento, CA

Governor Edmund G. Brown Jr. issued the following statement in response to today's settlement agreement between the California High-Speed Rail Authority and farmers and agricultural leaders in the Central Valley:
"This is a very positive development and I commend the High-Speed Rail Authority on working closely with California farmers and arriving at a very solid settlement."
