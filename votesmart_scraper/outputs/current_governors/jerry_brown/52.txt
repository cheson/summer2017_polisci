Governor Brown Issues Statement on Oakland Warehouse Fire
Statement
By: Jerry Brown, Jr.
Date: Dec. 3, 2016
Location: Sacramento, CA

Governor Edmund G. Brown Jr. today issued the following statement regarding last night's Oakland warehouse fire that resulted in multiple fatalities and injuries:
"Anne and I were saddened to learn of last night's tragic fire in Oakland. Our thoughts are with the entire city in this difficult time and we extend our condolences to the family and friends of those lost."
