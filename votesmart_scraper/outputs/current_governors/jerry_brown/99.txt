Governor Brown Issues Statement on Death of Nancy Reagan
Press Release
By: Jerry Brown, Jr.
Date: March 6, 2016
Location: Sacramento, CA

Governor Edmund G. Brown Jr. today issued the following statement regarding the death of former first lady Nancy Reagan:
"Nancy Reagan lived a remarkable life and will be remembered for her strength and grace. On behalf of all Californians, Anne and I extend our deepest condolences to the Reagan family."
In honor of Nancy Reagan, Capitol flags will be flown at half-staff.
