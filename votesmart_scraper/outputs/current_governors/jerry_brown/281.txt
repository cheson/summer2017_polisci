Governor Brown Issues Statement on Inmate Reduction Order
Statement
By: Jerry Brown, Jr.
Date: June 20, 2013
Location: Sacramento, CA

Governor Edmund G. Brown Jr. issued the following statement today on the inmate reduction order by the Three-Judge Panel:
"The state will seek an immediate stay of this unprecedented order to release almost 10,000 inmates by the end of this year."
