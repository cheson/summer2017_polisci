Governor Brown Issues Statement on Homeowner Bill of Rights
Statement
By: Jerry Brown, Jr.
Date: July 2, 2012
Location: Sacramento, CA

Governor Edmund G. Brown Jr. today issued the following statement regarding the Legislature's passage of key provisions of the Homeowner Bill of Rights.
"The Homeowner Bill of Rights will prevent banks from throwing Californians out of their homes while they are trying, in good faith, to renegotiate their mortgages. This legislation establishes important consumer protections that are long overdue and I commend Attorney General Kamala Harris for her determined pursuit of these changes."
