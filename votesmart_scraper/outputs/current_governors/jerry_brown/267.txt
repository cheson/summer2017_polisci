Governor Brown Issues Statement Following Passage of AB 60
Statement
By: Jerry Brown, Jr.
Date: Sept. 12, 2013
Location: Sacramento, CA

Governor Edmund G. Brown Jr. issued the following statement tonight following passage of AB 60 by Assemblymember Luis Alejo (D-Salinas):
"This bill will enable millions of people to get to work safely and legally. Hopefully, it will send a message to Washington that immigration reform is long past due."
