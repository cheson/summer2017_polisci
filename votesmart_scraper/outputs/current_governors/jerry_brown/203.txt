Governor Brown Issues Statement on Passage of AB 1522
Press Release
By: Jerry Brown, Jr.
Date: Aug. 30, 2014
Location: Sacramento, CA

Governor Edmund G. Brown Jr. issued the following statement after passage of AB 1522, the Healthy Workplaces, Healthy Families Act of 2014 by Assemblymember Lorena Gonzalez (D-San Diego):
"Tonight, the Legislature took historic action to help hardworking Californians. This bill guarantees that millions of workers -- from Eureka to San Diego -- won't lose their jobs or pay just because they get sick."
