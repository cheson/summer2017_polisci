Governor Brown Issues Statement on Paris Attacks
Statement
By: Jerry Brown, Jr.
Date: Nov. 13, 2015
Location: Sacramento, CA

Governor Edmund G. Brown Jr. issued the following statement regarding today's terrorist attacks in Paris, France:
"All of California is saddened by today's wanton and brutal attack in Paris. Our prayers are with the victims' families and all the people of France."
