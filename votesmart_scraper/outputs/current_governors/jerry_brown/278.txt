Governor Brown Issues Statement on Assembly Passage of AB 93
Press Release
By: Jerry Brown, Jr.
Date: June 27, 2013
Location: Sacramento, CA

Governor Edmund G. Brown Jr. today issued the following statement after the California State Assembly passed AB 93, the Governor's economic development plan:
"This is a big, bipartisan win for California businesses and working people. AB 93 will help grow our economy and create good manufacturing jobs."
