Governor Brown Issues Statement on Public Records Act
Statement
By: Jerry Brown, Jr.
Date: June 19, 2013
Location: Sacramento, CA

Governor Edmund G. Brown Jr. issued the following statement today on California's Public Records Act:
"We all agree that Californians have a right to know and should continue to have prompt access to public records and I support enshrining these protections in California's constitution."
