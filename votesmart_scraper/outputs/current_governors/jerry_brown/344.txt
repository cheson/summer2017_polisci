Governor Brown Issues Statement on Wisconsin Shooting
Statement
By: Jerry Brown, Jr.
Date: Aug. 6, 2012
Location: Sacramento, CA

Governor Edmund G. Brown Jr. today issued the following statement following the tragic shooting at a Sikh temple in Wisconsin:
"On behalf of all Californians, I wish to express the deepest sympathy and admiration for the Sikh people as we mourn the lives lost to horrific violence in Wisconsin."
