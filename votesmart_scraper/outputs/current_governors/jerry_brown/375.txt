Governor Brown Celebrates Lunar New Year
Statement
By: Jerry Brown, Jr.
Date: Jan. 23, 2012
Location: Sacramento, CA

Governor Edmund G. Brown Jr. released the following statement today regarding the celebration of the Lunar New Year.
"Chinese and Asian Americans have played an important role in the history of California. As Governor, it is my honor to recognize their enormous contributions to our Golden State.
My wife, Anne, and I wish every Californian a very lucky and happy Lunar New Year. We look forward to a healthy and prosperous Year of the Dragon.
Gung Hay Fat Choy!"
