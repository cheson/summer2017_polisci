Governor Brown Issues Statement on Death of Nelson Mandela
Statement
By: Jerry Brown, Jr.
Date: Dec. 5, 2013
Location: Sacramento, CA

Governor Edmund G. Brown Jr. today issued the following statement regarding the death of Nelson Mandela:
"Nelson Mandela fought heroically for freedom and a truly democratic society. His courageous life shows what's possible when one acts on his convictions."
In honor of Nelson Mandela, Capitol flags will be flown at half-staff.
