Governor Brown Issues Statement Concerning Martin Luther King, Jr. Day
Statement
By: Jerry Brown, Jr.
Date: Jan. 17, 2011
Location: Unknown

Today the Governor issued the following statement honoring the memory of Dr. King:
"The Reverend Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. transformed America by challenging us to bridge our differences and strive for equality. Today is a celebration of Dr. King's life, but it is also a reminder that there is still much work to be done to realize his goals. I hope every Californian will reflect on his vision, and take action to make it a reality."
