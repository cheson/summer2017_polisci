Governor's Office Issues Statement on LAO Report
Statement
By: Jerry Brown, Jr.
Date: Nov. 16, 2011
Location: Sacramento, CA

Gil Duran, Press Secretary for Governor Edmund G. Brown Jr., issued the following statement in response to today's Legislative Analyst's Office report on the budget:
"California's budget gap is the result of a decade of poor fiscal choices and a global recession. This year, we cut the problem in half. Next year, we'll continue to make the tough choices necessary until the problem is solved."
