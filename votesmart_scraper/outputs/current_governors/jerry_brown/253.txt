Governer Brown Issues Statement on Day of Remembrance for President John F. Kennedy
Statement
By: Jerry Brown, Jr.
Date: Nov. 22, 2013
Location: Sacramento, CA

Governor Edmund G. Brown Jr. issued the following statement today regarding the Day of Remembrance for President John F. Kennedy:
"I had the opportunity to meet John Kennedy and I've never met a politician since then that had an aura and charisma, a presence, a confidence that unique. To snuff out such a young, vibrant, unique life like that, it left a mark."
In honor of the Day of Remembrance for President Kennedy, Governor Brown ordered that state flags be flown at half-staff today.
