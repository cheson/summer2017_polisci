Governor Brown Issues Statement Following Senate Action to Suspend Three Senators
Statement
By: Jerry Brown, Jr.
Date: March 28, 2014
Location: Sacramento, CA

Governor Edmund G. Brown Jr. today issued the following statement after the California State Senate's vote to suspend Senators Leland Yee, Ron Calderon and Roderick Wright:
"Given the extraordinary circumstances of these cases -- and today's unprecedented suspensions -- the best way to restore public confidence is for these Senators to resign."
