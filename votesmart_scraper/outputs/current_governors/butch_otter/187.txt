Issue Position: Making Government More Effective and Responsive
Issue Position
By: Butch Otter
Date: Feb. 27, 2014
Location: Unknown

Governor Otter believes government can be more effective and responsive with these policies he has implemented as Governor:
*Insisting on transparency and accountability in the prudent use of taxpayer dollars.
*Insisting on greater stability and predictability in regulatory and tax policies.
*Ensuring state agencies are working together -- and collaboratively with local governments -- in response to the needs of citizens and communities.
