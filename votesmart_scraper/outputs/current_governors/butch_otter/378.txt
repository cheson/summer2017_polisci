Issue Position: Enhance Economic Opportunity
Issue Position
By: Butch Otter
Date: March 25, 2010
Location: Unknown

Governor Otter has taken the following steps to enhance economic opportunity for each Idahoan:
* Launching and tirelessly promoting Project 60 -- his comprehensive business strategy for growing our economy, the IDAHO way!
* Engaging some of our best, most experienced and successful employers, entrepreneurs and innovators in helping to create career-path jobs and opportunities for Idahoans.
* Laying the groundwork for hundreds of high-paying careers by helping to convince AREVA to build a multibillion-dollar uranium enrichment plant near Idaho Falls.
* Developing a plan for moving ideas to economic opportunities by creating the Idaho Innovation Council.
* Maintaining a jobs-friendly tax climate for small companies expanding in Idaho.
* Advancing international trade opportunities for career-creating Idaho businesses.
* Applying federal stimulus dollars to career-creating roads, bridges, water and wastewater projects.
