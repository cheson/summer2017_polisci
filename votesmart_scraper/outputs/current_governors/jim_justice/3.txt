Governor Justice Issues Statement on I-79 Fatalities
Statement
By: Jim Justice, Jr.
Date: June 28, 2017
Location: Unknown

Today, Governor Justice issued the following statement about the fatalities on I-79:
"We tragically lost two West Virginians on I-79 yesterday, including a state Division of Highways employee. A sudden loss of life like this is truly heartbreaking. My thoughts and prayers are with the families and loved ones of those who passed, and those who are recovering from injuries."
