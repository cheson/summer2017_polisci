Governor Larry Hogan Rescinds State of Emergency for All Areas of Maryland
Statement
By: Larry Hogan, Jr.
Date: Oct. 4, 2015
Location: Annapolis, MD

Governor Larry Hogan today signed an Executive Order rescinding the state of emergency in the 15 counties and Baltimore City that were at risk for flooding due to recent heavy rain. There are currently no jurisdictions in Maryland under a state of emergency.
"I want to thank the state agencies and emergency personnel that responded to the threat of severe weather this weekend," Governor Hogan said. "I also want to thank Marylanders for their vigilance during these storms."
