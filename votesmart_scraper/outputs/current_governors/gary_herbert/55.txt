Gov. Herbert Statement on the Passing of Neil Ashdown
Statement
By: Gary Herbert
Date: May 23, 2016
Location: Salt Lake City, UT

Gov. Gary R. Herbert issued the following statement today regarding the death of former Utah Gov. Jon Huntsman's chief of staff Neil Ashdown:
"I am saddened to learn of the passing of Neil Ashdown. He was a dedicated public servant and a friend and mentor to many. His loss is felt deeply by the many Utahns who had the opportunity to interact with him over the years. Jeanette and I express our deepest condolences and love to Neil's family."
