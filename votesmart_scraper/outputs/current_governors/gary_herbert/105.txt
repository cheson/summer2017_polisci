Governor announces three millionth Utahn
Statement
By: Gary Herbert
Date: Oct. 26, 2015
Location: Salt Lake City, UT

Gov. Gary R. Herbert and First Lady Jeanette Herbert celebrated the milestone of Utah's three millionth resident today. The event was held at Utah's newest hospital, Mountain Point Medical Center in Lehi.
"Utah's growth is validation that we are doing things right," said Gov. Herbert. "Our quality of life and sound economy are attracting people from around the world who are seeking opportunity. Perhaps more importantly, we are creating opportunities for our children, so they can pursue their dreams here at home in Utah."
