Governor Herbert Offers Condolences and Prayers for Japan
Statement
By: Gary Herbert
Date: March 12, 2011
Location: Salt Lake City, UT

Utah Governor Gary Herbert issued the following statement regarding the recent natural calamities in Japan:

"On behalf of the people of Utah, our thoughts and prayers are with the people of Japan at this time of devastating tragedy in their beautiful island nation. Utah has significant ties to Japan, both economically and culturally. While our hearts ache for the loss of loved ones, Jeanette and I join the world in faith and hope for both survivors and rescuers."
