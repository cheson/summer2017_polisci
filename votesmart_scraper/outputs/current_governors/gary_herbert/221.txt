Gov. Herbert Thanks Veterans for Their Service
Statement
By: Gary Herbert
Date: Nov. 11, 2014
Location: Salt Lake City, UT

Gov. Gary R. Herbert issued the following statement in honor of our nation's veterans:
"Today, we pause to honor the men and women who have so valiantly protected our freedoms. The men and women of our Armed Forces dedicate themselves to serving our nation and to promoting liberty. No calling is more noble. Jeanette and I are grateful for their service and we express our thanks on behalf of all Utahns for their dedication to our great nation."
