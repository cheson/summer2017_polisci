Governor Gary Herbert on Legislative Leadership Elections
Statement
By: Gary Herbert
Date: Nov. 7, 2014
Location: Unknown

"I congratulate Speaker--elect Greg Hughes and the newly elected members of House leadership. I look forward to working with them and the continuing leadership team in the Senate, led by President Wayne Niederhauser, to accelerate our state's remarkable economic success and enhance Utah's unsurpassed quality of life."
