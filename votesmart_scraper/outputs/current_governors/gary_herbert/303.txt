Governor Responds to Ted Wilson Being Named as UCAIR Director
Statement
By: Gary Herbert
Date: Sept. 12, 2013
Location: Salt Lake City, UT

Utah Governor Gary R. Herbert issued the following statement regarding Ted Wilson being named as the new Executive Director of the Utah Clean Air Partnership (UCAIR), a non-profit organization:
"Ted is a man who has a remarkable ability of bringing people together from all sides and getting things done. He is intrepid and passionate ? two characteristics that will be invaluable when tackling this critical issue of air quality. With Ted at the helm, I'm confident UCAIR will move forward as a non-profit organization geared towards helping all of us make changes to improve Utah's air."
