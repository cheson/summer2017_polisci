Governor Susana Martinez Orders Flags Flown at Half-Staff in Honor and Mourning of U.S. Army National Guard Sergeant First Class Matthew McClintock
Statement
By: Susana Martinez
Date: Jan. 28, 2016
Location: Washington D.C.

Today, Governor Susana Martinez issued an executive order for flags to be flown at half-staff from sunrise to sunset on Friday, January 29, 2016, in honor and mourning of Army National Guard Sergeant First Class Matthew McClintock. There will be a memorial service on Sunday, January 31, in Albuquerque.
"Sergeant First Class McClintock's service and sacrifice exemplify the solemn legacy of New
Mexicans who answered the call to serve in our Armed Forces," said Governor Susana Martinez.
"Our hearts go out to his family, and I ask New Mexicans to join me in flying flags at half-staff
in his memory."
