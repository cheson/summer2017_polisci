Governor Susana Martinez, PNM Announce $122,000 Investment in Eight New Mexico MainStreet Communities
Press Release
By: Susana Martinez
Date: Dec. 18, 2013
Location: Albuquerque, NM

Governor Susana Martinez, New Mexico Economic Development Department (EDD) Cabinet Secretary Jon Barela, and PNM President and CEO Pat Vincent-Collawn announced today that PNM will provide $122,000 in grants to eight New Mexico MainStreet organizations. New Mexico MainStreet is an economic development program that works to help local communities revitalize their traditional city and town centers.
"New Mexico MainStreet's accomplishments are remarkable, and PNM's commitment to this
program is an outstanding example of how public-private partnerships can work to create the jobs and opportunities our families and communities need to succeed," said Governor Martinez.
