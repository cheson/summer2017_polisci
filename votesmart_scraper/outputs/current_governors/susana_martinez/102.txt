Governor Susana Martinez Announces State Employee Health Clinic, Saving Millions of Taxpayer Dollars
Statement
By: Susana Martinez
Date: Oct. 21, 2015
Location: Santa Fe, NM

Today, Governor Susana Martinez announced the opening of the state's firstever
state employee health clinic, which will not only provide quality care for thousands of
families, but also save taxpayers up to $3.5 million every year. New Mexico is one of only a
handful of states to offer this type of care for its employees and their families.
"We're staying true to our commitment to promote fiscal responsibility in all aspects of state
government, while at the same time building a stronger and healthier New Mexico," said
Governor Martinez. "By taking a proactive approach, we're reducing healthcare costs by giving
our employees increased access to high-quality preventative care. This is an important step in
improving health and, at the same time, saving taxpayers millions of dollars."
