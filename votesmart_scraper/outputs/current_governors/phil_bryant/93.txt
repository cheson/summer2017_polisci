Governor Phil Bryant Comments on the Passing of Judge Dan Fairly
Statement
By: Phil Bryant
Date: April 3, 2015
Location: Jackson, MS

Governor Phil Bryant issues the following statement on the passing of Judge Dan Fairly:
"Deborah and I are saddened to learn of the untimely passing of Judge Fairly. I have known him for many years during my time living in Rankin County. Deborah and I are praying for his family."
