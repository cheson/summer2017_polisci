Governor Bryant Thanks Senate for Passing Iran Divestment Act
Press Release
By: Phil Bryant
Date: March 4, 2015
Location: Jackson, MS

Gov. Phil Bryant thanks the Mississippi Senate for passing the Iran Divestment Act. The measure cleared the full Senate today.
"I thank the Mississippi Senate for voting today to oppose doing business with a nation whose government is a serious threat to the United States and to our closest ally, Israel. The Legislature may continue to debate this bill as the measure is finalized, but as for me, I stand with Israel."
