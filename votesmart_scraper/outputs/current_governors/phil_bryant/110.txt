Gov. Bryant Orders Flags Lowered in Honor of Speaker Tim Ford
Press Release
By: Phil Bryant
Date: March 2, 2015
Location: Jackson, MS

Gov. Phil Bryant has ordered all United States and Mississippi flags flown at half-staff on all buildings and grounds of the State of Mississippi and all areas under its jurisdiction beginning immediately on March 2, 2015, and ending at sunset on Tuesday, March 3, 2015, in remembrance of former Speaker of the Mississippi House of Representatives Tim Ford, who died on Feb. 27, 2015.
