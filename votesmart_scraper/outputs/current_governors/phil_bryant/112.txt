State Offices in Jackson Capitol Complex Scheduled to Reopen
Press Release
By: Phil Bryant
Date: Feb. 26, 2015
Location: Jackson, MS

State offices in the Jackson Capitol Complex are scheduled to reopen today.
Agency directors should use discretion in reopening offices in areas of the state where winter weather conditions persist. Agency directors who do close offices should also exercise discretion in having essential personnel report to work.
Motorists should continue to exercise caution on roadways, as residual patches of ice may be present.
Gov. Bryant and emergency officials will continue to monitor the weather and will issue further guidance and updates as necessary.
