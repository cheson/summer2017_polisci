Governor Bryant Issues Statement on Passage of Education Legislation, SB 2658
Statement
By: Phil Bryant
Date: Feb. 12, 2013
Location: Unknown

"I applaud Lt. Gov. Reeves, Senate Education Chairman Gray Tollison and the Senate for passing the important measures included in my Education Works Act of 2013. Mississippi's economic future depends on its educational future, and we can improve both by taking serious steps to ensure our schools are working for our children."
