Governor Phil Bryant Responds to President Obama's Change in Immigration Policy
Statement
By: Phil Bryant
Date: June 15, 2012
Location: Jackson, MS

Gov. Phil Bryant issues the following statement upon President Obama's shift in immigration policy:
"This is simply an election-year maneuver to allow 800,000 non-citizens the opportunity to affect the November election. The nation should be alarmed by these actions of a desperate politician. It is just one more example of this administration ignoring the rule of law for political gain."
