Governor Bryant Commends House for Passing Health Care Zone Legislation
Statement
By: Phil Bryant
Date: March 28, 2012
Location: Jackson, MS

Legislation aimed at increasing the number of health care jobs in Mississippi, House Bill 1537, passed the House 119-0 today.
"I appreciate the House's passing the Health Care Industry Zone Act to help expand the health care industry in Mississippi and create new jobs. The Act fosters a positive environment for medical development while making sure Mississippians have the proper access to cutting edge medical care."
