Sandoval Statement on North Las Vegas Union Contract Agreements
Statement
By: Brian Sandoval
Date: April 11, 2014
Location: Las Vegas, NV

Governor Brian Sandoval released the following statement today after learning all four contracts between the City of North Las Vegas and the employee unions have been agreed upon:
"I am pleased to learn that all four employee union contracts with the City of North Las Vegas have now been agreed upon. These agreements are the result of many hours of hard work and show the extraordinary commitment of the Mayor and the unions to their community and the State of Nevada. I would like to commend Speaker Marilyn Kirkpatrick, Mayor John Lee, and the employee unions for their dedication to resolving this difficult situation. It is my hope that this will be a positive step towards financial recovery for the City of North Las Vegas."
