Sandoval Statement on Supreme Court Decision on Commerce Tax Referendum
Statement
By: Brian Sandoval
Date: May 11, 2016
Location: Carson City, NV

"I appreciate the Nevada Supreme Court for moving quickly to resolve this important issue. The sponsors of this referendum owed it to the voters to explain the effect signing this petition would have on the state budget and most importantly, students and classrooms across Nevada. This decision is a victory in the fight for transparency and a citizen's right to accurate and truthful information." Governor Brian Sandoval
