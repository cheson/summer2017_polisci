Sandoval Statement on Passage of National Defense Authorization Act
Statement
By: Brian Sandoval
Date: Dec. 13, 2014
Location: Carson City, NV

Governor Brian Sandoval released the following statement today after final passage of the National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA) which included important legislation concerning public land use in Nevada.
"The passage of this important legislation will have a direct impact on all Nevadans. This bill will open the door to economic development, job creation, mining, ranching, and protect some of Nevada's treasured lands which will preserve our state for its next generation of citizens. I would like to thank the members of our Congressional delegation who supported the NDAA and hope for a swift signature from President Obama so all Nevadans can begin to enjoy the benefits of this significant bill."
