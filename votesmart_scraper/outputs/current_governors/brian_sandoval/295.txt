Sandoval Statement on June Unemployment Numbers
Statement
By: Brian Sandoval
Date: July 22, 2011
Location: Carson City, NV

Governor Brian Sandoval today made the following statement regarding the June unemployment numbers:
"While I am disappointed that our state's jobless rate has risen slightly in the month of June, I am confident in Nevada's resiliency. We are in a stronger position this month than we were at this time last year. Today's news is further indication that we must remain aggressive in supporting new and expanding businesses in our state to get Nevadans back to work."
