Sandoval Statement on Vivan Freeman
Statement
By: Brian Sandoval
Date: Dec. 6, 2013
Location: Carson City, NV

Governor Brian Sandoval this morning released the following statement after learning of the death of former Nevada assemblywoman Vivian Freeman:
"I served with Vivian in the Legislature and was saddened to hear of her passing. Vivian was a friend, a fierce advocate for her constituents and was always fighting to protect natural resources and the environment. I had great respect for her and she will be missed. Kathleen and I extend our deepest condolences to Vivian's family and friends."
