Sandoval Statement on Carpenter Fire
Statement
By: Brian Sandoval
Date: July 5, 2013
Location: Carson City, NV

Governor Brian Sandoval tonight made the following statement on the Carpenter Fire:
"Throughout the day, I have continued to monitor and have received regular updates from the Departments of Public Safety and Emergency Management regarding the Carpenter Fire. A Type I fire crew is en route and the state stands ready to continue to assist in any other way we can.
"Our thoughts and prayers are with those who have been displaced and with the firefighters who continue to fight on the front lines."
