Nevada's Jobless Rate Continues to Improve in June
Press Release
By: Brian Sandoval
Date: July 18, 2014
Location: Unknown

Gov. Brian Sandoval said in a statement that Friday's news doesn't take the pressure off of initiatives to diversify Nevada's economy.
"I am pleased with the steady improvements reflected in the most recent labor market report," Sandoval said. "Unemployment numbers are down and we continue to see job growth in key sectors of our economy. Although this positive news is welcomed, we must remain vigilant in our economic development efforts to continue moving Nevada forward."
