Sandoval Orders Flags to Half-staff Following Attacks in Brussels
Statement
By: Brian Sandoval
Date: March 22, 2016
Location: Carson City, NV

Governor Brian Sandoval today ordered flags to half-staff beginning immediately until sunset March 26, 2016 as a mark of respect for the victims of the attacks in Brussels, Belgium.
"The State of Nevada stands with the people of Brussels and the hundreds of victims of today's senseless and tragic attack. I have ordered the Department of Public Safety and the Division of Emergency Management to remain vigilant in the wake of these horrific attacks. Our state authorities will continue to actively monitor the situation and remain in constant communication with their local and federal partners," said Governor Brian Sandoval.
