Joint Statement from Sandoval and Moniz
Statement
By: Ernest Moniz Brian Sandoval
Date: Aug. 13, 2013
Location: Las Vegas, NV

Governor Brian Sandoval and Energy Secretary Ernest Moniz made the following statement after today's meeting:
"We enjoyed another productive conversation today about the importance of the Nevada Nuclear Security Site and its mission to both the Department and the State of Nevada. We discussed issues surrounding the U-233 shipments and the creation of a working group to strengthen communication on these issues. Both the State of Nevada and the Department of Energy look forward to continuing our dialogue as we continue working to resolve these issues."
