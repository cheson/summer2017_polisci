Sandoval Statement on Economic News Released This Morning
Press Release
By: Brian Sandoval
Date: April 18, 2011
Location: Carson City, NV

Governor Brian Sandoval made the following statement regarding the economic news released by the state this morning:
"This morning's economic news is a welcome indication that the recovery is taking hold in Nevada. I am pleased to see that we are once again experiencing real job growth, and I am more committed than ever to doing everything I can as Governor to help businesses continue this trend. We need a new economic development structure to recruit growing businesses to our state, and we need a tax and regulatory climate that spurs local businesses to continue hiring Nevadans. We have a long way to go but all Nevadans can feel optimistic this morning as we look forward to getting our state working again."
