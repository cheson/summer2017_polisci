Governor Sandoval's Statement Concerning Senator Debbie Smith
Statement
By: Brian Sandoval
Date: Feb. 3, 2015
Location: Carson City, NV

Governor Brian Sandoval released the following statement after Nevada State Senator Debbie Smith announced that she had been diagnosed with an operable brain tumor.
"Senator Smith is a devoted public servant and has earned the respect of her colleagues on both sides of the aisle. She is a courageous woman whose spirit and strength should inspire us all. I ask all Nevadans to join me in keeping Senator Smith in our thoughts and prayers as she makes strides toward a full recovery."
