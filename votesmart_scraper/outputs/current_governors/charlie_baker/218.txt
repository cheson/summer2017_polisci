Governor Baker Statement on MBTA Reform Plan
Statement
By: Charlie Baker
Date: May 20, 2015
Location: Boston, MA

Today Governor Charlie Baker issued the following statement regarding the administration's MBTA reform efforts:
"I deeply appreciate the leadership of House Speaker Robert DeLeo in standing with riders, taxpayers, businesses and workers in support of much needed MBTA reform. I join the speaker in recognizing the seriousness of the problems that plague the MBTA and the immediacy of the need for reform. I urge the Senate to support a bipartisan reform proposal and provide the Commonwealth with the world class transit system it deserves."
